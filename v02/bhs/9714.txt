{@pūrva-nimitta@}¦, <lex>nt.</lex> (= <lang>Pali</lang> pubba°), {%advance-sign%}, prognosticating something that is to happen in the future: °tāni <ls>LV</ls> 〔76.9〕 and 〔77.21〕, of the 32 signs which precede and forecast the birth of the Bodhisattva (so also in <lang>Pali</lang>); they are listed in detail between these two points in <ls>LV</ls>; in <ls>Gv</ls> 〔373.20 ff.〕 only ten pūrvanimittāni are listed as preceding the Bodhisattvaʼs birth; in <ls>Divy</ls> 〔193.20〕 of the five signs that a god is about to ‘fall’ (so also in <lang>Pali</lang>); used (as also in <lang>Pali</lang>) of dreams that foretell events, so <ls>LV</ls> 〔186.5〕 of Śuddhodanaʼs dream presaging the Bodhisattvaʼs retirement from the world; in <ls>Gv</ls> 〔375.2 ff.〕, ten pūrva-ni° occurred in the Lumbinī-grove when Māyā came out of Kapilavastu; in <ls>Gv</ls> 〔531.4〕, ten precede the revealing of the Bodhisattva Samantabhadra (these are all esoteric, dealing with occurrences in Buddhakṣetras).
