{@gati@}¦, <lex>f.</lex>, ({@1@}) (= <lang>Pali</lang> <ab>id.</ab>) {%state of existence%} into which rebirth is possible; {%destiny%}, (future) {%state%}. As in <lang>Pali</lang>, there are normally five: hell (naraka, niraya; nairayika), animals (tiryak, tiryagyoni, tiryaggata), ghosts (preta, yamaloka, °kika), gods, men; or six, with addition of asuras. The first three are evil, durgati (tisṛṇāṃ durgatīnāṃ <ls>SP</ls> 〔260.8〕, listed 9), or {@apāya@}, <ab>q.v.</ab> A brief summary of the 5 or 6 gati in <ls>LaVallée Poussin, AbhidhK.</ls> 〔iii.11〕. Lists of 5, [Page209-a] <ls>Samādh</ls> 〔19.17〕; <ls>Divy</ls> 〔300.10—11〕; 〔301.20〕; of 6, <ls>Dharmas</ls> 〔57〕; <ls>SP</ls> 〔244.12—14〕; without listing, aniṣṭa-gati-(= durgati)-traya-, and abhimata-gati-dvaya- (= gods and men), <ls>Av</ls> 〔i.244.14〕; pañca-gati- <ls>SP</ls> 〔131.16〕; <ls>LV</ls> 〔173.16〕; ṣaḍgati-, <ab>v.l.</ab> pañcagati-, <ls>SP</ls> 〔135.14〕, ṣaṭsu gatiṣu, or (<ab>vss</ab>) ṣaṭsū gatīṣū, gatīṣu ṣaṭsū, <ls>SP</ls> 〔6.9〕; 〔9.6〕; 〔48.3〕; 〔54.11〕; ṣaṭsu gatīhi (<lex>loc.</lex>!) <ls>Mv</ls> 〔i.42.17〕 (<ab>vs</ab>); ṣaṭsu gatiṣu 〔337.5〕 (prose); six also <ls>Mv</ls> 〔ii.368.12〕 (text uncertain); existence even in the relatively favorable states is still evil, <ab>cf.</ab> <ls>SP</ls> 〔48.3〕 ṣaṭsū gatīṣū parikhidyamānāḥ; in <ls>Śikṣ</ls> 〔147.14〕 a totally different list of four (evil) gati is given, <ab>viz.</ab> (1) akṣaṇa-gati (see <ab>s.v.</ab> {@akṣaṇa@}), (2) going to a Buddha-field which contains no Buddha, (3) birth in a heretical family, (4) sarvadurgatigati; ({@2@}) a high number: <ls>Mvy</ls> 〔7800〕; 〔7930〕 (cited from <ls>Gv</ls>); 〔8026〕; <ls>Gv</ls> 〔106.20〕; 〔134.5〕; ({@3@}) in {@gatiṃ-gata@}, <ab>q.v.</ab>, perhaps to be taken in the sense of {%understanding, comprehension, knowledge%}; <lang>Tib.</lang> in this <ab>cpd.</ab> renders <tib>rtogs pa</tib>, {%understanding%}, and uses the same translation when gati is associated with such words as smṛti, mati, as in <ls>LV</ls> 〔8.2〕 smṛti-mati-gati-dhṛty-uttapta-; see <ab>s.v.</ab> {@gatima(nt)@}.
