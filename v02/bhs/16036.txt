{@samādhi@}¦, {%concentration, trance%}, in <lang>Skt.</lang> and <lang>Pali</lang> recorded only as <lex>m.</lex>; <ab>acc.</ab> to <ls>Ratnach.</ls> only <lex>m.</lex> in <lang>AMg.</lang>, but also <lex>f.</lex> in <lang>Pkt.</lang> <ab>acc.</ab> to <ls>Sheth</ls>; here <lex>f.</lex> and <lex>nt.</lex> occasionally: <lex>f.</lex>, mahāvyūhāya (<lex>loc.</lex>) sthitaḥ samādhiye (<lex>loc.</lex>) <ls>LV</ls> 〔60.4〕 (<ab>vs</ab>; in <ab>prec.</ab> prose mahāvyūhasya samādher 〔59.20—21〕); etatpraveśo (<ab>v.l.</ab> °śā, so read?) yat samādhiḥ paramā jāyata iti <ls>Laṅk</ls> 〔21.4〕 (prose); -vipañcitāyāḥ (all <ab>mss.</ab>, <ls>Régamey</ls> <ab>em.</ab> °citāt, which indeed occurs 〔19.5, 14〕) samādher (<lex>gen.</lex>) <ls>Samādh</ls> 〔19.1〕 (prose); samādhiya (certainly <lex>gen.</lex>) lābhī <ls>Samādh</ls> 〔19.27〕 (<ab>vs</ab>); also in {@ceto-samādhi@}, <ab>q.v.</ab>, <ls>Mv</ls> 〔iii.409.12〕; <lex>nt.</lex>, tāni samādhīni <ls>Kv</ls> 〔51.7—8〕 (prose; line 2 above ete samādhayaḥ). As 8th step in the 8-fold [Page569-a] Path, samyak-sa°, see {@mārga@}. Sa° is 5-fold, ārya-pañcāṅgika-samādhi-saṃpannā(ḥ) <ls>Mv</ls> 〔ii.292.9〕, of bodhisattvas; <ab>acc.</ab> to a <lang>Pali</lang> list (<ls>Childers</ls> <ab>s.v.</ab>) the 5 aṅga are pīti-pharaṇatā, sukha-phar°, ceto-phar°, āloka-phar°, and paccavekkhanānimittaṃ; see also <ab>s.v.</ab> {@pañcāṅgika@} (2). Four kinds of sa° <ls>Mvy</ls> 〔967—970〕: chanda-, citta-, vīrya-, mīmāṃsā-sa° (= <lang>Pali</lang> cha°, ci°, viriya-, vīmaṃsā-); another list of four kinds <ls>Dharmas</ls> 〔101〕, somewhat corrupt, <ab>cf.</ab> the adhimukticaryābhūmi-nāmāni <ls>Mvy</ls> 〔896—901〕, and <ls>Sūtrāl.</ls> 〔xiv.24—26〕 with <ab>comm.</ab>; <ab>acc.</ab> to <ls>Dharmas</ls> āloka- (<ls>Mvy</ls> 〔898〕 °ka-labdha, read °lābha, <ls>Lévi on Sūtrāl.</ls>), vṛtāloka- (read vṛddhā°, <ab>cf.</ab> <ls>Mvy</ls> 〔899〕 āloka-vṛddhiḥ), ekādaśa-(read ekadeśa-)-pratiṣṭha- (<ab>cf.</ab> <ls>Mvy</ls> 〔900〕 tattvārthaikadeśānupraveśaḥ), and ānantarya-sa° (= <ls>Mvy</ls> 〔901〕, misprinted an°). There are a number of lists, some of them very long, of particular samādhis; over 100 in <ls>Mvy</ls> 〔505—623〕, from ‘Prajñāpāramitā’, <ab>e.g.</ab> <ls>ŚsP</ls> 〔1412.8 ff.〕 (these are all included in this <ab>Dict.</ab>); a very different list <ls>AsP</ls> 〔490.8—492.6〕; nearly 70 (practised by Avalokiteśvara) <ls>Kv</ls> 〔51.9 ff.〕; list of 17, <ls>Kv</ls> 〔77.8 ff.〕; of 37, <ls>Kv</ls> 〔92.17 ff.〕; only four, {@Śūraṃgama@} (which occurs frequently), {@Gagaṇagañja, Vimalaprabha@}, and {@Siṃhavikrīḍita@}, <ls>Dharmas</ls> 〔136〕; 〔100〕 Bodhisattva-sa° <ls>Gv</ls> 〔36.22 ff.〕 (this term occurs elsewhere in <ls>Gv</ls> and other works, <ab>e.g.</ab> <ls>Dbh</ls> 〔2.27〕, of named samādhis practised by Bodhisattvas). Few of the names in these and other lists recur, and most of them are not separately listed in this <ab>Dict.</ab>; they seem mostly to be ad hoc inventions of the respective authors. For samādhi in relation to {@samāpatti@} see the latter.
