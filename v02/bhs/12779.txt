{@rāśi@}¦, <lex>m.</lex>, {%group, collection%}; ({@1@}) (= <lang>Pali</lang> rāsi) applied to three {%groups%} or {%categories of%} (human) {%beings%}, called mithyātva-(or mithyatva-)-niyata, samyaktva-ni°, and aniyata, respectively (= <lang>Pali</lang> micchatta-niyata, sammatta-ni°, ani°); <ls>Childers</ls>, <ls>PTSD</ls>, and <ls>Senart</ls> <ls>Mv</ls> 〔i.517〕 (on 〔175.13—16〕) all wrongly apply the term to {%views%} or {%behavior%} instead of {%creatures%}.
