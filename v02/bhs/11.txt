{@akaniṣṭha@}¦ (= <lang>Pali</lang> akaniṭṭha; see also {@aghaniṣṭha@}), ({@1@}) <ab>n.</ab> of the fifth and highest class of the Śuddhāvāsakāyika gods (see {@deva@}), and ({@2@}) <ab>sg.</ab>, <ab>n.</ab> of the region where they live (<ls>Bbh</ls> 〔61.4〕 yāvad akaniṣṭhād; but more normally yavad akaniṣṭhabhavanam, <ls>Divy</ls> 〔162.16〕, or the like). They dwell brahmaloke <ls>SP</ls> 〔359.1〕. Often mentioned alone as the highest of the ‘form’ ({@rūpāvacara@}) gods, as also in the lists of classes of gods: <ls>Mvy</ls> 〔3106〕; <ls>Dharmas</ls> 〔128〕; <ls>LV</ls> 〔47.1〕; 〔150.11〕; 〔227.2〕; 〔266.8〕; 〔342.18〕; <ls>Mv</ls> 〔i.266.3, 7〕; 〔ii.314.3, 9〕; 〔319.7〕; 〔349.2〕; 〔iii.139.3〕; <ls>Divy</ls> 〔68.17〕; 〔367.14〕; <ls>Av.</ls> 〔i.5.4〕; 〔ii.105.11〕; <ls>RP</ls> 〔6.16〕; <ls>Mmk</ls> 〔19.10〕; 〔69.7〕; <ls>Bbh</ls> 〔69.19〕; 〔360.26〕; <ls>Sukh</ls> 〔64.11〕. Sometimes in <ab>sg.</ab> of a single member of the class, <ls>LV</ls> 〔44.13〕.
