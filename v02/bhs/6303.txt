{@cīraka@}¦, <lex>m.</lex> or <lex>nt.</lex> (in <ab>mg.</ab> 1 = <lang>Pali</lang> <ab>id.</ab>, <lang>Skt.</lang> cīra), ({@1@}) {%strip%}, primarily of cloth, bark, or the like: °ka-vadhrāṇi, here of strips of flesh, <ls>Mv</ls> 〔i.19.9〕 (prose); of either cloth or bark garments, 〔i.19.11〕 (prose), see {@vārṣikā@}; ({@2@}) in <ls>Mmk</ls>, {%lock%} or {%braid of hair%}, artificially arranged (compared to a {%strip%}) = <lang>Tib.</lang> (<tib>skra</tib>, {%hair%}) <tib>zur phud</tib> ({%hair-knot%}), <ls>Lalou, Iconographie</ls> 〔66 f.〕 (but I do not think, with <ls>Lalou</ls>, that this or any <ab>cpd.</ab> ever refers to a {%diadem%}); Mañjuśrīʼs head is adorned with five such, but some passages suggest that in this he was like an ordinary youth in festive garb, sarvabālālaṃkārabhūṣitaḥ (<ab>ed.</ab> °ta-)pañcacīrakopaśobhitaḥ <ls>Mmk</ls> 〔41.24〕; pañcacīrakopaśobbhita-(text °taṃ) [Page231-b] śiraṃ bāladārakālaṃkārālaṃkṛtaṃ 〔305.6〕; (kumārākāracihnitaḥ) pañcacīrakamūrdhāno 〔436.3〕; note especially pañcacīrakopaśobhitaṃ ekacīrakopaśobhitaṃ śikhopaśobhita-śiraskaṃ (so read for text °bhitaṃ aśiraskaṃ!) vā rājaputraṃ mūrdhābhiṣiktaṃ kṣatriyaputraṃ vā … 〔49.13〕 (the youth symbolizes Mañjuśrī, <ab>cf.</ab> 〔49.16 f.〕); Mañjuśrī is pañcacīrakopaśobhitaḥ 〔41.24〕; with more specific reference to his head, pañcacīrakaśiraskaḥ 〔62.8〕; similarly 〔68.16〕; 〔75.14〕; ({@3@}) also in <ls>Mmk</ls>, cīraka-mudrā is a position of the two hands, interlocked, in which one index finger is made to project (muktvā) between the two thumbs, so that it resembles a {%hair-braid%}: (anyonyasaktāṅgulimuṣṭayoḥ pradeśinīṃ muktvā aṅguṣṭhayugalaṃ [<ab>prob.</ab> read °la-]madhyataḥ, eṣā sā Mañjuśrīḥ) tvadīyā aparā cīraka-mudrā 〔382.(15—)17〕; <ab>prob.</ab> this is alluded to by ekacīrakam (<ab>sc.</ab> mudram; see <ab>s.v.</ab> {@mudra@}) 〔355.27〕, also sacred to Mañjuśrī; its description in 〔382.15 ff.〕 occurs in a list of mudrās the names of which refer (presumably because of their shape) to various parts of Mañjuśrīʼs body, <ab>e.g.</ab> tvadīyā vaktramudrā 〔382.11〕, <ab>etc.</ab> (note <ab>esp.</ab> 〔383.1 ff.〕 śravaṇo grīvā bhujau <ab>etc.</ab>, without specific descriptions).
