But parī- occurs for pari-. <ls>Dr. Paul Tedesco</ls> would derive by the <ger>Morengesetz</ger> from *pariddāmi for paridadāmi with loss of -a-; as a parallel he cites <lang>Skt.</lang> ujjhāmi <ab>etc.</ab>, which he also derives directly from uj-jahāmi with loss of -a-. That ujjh- is somehow derived from ud plus root hā, few will doubt; but I am not sure that this is the precise way (for a different one see <ls>Uhlenbeck</ls> <ab>s.v.</ab>); and I should like other examples of such loss of -a-.
