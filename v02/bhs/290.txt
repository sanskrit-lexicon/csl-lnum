{@atikrānta@}¦, <lex>adj.</lex> (<lex>ppp.</lex>), {%surpassing, wonderful%}, (specifically of appearance) {%beautiful%}: atikrāntā (so <ab>mss.</ab>, <ls>Senart</ls> abhi°) ca mānuṣāḥ <ls>Mv</ls> 〔iii.96.19〕; 〔100.20〕; °ta-varṇa, {%of beautiful appearance%}, <ls>LV</ls> 〔396.17〕; <ls>Mv</ls> 〔iii.316.13〕; 〔317.11〕; also <ls>Mv</ls> 〔ii.257.9〕; 〔258.7〕; 〔iii.197.15〕; 〔302.10〕 (in these four <ls>Senart</ls> <ab>em.</ab> abhikrānta°). On the other hand, atikrānta is erroneously read, <ab>e.g.</ab> in <ls>Divy</ls> 〔462.13〕, for {@abhikrānta@} (1), which is correctly read in the same passage in <ls>MSV</ls> 〔i.70.3〕, and elsewhere in that text.
