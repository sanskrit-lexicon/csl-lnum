{#SvapAka#}¦ m. ({#-kaH#}) A man of a degraded and outcaste tribe, the son of an
Ugra woman by a Kshettṛ male, and classed with the Chaṇḍāla,
with whom he is ordered to live out of the town, to feed from
broken vessels, and wear the clothes of the dead; to possess no
other property than asses and dogs, and to be excluded from
all intercourse with other tribes; he can only be employed
as public executioner, or to carry out the bodies of those who die
without kindred. E. {#Sva#} a dog, {#pac#} to cook, (to feed on or to cherish,)
aff. {#GaYH#} see {#Svapaca .#}
