{#raGuvaMSa#}¦ m. ({#-SaH#}) The race or family of RAGHU; hence also the name of
a poem by KĀLIDĀSA, which treats of the ancestors of RĀMA,
from DILĪPA to that prince, and his descendants to AGNIVARṆA;
nearly one half relates to the history of RAGHU and as much to
that of RĀMA, the remainder is occupied with the remaining
princes. E. {#raGu#} as above, and {#vaMSa#} race.
