{#svastivAcana#}¦ n. ({#-naM#}) 1. A religious rite, preparatory to a sacrifice or any
important observance, in which the Brāhmans strew boiled rice
on the ground, and invoke the blessings of the gods on the
ceremony about to commence. 2. An offering of flowers, sweet-
meat, &c., to any one intended to secure good wishes and blessings.
E. {#svasti#} inceptive and benedictory particle, {#vAcana#} uttering.
