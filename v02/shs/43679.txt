{#sAntapana#}¦ n. ({#-naM#}) 1. A sort of penance; the lighter kind is feeding on a
mixture of curds, &c. for one day and fasting the next; the
severer is feeding six successive days, severally, on the same
articles, or on cow's urine, cow's ordure, milk, curds, Ghee, and
water in which Kuśa grass has been steeped, and fasting on the
seventh. E. {#sam#} completely, {#tapana#} austerity, {#aR#} added.
