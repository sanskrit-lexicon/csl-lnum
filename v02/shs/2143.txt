{#apasada#}¦ m. ({#-daH#}) A low man, an outcaste; it especially applies to the
children of six degrading connexions, or of a Brāhman with the
women of the three lower classes; of a Kshetriya with
women of two, the Vaisya, and Sūdra; and of a Vaisya with one
of a lower class or a Sūdra. E. {#apa#} low, {#sada#} to abide, and {#ac#} aff.
