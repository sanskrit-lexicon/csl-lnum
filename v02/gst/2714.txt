{#aniyata#}¦ Tatpur. m. f. n. ({#-taH-tA-tam#}) <sup>1</sup> Not restrained, not
fastened. <sup>2</sup> Not necessarily connected. <sup>3</sup> Not inevitable,
not certain, not always occurring. <sup>4</sup> Not positive, not de-
finite, undefined, undetermined. (The {#aniyatA DarmAH#} or
‘undetermined rules’ of the Buddhists, are the topic of
one of the chapters of their Phātimokkha, and treat on
transgressions that involve exclusion, suspension and pe-
nance, but not permanent exclusion, of a Bauddha priest.)
E. {#a#} neg. and {#niyata#}.
