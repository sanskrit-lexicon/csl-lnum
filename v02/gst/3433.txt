{#anuzWAnaSarIra#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-ram#}) (In the Sāṅkhya philos.) A
body which is presumed to be intermediate between the subtle
body ({#liNgaSarIra#} or {#sUkzmaSarIra#}) and the grosser body ({#sTU-#}
{#laSarIra#}), composed of the five elements but tenuous or re-
fined and the vehicle of the subtle body. Comp. {#aDizWAna-#}
{#SarIra#}. E. {#anuzWAna#} and {#SarIra#}.
