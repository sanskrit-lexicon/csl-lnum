{#antaHpurasahAya#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-yaH#}) An assistant belonging to
the gynæceum or inner apartments (compare {#sTApatya#}); such
assistants are: ‘dwarfs, eunuchs, Kirātas or mountaineers
--retained as guards--Mlechchhas or barbarians, Abhī-
ras, the mock-brother-in-law of a king--i. e. the brother
of his concubine--humpbacks, mutes &c. E. {#antaHpura#} and
{#sahAya#}.
