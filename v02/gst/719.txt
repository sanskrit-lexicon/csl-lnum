{#aNgulitoraRa#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-Ram#}) Three semicircular lines drawn
across the forehead, with sandal or ashes of burnt cow-
dung &c., a sectarial mark, especially of the followers of
Śiva, more commonly termed Tripunḍra. E. {#aNguli#} and
{#toraRa#} the ornamented arch of a gateway, to which the mark
made by the fingers upon the forehead may be compared.
