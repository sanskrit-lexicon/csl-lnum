{#aprayukta#} is one of the seventeen, accord. to the {%Sāhityad.,%}
one of the thirteen defects of a word (see {#padadoza#}), which
must be avoided in composition; e. g. in the sentence:
{#yaTAyaM dAruRAcAraH sarvadEva viBAvyate . taTA manye dEvato'sya#}

the {%Kāvyapr.%} says, although the masc. of {#dEvata#} is gramma-
