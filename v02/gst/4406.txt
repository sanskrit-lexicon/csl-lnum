{#anvarTagrahaRa#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-Ram#}) Taking or understanding a
word in its literal or etymological (not in its conventional
or technical) sense; e. g. the word {#bahuvacane#} in Pāṇini VIII.
2. 81, where it is not be taken in the technical sense ‘plural’,
but in the sense {#bahUnAmarTAnAM vacane,#} as otherwise the form
{#amI#} would not result from the rule. E. {#anvarTa#} (Avyayībh.)
and {#grahaRa#}.
