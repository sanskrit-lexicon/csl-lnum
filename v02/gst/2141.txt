{#aDvarakalpA#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-lpA#}) One of the Kāmyeṣṭis (q. v.)
or voluntary iṣṭis with three yāgas (q. v.) in the morning,
in the middle of the day and in the evening, which may
be instituted by a man of either of the three first classes.
E. {#aDvara#} and {#kalpa#}.
