{#animittaliNganASa#}¦ Karmadh. m. ({#-SaH#}) (In medicine.) A dis-
ease of the pupil of the eye, when the whole crystalline
lens is affected; it is described as being produced ‘by the
sight of the divine Ṛṣis, the Gandharvas, the great Ser-
pents and luminous bodies’ and causing the entire loss of
vision. See also {#liNganASa#} and {#sanimittaliNganASa#}. E. {#ani-#}
{#mitta#} and {#liNganASa#}.
