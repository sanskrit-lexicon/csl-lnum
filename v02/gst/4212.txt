{#anyatas#}¦ ind. Used in the same manner as 1. the ablative and
2. the locative of {#anya,#} absolutely or with the ellipsis of a
word implying ‘place’, ‘manner’ &c., viz.
<div n="P">1. = the ablative of {#anya,#} ‘from another’; e. g. {#BAvitaM#}
{#pakzato'BIzwO tyaktvA varRO sarUpakO . anyato BAvitANkena#}
{#tataH pakzO viBajya ca#} &c., i. e. ‘removing the factum from one
side and the simple colours and absolute numbers from the
other, as optionally selected, and dividing both sides of the
equation by the coefficient of the factum &c.’; also with a
noun depending on it in the ablative, e. g. in the comm. of
the Kāsikā on Pāṇ. IV. 1. 40. ‘{#topaDAdanyataH prAtipadikA-#}
{#dvarRavAcino'nudAttAtstriyAM NIzpratyayo Bavati#}.
<div n="P">2. = the locative of {#anya#} and in the same sense as {#anyatra,#}
viz. <sup>1</sup> In another, on another; e. g. {#anyato vfttO#} (comm.

explanation of {#vijYAna, ‘mokze jYAnaM vijYAnamanyataH,#} i. e.
{#jYAna#} means (knowledge of what concerns) final emancipa-
tion, {#vijYAna#} other (knowledge), where {#anyataH#} represents
the locative {#anyasmin,#} the explaining words being put in
the locative, and does not mean ‘in another case, else-
where’. <sup>2</sup> (Scil. {#deSe#}) Elsewhere; e. g. {#pratipattiSUnyamutTA-#}
{#pyAnyataH pravfttaH;#} or {#yAnto'nyataH…AtmavargyAH;#} also re-
peated: ‘in one place--in another place’, e. g. {#aSfRvanna-#}
{#nyataH SabdaM prapalAyanta cAnyataH . Akrandamanyato'kurvestenAhanyanta#}
{#cAnyataH ..#}. Comp. {#anyatovAta#}. <sup>3</sup> (Scil. {#deSe#}) In one place,
in one direction (ved.); comp. {#anyatoGAtin, anyatomuKa,#}
{#anyato'raRya#}. <sup>4</sup> (Scil. {#deSe#}) On the other side, on the con-
trary. <sup>5</sup> (Scil. {#prakAre#}) In another way or manner, otherwise;
