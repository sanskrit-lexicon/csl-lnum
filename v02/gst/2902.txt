{#anugrahasarga#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-rgaH#}) (In the Sāṅkhya philosophy.) The
creation of mental conditions; in some of the Purāṇas it
is considered as an eighth creation, in others as a fifth, and
subdivided into {#viparyaya, aSakti, sidDi#} and {#tuzwi#} qq. vv.
See also {#pratyayasarga#}. E. {#anugraha#} (perhaps in the sense of
‘feeling’ in general) and {#sarga#}.
