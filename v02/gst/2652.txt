{#anAhatanAda#}¦ Karmadh. m. ({#-taH#}) A sound not produced in the
ordinary manner; the mysterious sound (om) which is heard

{#nAda#} (q. v.) or the real musical sound. E. {#anAhata#} and {#nAda#}.
