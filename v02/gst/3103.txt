{#anupAkftamAMsa#}¦ Karmadh. n. ({#-sam#}) Flesh of an animal which
has not been made fit for sacrificial purposes by the recital
of vaidik hymns, flesh of an animal which has not been
killed at a sacrifice. (A Brahmaṇa is forbidden to eat such
flesh.) E. {#anupAkfta#} and {#mAMsa#}.
