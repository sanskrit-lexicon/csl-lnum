{#anuBUti#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-tiH#}) (In the Vaiśeṣika phil.) Appre-
hension, one of the two essential properties of intellect
({#budDi#}); it is four-fold viz. knowledge arising from the senses
(see {#pratyakza#}), inference (see {#anumiti#}), comparison (see
{#upamiti#}) and verbal knowledge ({#Sabdaja#}). Comp. {#anuBava#}.
E. {#BU#} with {#anu,#} kṛt aff. {#ktin#}.
