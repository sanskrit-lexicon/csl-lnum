{#anilaparyAya#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-yaH#}) (In Medicine.) An inflammation
affecting the whole eye, described as produced from the
vitiated Rasa wind (see {#anila#} 1. f.) ‘which pervades the eye-
lids, eye and eyebrows and makes them diseased one after the
other’. The less correct reading of this word is {#anilapayaya#}.
See also {#mArutaparyAya#} and {#vAtaparyAya#}. E. {#anila#} and {#paryAya#}.
