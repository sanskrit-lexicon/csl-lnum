{#anilaparyAya#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-yaH#}) (In Medicine.) An inflammation
<div n="lb">affecting the whole eye, described as produced from the
<div n="lb">vitiated Rasa wind (see {#anila#} 1. f.) ‘which pervades the eye-
<div n="lb">lids, eye and eyebrows and makes them diseased one after the
<div n="lb">other’. The less correct reading of this word is {#anilapayaya#}.
