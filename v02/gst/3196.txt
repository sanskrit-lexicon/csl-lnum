{#anumaraRa#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-Ram#}) Dying with or following in death;
the term usually denotes the cremation of a Hindu widow,
whose husband's corpse is not on the spot, and with whose
shoes she, therefore, ascends the pile: this is prohibited to
Brāhmaṇa women; or the cremation of a widow with the
body of the husband (see {#sahamaraRa#}). E. {#anu#} and {#maraRa#}.
