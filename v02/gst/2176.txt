{#anakadunduBi#}¦ Bahuvr. m. ({#-BiH#}) A name of Vasudeva, the
father of Kṛṣṇa. More usually written {#AnakadunduBi,#}
but given also with the short initial by some commentators
of the Amarakosha. E. {#anaka#} (instead of {#Anaka#}) and {#dundu-#}
{#Bi#} ‘because the gods beat the Anaka-drums in his house
when Kṛṣṇa was born’.
