{#aniyukta#}¦ Tatpur. 1. m. f. n. ({#-ktaH-ktA-ktam#}) Not authorised,
not called, not appointed &c. See {#niyukta#}.
<div n="P">2. m. ({#-ktaH#}) (In Law.) A Brāhmaṇa who attends a court
without having been appointed a member of it, whose vote
is therefore not binding, and who is at liberty to attend
the court meetings and to debate in them, or not. (See,
on the contrary, {#saBAsad#}.)
