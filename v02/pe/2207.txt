DUṢPARĀJAYA¦ (DURJAYA). One of the hundred sons
of Dhṛtarāṣṭra. On the occasion of Duryodhana's jour-
ney to Dvaitavana Duṣparājaya was taken captive by
the Gandharvas. In the war with the Gandharvas he
clashed with Nīla. (Droṇa Parva, Chapter 25). He was
killed by Bhīma. (Śalya Parva, Chapter 26).
