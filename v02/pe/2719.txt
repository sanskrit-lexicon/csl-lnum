HUṆḌA¦(Ṃ). An urban region in ancient India. The
people of this area were known as Huṇḍas. Īn the great
war they fought on the side of the Pāṇḍavas. They took
their positions in the Krauñcāruṇa vyūha formed by
Nakula and Sahadeva. (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 50,
Verse 51).
