DARADA I¦. King of an ancient country known as Bāl-
hīka. It is stated in Mahābhārata Ādi Parva, Chapter 67,
Stanza 58 that this King was the incarnation of a portion
of the asura named Sūrya. At the time of his birth the
earth was cleaved because of his weight.
