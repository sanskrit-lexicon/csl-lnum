VASUMĀN¦ (VASUMANAS) III. A fire. It is stated
in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 221, Stanza 27,
that if a woman during her monthly course happens
to touch the holy fire, an aṣṭakapāla should be offered
to the fire Vasumān. This fire called Vasumān now
stays in the palace of Brahmā. (M.B. Sabhā Parva,
Chapter 11, stanza 30).
