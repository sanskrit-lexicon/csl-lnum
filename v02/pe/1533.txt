CIKṢURA¦. War minister of Mahiṣāsura. Tāmra was
his finance minister, Asiloma prime minister, Biḍāla
foreign minister, Ubharka Commander-in-chief, Śukra,
minister for education and Bāṣkala, Trinetra and Kāla-
bandhaka consulting ministers. (Devī Bhāgavata,
Pañcama Skandha).
