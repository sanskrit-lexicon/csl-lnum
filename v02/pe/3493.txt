KETUMĀN V¦. Antapāla (the guard of the boundary)
of the west. At the beginning of the creation of the
world Brahmā had appointed as guards of the bounda-
ries, Sudhanvan in the east, Śaṅkhapāda in the south,
Ketumān in the West and Hiraṇyaromaka in the North.
(Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 19).
