DUŚŚALĀ¦. The only daughter of Dhṛtarāṣṭra by Gān-
dhārī.
<div n="NI"/>(1) She was married to Jayadratha the Rājā of Sindhu.
<div n="NI"/>(2) {%Her birth.%} See under Kauravas.
<div n="NI"/>(3) Yudhiṣṭhira did not permit Jayadratha to be killed
when he abducted Pāñcālī as he was the husband of
Duśśalā. (Vana Parva, Chapter 271, Verse 43).
<div n="NI"/>(4) When Arjuna reached Vidarbha attending the
Aśvamedha yajña the archers obstructed his progress,
and he killed them all. Suratha, son of Duśśalā, was
also among those killed, and she came to the battlefield
with her infant child crying, whereupon Arjuna stopped
the fighting and in remorse crowned the son of Suratha
as the King of Sindhu. (Aśvamedha Parva, Chapters 78
and 89).
