VINATĀ¦. A wife of Kaśyapa. Kaśyapa took the daughters
of Dakṣa such as Vinatā, Kadrū and others as wives.
Two sons, Aruṇa and Garuḍa and a daughter, Sumati
were born to Vinatā. (Details relating to Sumati are
given in Chapter 19 of Brahmāṇḍa Purāṇa). The Nāgas
(serpents) were horn to Kadrū. For details see under
Garuḍa.
