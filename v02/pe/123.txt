AJAKA¦. An Asura.
<div n="NI"/>{%Birth.%} Kaśyapa was born the son of Marīci, son of
Brahmā. Kaśyapa married Danu, one of the daughters
of Dakṣa and had two sons by her. They were Ajaka
and Vṛṣaparvā. (M.B., Ādi Parva, Chapter 65, Verse
24; Chapter 67, Verse 16).
