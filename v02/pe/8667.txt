VYĀGHRADATTA I¦. A King who fought on the side
of the Pāṇḍavas. This King who was a great fighter on
the chariot, fought with the teacher Droṇa. It is men-
tioned in Mahābhārata, Droṇa Parva, Chapter 16,
Stanza 32, that he was killed by Droṇa.
