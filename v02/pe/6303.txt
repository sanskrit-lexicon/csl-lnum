ŚARAVINDU¦ (ŚAŚABINDU). A famous King born
in the dynasty of Bharata, son of Duṣyanta. His father's
name was Citraratha. He was a great ascetic and a
powerful emperor. (Bhāgavata, 9th Skandha). He had
[Page697-a+ 65]
no less than ten thousand wives and lakhs of children
by each wife.
