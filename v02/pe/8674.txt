VYĀSASTHALĪ¦. An ancient holy place on the border of
Kurukṣetra. It is said that because of his grief due to
the separation of his son, Vyāsa tried to commit suicide
at this place. He who visits this place would get the
[Page888-b+ 64]
fruits of giving thousand cows as alms. (M.B. Vana
Parva, Chapter 83, Stanza 93).
