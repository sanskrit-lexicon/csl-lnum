VIŚVĀCĪ¦. A celestial maid. She is one of the prominent
celestial maids such as Urvaśī and others. Once King
Yayāti played with Viśvācī. It is mentioned in Mahā-
bhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 122, Stanza 65, that
Viśvācī had attended the birth festival of Arjuna and
sang some songs. Her duty is to stay in the palace of
Kubera and serve him. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter
10, Stanza 11).
