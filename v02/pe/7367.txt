SUVRATA II¦. A King of the Aṅga royal dynasty. He
was the son of King Uśīnara who begot of his wife Nṛgā
the son called Nṛga, Nara by his wife, Narā; Kṛmi by
the wife Kṛmī; Daśa by the wife Suvratā and Śibi by his
wife Dṛṣadvatī. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 277).
