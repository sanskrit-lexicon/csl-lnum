NIYATI¦. A daughter called Lakṣmī and two sons called
Dhātā and Vidhātā were born to maharṣi Bhṛgu of his
wife Khyāti. Dhatā and Vidhātā married Āyati and
Niyati, daughters of Meru. A son named Prāṇa was
born to Vidhātā, and this Prāṇa became father of Mṛk-
aṇḍu and grandfather of Mārkaṇḍeya. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa,
Part 1, Chapter 10).
<div n="NI"/>Niyati after her death became a Devī, and she conti-
nues to worship Brahmā in his court. (Sabhā Parva,
Chapter 11, Verse 43).
