KHĀṆḌAVĀYANA¦. Paraśurāma gave all the coun-
tries conquered by him to Kaśyapa. Along with the lands
he gave a golden dais also. The group of hermits called
Khāṇḍavāyanas cut the dais into pieces and shared them
with the permission of Kaśyapa. (M.B. Vana Parva,
Chapter 117, Stanza 13).
