SVAYAMPRABHĀ¦. A daughter of Maya, an asura.
Two daughters named Svayamprabhā and Somapra-
bhā were born to Mayāsura. From birth Svayam-
prabhā became a celibate. Nalakūbara the son of
Vaiśravaṇa married Somaprabhā.
<div n="NI"/>Svayamprabhā, the celibate, became the maid of
Rambhā. (For the rest of the story, see under Rāma
Para 25). (Kathāsaritsāgara, Madanamañcukālambaka,
Taranga 3).
