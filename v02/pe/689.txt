ĀDITYAHṚDAYA¦. A mantra (incantation) of great
power to destroy all enemies. During the combat with
Rāvaṇa at one time Rāma fell exhausted in the battle-
field. Seeing this the sage Agastya rushed to him and
taught him this mantra. (Sarga 107, Yuddha Kāṇḍa,
Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa).
