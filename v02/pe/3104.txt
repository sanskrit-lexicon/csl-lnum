KĀLAKETU¦. A renowned Asura Emperor who was the
son of Danu, the wife of Kaśyapa. Once he abducted
Ekāvalī, the wife of Ekavīra and went to Pātāla (under-
world). Ekavīra fought a fierce battle with Kālaketu
and recovered Ekāvalī. (See under Ekavīra).
