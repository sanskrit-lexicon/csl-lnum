HAVIRDHĀNA¦. A grandson of emperor Pṛthu, who
had two sons called Antardhāna and Vādi and to
Antardhāna was born a son called Havirdhāna by
Śikhaṇḍinī, and they had six sons called Prācīnabarhis,
Śukra, Gaya, Kṛṣṇa, Vraja and Ajina. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa,
Part 1, Chapter 14).
