VĀMADEVA III¦. One of the seven sons born to Manu
by his wife Śatarūpā. It is stated in Matsya Purāṇa,
Chapter 4, that the Brahmin was born from the face,
Kṣatriya from the hand, Vaiśya from the calf of the leg
and Śūdra from the foot, of Vāmadeva, who was an
incarnation of Śiva. This Vāmadeva who had five
faces and a trident in his hand, fought with Candra,
when Tārā the wife of Bṛhaspati was carried away by
Candra. (Matsya Purāṇa, 4-13).
