SVIṢṬAKṚT II¦. The Agni (fire) called Visvapati,
who was the second son of Manu. Rohiṇī the second
[Page782-a+ 65]
daughter of Manu also is known by the name Sviṣṭakṛt.
It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter
221, that the offering in fire, of the fat portions of the
sacrificial animals, is carried out properly by the help
of this fire.
