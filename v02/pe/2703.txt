HLĀDINĪ¦. A tributary of the Gaṅgā. Gaṅgā brought
down by Bhagīratha to the earth first fell on Śiva's
[Page315-b+ 64]
head and thence on Bindusaras, and therefrom flowed
in seven tributaries. Three tributaries, Hlādinī,
Pāvanī and Nalinī flowed eastwards, Sucakṣus, Sītā
and Sindhu westwards, and the seventh tributary
followed Bhagīratha. The mortal remains of the sons
of Sagara were flooded with its waters, and their souls
attained heaven.
