BABHRU II¦. A Yādava of the Vṛṣṇi dynasty. He was
one of the ablest of Yādava warriors. (Chapter 14,
Dākṣiṇātya Pāṭham, M.B.). Even in his old age he
used to do penance. Śiśupāla carried away his wife when
once he was away in Dvārakā. He was a friend of Śrī
Kṛṣṇa. He died when hit by an arrow from Vyāsa.
(Chapter 4, Mausala Parva, M.B.).
