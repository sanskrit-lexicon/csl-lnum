PIŚĀCA III¦. An inhabitant of the country of Piśāca in
ancient Bhārata. These Piśācas fought against the
Kauravas on the side of the Pāṇḍavas during the great
battle. It was these piśācas that stood on the southern
side of the Krauñcavyūha of Yudhiṣṭhira in the great
battle. (Śloka 50, Chapter 50, Bhīṣma Parva). A few
of these Piśācas were with Bhagadatta in the army of
Duryodhana. (Chapter 87, Bhīṣma Parva). Śrī Kṛṣṇa
cursed the piśācas. Chapter 11, Droṇa Parva).
