ṚCEYU¦. A king of the Pūru Dynasty.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} From Viṣṇu were descended in the follow-
ing order:-Brahmā-Atri-Candra-Budha-Purūravas. Āyus-
Nahuṣa. Yayāti-Pūru-Janamejaya-Prācinvān-Pravīra-
Namasyu-Vītabhaya-Śuṇḍu-Bahuvidha-Saṃyāti-Raho-
vādī-Raudrāśva and Ṛceyu.
<div n="NI"/>(2 {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) His mother, Miśrakeśī was a goddess. (M.B. Ādi
Parva, Chapter 94, Verse 10).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He had two other names; Anvagbhānu and Anā-
vṛṣṭi. Matināra was the son of Ṛceyu. (M.B. Ādi Parva.
Chapter 94, Verses 11-13).
