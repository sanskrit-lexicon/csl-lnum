KAVYAVĀHA¦ (S). A group of Pitṛs. The Devī
Bhāgavata, Skandhas 11 and 15 state that this group of
Piṭrs were born from the drops of sweat which came
out of the bodies of Dakṣa and others when they were
fascinated by the charm of Sandhyā, the spiritual
daughter of Brahmā.
