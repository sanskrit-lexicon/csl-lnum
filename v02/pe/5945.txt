ŚAIŚIRĀYAṆA¦. A Mahaṛṣi. The famous Kālayavana
was his son, born to him by his wife Gopālī. Śaiśirāyaṇa
was the priest of King Trigarta. Once the king ordered
the sage to have sexual union with his wife Vṛkādevī
to test his virility. (Harivaṃśa, 1; 35; 12).
