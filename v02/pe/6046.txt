ŚAMBARA II¦. An asura.
<div n="NI"/>He was the son of Hiraṇyākṣa and brother of Śakuni,
Dvimūrdhā, Śaṅku and Ārya. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter
17). Śambara, who was a great adept in magic, was
killed in the company of his wife Māyāvatī, by
Pradyumna, the son of Śrī Kṛṣṇa. (See under
Pradyumna).
