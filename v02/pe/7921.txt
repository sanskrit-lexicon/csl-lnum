VĀJASANEYA¦. A religion or religious book (scripture).
At the end of Kaliyuga, people will become thieves and
lose all good qualities, and moreover fifteen branches
of the Veda Vājasaneya alone will be accepted as
Regulations of life. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 16).
