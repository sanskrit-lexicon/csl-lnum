BHAṬṬANĀRĀYAṆA¦. A sanskrit poet who flourished
near about A.D. 700. Veṇīsaṃhāra, a drama in six
acts is the only composition of his found out, yet. He
is also known by another name, Mṛgarājalakṣma.
Certain scholars opine that he might have lived before
Bāṇa and after Vāmana.
