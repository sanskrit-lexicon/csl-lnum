KAUSALYA¦. A teacher, who belonged to the line of
disciples of Vyāsa. Jaimini was the disciple of Vyāsa.
Sumantu was the son of Jaimini. A son named Sutvā,
was born to Sumantu. Sukarman was the son of Sutvā.
Sukarman had two disciples Hiraṇyanābha. and Pauṣ-
piñji. Kausalya was another name of Hiraṇyanābha.
(Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśā 3, Chapter 6).
