BALA I¦. (VALA). A demon born to Kaśyapa Prajāpati
of his wife Danāyu. This demon had three brothers:
Vikṣara, Vīra and Vṛtra. It was this Bala who later on
became the king of Pāṇḍyadeśa. (Śloka 42, Chapter
67, Śloka 33; Chapter 65, Ādi Parva, M.B.).
<div n="NI"/>Chapter 168 of Vana Parva states that this Bala was
killed by Indra. (For details see Vala).
