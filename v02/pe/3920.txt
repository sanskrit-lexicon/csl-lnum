KUŚĀVARTA I¦. Son of King Ṛṣabha, Kuśāvarta had
nineteen brothers, viz. Bharata, Ilāvarta, Brahmāvarta,
Āryāvarta, Malaya, Bhadraketu, Sena, Indraspṛk,
Vidarbha, Kīkaṭa, Kavi, Hari, Antarīkṣa, Prabuddha,
Pippalāyana, Āvirhotra, Dramiḍa, Camasa and
Karabhājana. (Bhāgavata, 5th Skandha).
