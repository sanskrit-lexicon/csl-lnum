DADHĪCA¦(M). A holy place in Kurukṣetra. Aṅgiras
the son of Sarasvatī was born in this place. From that
day onwards this place became a holy place. It is men-
tioned in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 83,
Stanza 186 that he who bathes in this holy place will
[Page192-a+ 66]
obtain the fruits of performing the sacrifice Aśvamedha,
and enter the world of Sarasvatī.
