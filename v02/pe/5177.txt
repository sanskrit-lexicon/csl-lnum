PIṆḌĀRAKA II¦. A sacred place situated near
Dvārakā in Saurāṣṭra. He who bathes in a holy river
there would get immense gold. The place is worshipped
by the sages. He who stays there for one day bathing
in that river would get the benefit of conducting an
Agniṣṭomayajña. (Chapter 82, Śloka 62, Vana Parva;
Śloka 21, Chapter 88, Vana Parva; Śloka 57, Chapter
25, Anuśāsana Parva).
