ṚṢYAMŪKA¦. A mountain. Mahābhārata, Vana Parva,
Chapter 25, Verse 9 states that Rāma and Lakṣmaṇa
visited the sage Mārkaṇḍeya on the top of this moun-
tain. It was here that Rāma and Lakṣmaṇa met
Sugrīva. Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 279,
Verse 44 states that the river Pampā runs round the
Ṛṣyamūka mountain.
