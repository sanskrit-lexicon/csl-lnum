PUṆYANIDHI¦. A king of the lunar race. The King
was a great devotee of Viṣṇu and he went to Rāmeśvara
to do worship of Viṣṇu there. Pleased with the pure
devotion of the king Mahāviṣṇu started living at Rāme-
śvara under the name of Setumādhava. (Chapter 3,
Skanda Purāṇa).
