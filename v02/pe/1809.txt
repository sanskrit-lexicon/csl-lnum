DEVADYUMNA¦. A king of the dynasty of Bharata.
From Bharata, son of Ṛṣabha, was born Sumati, from
Sumati Devajit and from him Devadyumna was born.
Devadyumna was the grand-father of King Pratīpa and
[Page209-b+ 65]
father of King Parameṣṭhi. (Bhāgavata Pañcama
Skandha).
