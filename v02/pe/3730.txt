KṢEMADHŪRTI¦. I. A Kṣatriya King of Ancient
India. It is stated in Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva,
Chapter 67, Stanza 64 that this King was born from a
portion of the Asura called Krodhavaśa. The King
ruled over the country called Kulūta. In the battle of
Bhārata he fought on the side of the Kauravas and
was killed by Bhīmasena. (M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter
12, Stanza 44).
