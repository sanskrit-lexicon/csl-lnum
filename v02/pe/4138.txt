MAHĀPADMA I¦. The first King of the Nanda dynasty.
According to Vāyu Purāṇa and Matsya Purāṇa,
Mahāpadma was the son of Mahānandī, the last King
of the Śiśunāga dynasty. He was the son of Mahānandī
by a Śūdra woman. He killed his father and founded
the Nanda dynasty.
