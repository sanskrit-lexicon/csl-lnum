MERUPRABHA¦. A forest. There were three beautiful
forests about the waist of the mountain Latāveṣṭa,
situated to the south of Dvārakāpurī. Meruprabha is
one of them. The other two are Tālavana and Puṣpaka-
vana. (Chapter 26, Sabhā Parva, Dākṣiṇātya Pāṭha).
