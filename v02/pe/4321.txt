MAṆIBHADRA III¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} A Yakṣa. He was a deity of
merchants and travellers. (Śloka 130, Chapter 64, Vana
Parva).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) He stays in the court of Kubera. (Śloka 15, Chapter
10, Sabhā Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Once this Yakṣa on the request of a cloud named
Kuṇḍadhāra gave a boon to a brahmin. (Śloka 21,
Chapter 171, Śanti Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) The sage Aṣṭāvakra once welcomed this Yakṣa.
(Śloka 33, Chapter 19, Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) When Arjuna went to Marutta to bring his wealth
Arjuna worshipped this Yakṣa and the Yakṣa blessed
him. (Śloka 7, Chapter 65, Aśvamedha Parva).
