ŚAṄKHA III¦. Son of the Virāṭa King.
<div n="NI"/>(i) He was present at the wedding of Draupadī in the
company of his brother Uttara and sister Uttarā. (Ādi
Parva, Chapter 31, Verse 16).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He also came out to fight Duryodhana and others
when they lifted the cows of the Virāṭa King. (Virāṭa
Parva, Chapter 31, Verse 16).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) On the first day of the great war he clashed with
Bhūriśravas. (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 49, Verse 26).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) He was killed in fighting with Droṇa at Kuru-
kṣetra. (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 5, Verse 17).
[Page688-a+ 65]
<div n="NI"/>(v) After death he got absorbed in the Viśvadevas.
(Svargārohaṇa Parva, Chapter 5, Verse 17).
