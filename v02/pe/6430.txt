SĀTVATA I¦. A King of the Yadu dynasty and son of
Devakṣatra, Sātvata had seven sons called Bhaja, Bhaji,
Divya, Vṛṣṇi, Devapṛṣṭha, Antaka and Mahābhoja.
Sātvata was one of the Sātvatas and the men born in
his dynasty are called Sātvatas. (Sabhā Parva, Chapter
2, Verse 30).
