PĀKA¦. A mighty asura. Once this asura gathered a big
army and went to fight against Indra. A grim battle
which lasted for several days took place in which the
asura army was destroyed and Pāka killed. Indra got
thenceforth the name Pākaśāsana. (Chapter 70, Vāyu
Purāṇa).
