PĀRADA¦. An ancient place of Bhārata. Those who
resided there were called Pāradas. The descendants of
these people live in north Baluchistan. Pāradas gave
Dharmaputra many valuable presents. (Chapter 51,
Sabhā Parva). They followed Bhīṣma. (Chapter 87,
Bhīṣma Parva).
