VĀRDHAKṢEMI¦. A mighty King of Vṛṣṇi dynasty.
The following details about him are taken from Mahā-
bhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(i) He was a great warrior who fought on the side of
the Pāṇḍavas against the Kauravas. (M.B. Udyoga
Parva, Chapter 171, Stanza 17).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He was present at the Svayaṃvara (marriage) of
Draupadī. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 185, Stanza 9).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) He confronted the Teacher Kṛpa in the
Bhārata battle and was killed by him. (M.B. Karṇa
Parva, Chapter 6, Stanza 21).
