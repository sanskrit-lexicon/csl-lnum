BṚHATKṢATRA II¦. A king of the country called
Kekaya. On the first day of the battle of Bhārata at
Kurukṣetra, this King fought with Kṛpa, the teacher.
Later he killed Kṣemadhūrti in the battle. In Mahā-
bhārata, Droṇa Parva, Chapter 125, mention is made
that he fought with Droṇa, the teacher and was killed.
