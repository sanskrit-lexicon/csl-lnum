KAPOTAROMĀ¦ (KAPOTALOMĀ). Son of the
emperor Śibi who was greatly devoted to his depend-
ants, (For genealogy see under Śibi). He had another
name Audbhida. (Chapter 197, Vana Parva). He was a
prominent member of the court of Varuṇa. (Śanti
Parva).
