AŚVAVĀN¦. The first child of King Kuru. (See under
genealogy of Kuru). His mother was Vāhinī. He is known
as Avikṣit also. He got eight sons: Parīkṣit, Śabalāśva,
Ādirāja, Virāja, Śālmali, Uccaiḥśravas, Bhayaṅkara
and Jitāri. (Ślokas 50 to 53, Chapter 94, Ādi Parva,
M.B.)
