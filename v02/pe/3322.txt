KARATOYĀ¦. A holy river. This river worships Varuṇa
sitting in his court. (Śloka 22, Chapter 9, Sabhā
Parva). If one stays on the shores of this river and
observes fasting for three days one would get the bene-
fit of performing an Aśvamedha yāga. (Chapter 85,
Vana Parva).
