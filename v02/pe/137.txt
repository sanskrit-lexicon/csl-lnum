<div n="NI"/>2) {%Birth.%} Pṛthu had two sons, Antardhāna and Vādi.

dhāna's wife. Six sons were born to them, Prācīna-
barhis, Śukra, Gaya, Kṛṣṇa, Vraja and Ajina. (Viṣṇu
Purāṇa, Part I, Chapter 14).
