SINDHUDVĪPA¦. A King of the Solar dynasty. (Mahā-
bhārata, Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 4, Verse 4). This
King was the son of Jahnu and the father of Balākāśva.
This King did penance in the holy place called Pṛthū-
dakatīrtha and attained Brahminhood. (M.B. Śalya
Parva, Chapter 39, Verse 37).
<div n="NI"/>It is mentioned in Bhāgavata, Skandha 9, that Sindhu-
dvīpa was the son of Śrutanābha who was the son of
Bhagīratha.
