SUDARŚANA I¦. A king of ancient India. He was a con-
temporary of Śrī Kṛṣṇa. This king who was esteemed
even by the gods, was once captured and made a
prisoner by another king named Nagnajit. Śrī Kṛṣṇa
defeated Nagnajit and all the other Kings and made
this King free. This story occurs in Mahābhārata, Udyoga
Parva, Chapter 48, Verse 75.
