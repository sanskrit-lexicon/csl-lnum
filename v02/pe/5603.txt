RAIVATAKA I¦. A mountain in Gujarat. It stands near
the present Junagaḍh. The present name of Raivataka
mountain is Girnar. In Mahābhārata it is spoken of as
Ujjayantagiri. It is stated in Mahābhārata that while
Śrī Kṛṣṇa and Arjuna were travelling through Prabhāsa-
kṣetra once, they got to the top of this mountain.
(Ādi Parva, Chapter 217, Verse 8).
[Page627-a+ 65]
<div n="NI"/>The Yādavas once celebrated a great festival on the
Raivataka mountain. It was during this festival that
Arjuna carried away Subhadrā, the sister of Śrī Kṛṣṇa.
(M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 219; Bhāgavata, Skandha
10).
