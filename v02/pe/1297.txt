BṚHADBALA III¦. A king of the country known as
Kosala. During his conquest of the East, Bhīmasena
defeated this King. This powerful King of Kosala had
given fourteen thousand horses for the Rājasūya (im-
perial inauguration) of Yudhiṣthira. He fought with
Abhimanyu and Ghaṭotkaca in the battle of Bhārata.
It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Droṇa Parva, Chap-
ter 40, Stanza 20, that Abhimanyu killed this Bṛhad-
bala in the battle.
