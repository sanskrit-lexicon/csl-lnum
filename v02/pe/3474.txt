KEŚINĪ I¦. A celestial woman. In Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva
Chapter 65 it is stated that the twelve celestial maids,
Alambuṣā, Miśrakeśī, Vidyutparṇā, Tilottamā, Aruṇā,
Rakṣitā, Rambhā, Manoramā, Keśinī, Suratā, Surajā
and Supriyā were born to Kaśyapa of his wife Pradhā.
