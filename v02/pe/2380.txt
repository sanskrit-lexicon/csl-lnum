GAUTAMA II¦. A brahmin who was a member of the
court of Yudhiṣṭhira. He was a member of Indrasabhā
also. This Gautama while he was living in Girivraja
had sexual relations with a Śūdra woman from the
country of Uśīnara and a son was born to them named
[Page286-b+ 65]
Kakṣivān. (Ślokas 17, 18 and 3 of Chapters 4, 7 and
21 respectively of Sabhā Parva, M.B.).
