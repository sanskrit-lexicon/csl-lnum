MEGHAHĀSA¦. A son of Rāhu. When he heard that
Mahāviṣṇu had cut off the head of his father he went
to the banks of the river Gautamī and practised severe
penance. As a result of that Rāhu, his father, got a
place among the other planets in the sky. (Chapter 142,
Brahmāṇḍa Purāṇa).
