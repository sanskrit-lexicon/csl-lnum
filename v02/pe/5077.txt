PARVAṆA¦ (S). A company of rākṣasas and devils. Par-
vaṇas, Patanas, Jambhas, Kharas, Krodhavaśas, Haris,
Prarujas, Arujas and Praghasas belonged to a group of
invisible demons and they fought against Śrī Rāma on
the side of Rāvaṇa. (Chapter 285, Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa).
