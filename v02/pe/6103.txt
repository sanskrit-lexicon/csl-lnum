SĀMUDRAKATĪRTHA¦. A holy place near Arundhatī-
vaṭa. He who bathes here and fasts for three days
will derive the same benefits as those of performing the
aśvamedha yajña and gifting away of thousand cows.
(Vana Parva, Chapter 84, Verse 81).
