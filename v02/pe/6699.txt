SOMATĪRTHA II¦. Another ancient holy bath situated
in Kurukṣetra. In Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter
83, Stanza 114, it is mentioned that by bathing in
this holy place, one could attain the world of Candra
(Moon). (See under Prabhāsa Tīrtha also).
