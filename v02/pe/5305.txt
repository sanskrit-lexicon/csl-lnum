PRAMATI I¦. (PRAMITI).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} Great Sage Bhṛgu, got of his wife
Pulomā Cyavana and Cyavana got of his wife Sukanyā,
Pramati. This sage Pramati married the beautiful
Pratāpī and their son was Ruru.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Pramiti visited Bhīṣma while he was lying on his
bed of arrows. (Śloka 5, Chapter 26, Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Pramiti is described as the son of Vāgindra born
in the family of Gṛtsamada son of Vītahavya. (Śloka
58, Chapter 30, Anuśāsana Parva). For details see
under Ruru).
