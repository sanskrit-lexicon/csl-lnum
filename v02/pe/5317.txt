PRĀṆA I¦. Grandson of sage Bhṛgu. Bhṛgu got of his wife
Khyāti a daughter named Lakṣmī and two sons named
Dhātā and Vidhātā. They married Āyati and Niyati
respectively and they got two sons named Prāṇa and
Mṛkaṇḍu. Mārkaṇḍeya was the son of Mṛkaṇḍu.
(Chapter 10, Aṃśa 1, Viṣṇu Purāṇa).
