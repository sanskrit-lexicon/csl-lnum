SĪTĀ II¦. A river. Mention is made in Mahābhārata,
Vana Parva, Chapter 188, Verse 102, that once the
hermit Mārkaṇḍeya saw this river also in the stomach
of child Mukunda (Śrī Kṛṣṇa). This is one of the seven
branches of the river Ganges. (See under Sindhu).
