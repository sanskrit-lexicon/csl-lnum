KETUVARMAN¦. A prince of the country of Trigarta.
He was the youngest brother of Sūryavarmā, the King
of Trigarta. When Arjuna led the sacrificial horse of the
horse-sacrifice of the Pāṇḍavas, Ketuvarman accompani-
ed Arjuna as a helper. (M.B. Aśvamedha Parva,
Chapter 74).
