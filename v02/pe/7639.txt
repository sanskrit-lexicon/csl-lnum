TURVĪTI II¦. A King. But since in some places he is
mentioned as a sage, it must be presumed that he must
have been a King who had become a sannyāsin during
the latter part of his life. Once this sage was drowned
and Indra came in time and rescued him. (Sūkta 61,
Maṇḍala 1, Ṛgveda).
