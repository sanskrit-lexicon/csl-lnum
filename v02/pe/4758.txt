NARĀNTAKA II¦. Son of Rudraketu, an asura. This
asura terrorized the three worlds by his wicked and
cruel deeds. When the wickedness and cruelty of this
asura became unbearable Gaṇapati incarnated in the
house of Kaśyapa to protect the three worlds. Knowing
this Narāntaka resorted to various means to kill
Gaṇapati. But his attempts were futile. Finally he was
killed by Gaṇapati. (Gaṇeśa Purāṇa).
