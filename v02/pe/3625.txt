KRAUÑCAVYŪHA¦. A strategic formation of the army
in the shape of Krauñca bird (snipe). In the battle
of Bhārata, Bhīṣma made the formation of Krauñca.
(M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 75). The Krauñca dis-
position has eight strategical positions, the face, eyes,
head, neck, stomach, left flank, right flank and thighs.
In the disposition formed by Bhīṣma, Droṇa stood at
the face, Aśvatthāmā and Kṛpa stood at the eyes,
Hārdikya at the head and Śūrasena at the neck. The
King of Prāgjyotiṣa stood at the stomach of the form-
ation. The Tuṣāras, Yavanas, Śakas and Cūpuvas
guarded the right flank. Śṛtāyus and Bhūriśravas kept
the thighs.
