CHĀYĀGRĀHI¦. Ī. A demoness who harassed Hanū-
mān on his flight to Laṅkā from the Bhārata shore.
She, by a trick of the shade obstructed his path and
Hanūmān realising the danger killed her by a blow
with his left foot and sprang forward. (Sundara Kāṇḍa,
Kampa Rāmāyaṇa).
