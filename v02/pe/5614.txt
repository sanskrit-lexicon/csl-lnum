RĀJAGṚHA¦ (GIRIVRAJA). An ancient city in India,
capital of Magadha. Dīrgha King of Girivraja was killed
by King Pāṇḍu. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 112, Verse 27)
Another King, Ambuvīca, had for a time ruled over the
city. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 203, Verse 17). Afterwards
Jarāsandha became its King. Girivraja is a holy place
as well. He who bathes here will become as pleasant-
looking as King Kakṣīvān. (Vana Parva, Chapter 84,
Verse 104).
