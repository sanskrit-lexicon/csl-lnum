BHAJAMĀNA¦. A Yādava King, whose father was Sat-
vata and mother Kausalyā, and they had, beside
Bhajamāna, another son called Satvata or Andhaka.
Bhajamāna married the two daughters of Sñjaya,
named Bāhyakā and Upabāhyakā by whom he got six
sons. (Bhāgavata, Navama Skandha).
