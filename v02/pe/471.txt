ARYAMĀ¦. One of the twelve Ādityas born to Kaśyapa
and Aditi. (Ādityas = sons of Aditi). The twelve
Ādityas are, Dhātā, Aryamā, Mitra, Śakra, Varuṇa,
Aṃśa, Bhaga Vivasvān, Pūṣā, Savitā, Tvaṣṭā and
Viṣṇu. (M.B., Ādi Parva, Chapter 65, Stanza 15).
Aṃśa is sometimes called AṂŚU.
