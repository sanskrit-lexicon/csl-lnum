ĀVARAṆA¦. A King of Viśvakarmā's dynasty.
<div n="NI"/>{%Genealogy.%} From Viṣṇu were born in the following
order: Brahmā-Dharma-Prabhāsa-Viśvakarmā-Priya-
vrata-Āgnīdhra-Nābhi-Ṛṣabha-Bharata-Āvaraṇa.
<div n="NI"/>Bharata married the world-beauty, Pañcajanī. Five
children, Sumati, Rāṣṭrabhṛt, Sudarśana, Āvaraṇa, and
Dhūmraketu were born to her. (Bhāgavata, Daśama
Skandha).
