VṚṢABHA I¦. Son of Subala, the King of Gāndhāra. He
was the brother of Śakuni. In the battle of Bhārata, this
Vṛṣabha, with his five brothers, attacked Irāvān, who
killed the five brothers. Vṛṣabha alone escaped death.
(M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 90, Stanza 33).
