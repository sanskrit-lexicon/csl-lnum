SUDHANVĀ VI¦. A King of Sāṅkāśya. This King
surrounded the city of Mithilā and sent word that
unless the bow of Śiva and the princess Sītā were given
to him he would kill Janaka, who subsequently fought
a battle with him. In this battle Sudhanvā was killed.
Sāṅkāśya, the kingdom of Sudhanvā was given to
Kuśadhvaja by his brother Janaka. (Vālmīki Rāmā-
yaṇa, Bāla Kāṇḍa, Sarga 71).
