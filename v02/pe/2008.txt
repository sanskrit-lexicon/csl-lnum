DHṚTI I¦. A daughter of Prajāpati Dakṣa. She was one
of the wives of Dharmadeva. Mādrī, the mother of
Nakula and Sahadeva, was the rebirth of Dhṛtī. (M.B.
Ādi Parva, Chapter 67). Dhṛti had given birth to Niya-
ma when she was the wife of Dharmadeva, who had
married Śraddhā, Lakṣmī, Dhṛti, Tuṣṭi, Medhā, Puṣṭi,
Kriyā, Buddhī, Lajjā, Vapus, Śānti, Siddhi and Kīrti,
thirteen of the daughters of Dakṣa. Viṣṇu Purāṇa,
Aṃśa I, Chapter 7).
