VṚDDHIKĀ¦. A kind of goblin. It is mentioned in Mahā-
Bhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 231, Stanza 16, that
once the semen of Śiva fell scattered over the trees and
that these goblins were born from that. Human flesh is
the food of these goblins. It is said those who want
children need only worship these Vṛddhikās.
