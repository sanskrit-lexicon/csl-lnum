BRĀHMA¦. A kind of marriage. The bride is adorn-
ed with all kinds of ornaments and is taken to bride-
groom; and given to him as wife. This is called Brāhma
marriage. Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 73,
Stanza 8 states that this mode of marriage was allowed
to all the four castes of ancient Bhārata.
