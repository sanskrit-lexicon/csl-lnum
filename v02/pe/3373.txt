KARŪṢA III¦. A Yakṣa. This Yakṣa accompanied by
his brothers performed penance on the shores of the
river Kālindī, to propitiate Devī, taking in only
air. Devī was pleased and appearing before him in
person said “You will become the lord of Manvan-
tara”. (Skandhas 10 and 13, Devī Bhāgavata).
