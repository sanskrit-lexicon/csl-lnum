VATSARA¦. The younger of the two sons of Dhruva.
Utkala, the elder was a man of abstinence. So the
younger brother Vatsara became the prince apparent.
<div n="NI"/>Vatsara married the princess named Svarvīthī. Six sons
named Puṣpārṇa, Tigmaketu, Īśa, Ūrja, Vasu and Jaya
were born to them. (Bhāgavata, Skandha 4).
