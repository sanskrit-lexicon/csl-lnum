VĀRĀHA II¦. A holy place in the middle of Kurukṣetra.
It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter
83, Stanza 88, that Mahāviṣṇu stayed in this place for
a while in the shape of Varāha (Boar) and that those
who bathe in this holy bath would obtain the fruits of
performing the sacrifice Agniṣṭoma.
