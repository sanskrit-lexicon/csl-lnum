PARIHYAṄGA¦. A maharṣi. One of the sons born to
Rṣi Marīci of his wife Ūrṇā. There were six sons and
the five others were: Smara, Udgītha, Kṣudrabhṛt,
[Page572-b+ 64]
Agniṣvātta and Ghṛṇī. In his next birth he was born
as one of the relatives of Śrī Kṛṣṇa and was killed by
Kaṃsa. (10th Skandha, Bhāgavata).
