PRAVĪRA II¦. A son of Pūru, a king of the Pūru dynasty
His mother was Pauṣṭī. He had two brothers named
Īśvara and Raudrāśva. Pravīra got of his wife Śūrasenī
a son named Manasyu. He was known by the name
Janamejaya also. After conducting three aśvamedhas,
Pravīra accepted Vānaprastha (third stage of life).
(Śloka 11, Chapter 95, Ādi Parva).
