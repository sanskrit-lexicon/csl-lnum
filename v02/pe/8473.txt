VIŚĀKHADATTA¦. A Sanskrit playwright. It is believed
that his period was between the centuries five and nine
A.D. His prominent play is “Mudrā Rākṣasa”. He is
known by the name Viśākhadeva also. He was the son
of King Bhāskaradatta and the grandson of Vaṭeśvara-
datta. Of his works, only Mudrārākṣasa has been found
till now.
