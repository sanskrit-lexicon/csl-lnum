MAHĪDĀSA¦. An ācārya (Preceptor). He is believed
to be the author of “Aitareya Brāhmaṇa” and
“Aitareya Āraṇyaka”.
<div n="NI"/>According to Chāndogyopaniṣad and Jaiminīya
Upaniṣad Brāhmaṇa, Mahīdāsa lived for 117 years.
Although this ācārya was a victim of diseases he was
quite indifferent to the painful sufferings caused by
them. (Chāndogyopaniṣad 3; 16; 7—Jaiminīya
Upaniṣad Brāhmaṇa, 4; 2; 11).
