DEVĪTĪRTHA¦(M). The common name of three holy
centres (Śaṅkhinī, Madhuvaṭī, Mṛgadhūmā) in Kuruk-
ṣetra. Those who bathe in Śankhinī will become beauti-
ful in appearance, while those who bathe in Madhu-
vaṭī and Mṛgadhūmā will get the benefits of perform-
ing a thousand godānas (gifts of cows).
