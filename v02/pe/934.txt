BALVALA¦ (VALKALA). An asura. This asura had
been tormenting the hermits of the forest of Naimiśa.
[Page107-a+ 64]
This asura was killed by Balabhadrarāma.
<F>*) In the original of Bhāgavata this name is given as Balvala. But in the Malayalam translation the name used is “Valkala”.</F> For
detailed story see para 6 of the word Balabhadra-
rāma.
