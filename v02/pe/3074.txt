KAKṢEYU¦ (KṚṢEYU). Son of Raudrāśva born of a
nymph called Miśrakeśī. He had nine brothers, named
Ṛkṣeyu, Sannateyu, Ghṛteyu (Kṛteyu), Citeyu. Sthaṇḍi-
leyu, Dharmeyu, Sammiteyu, Ṛteyu and Matināra. For
genealogy see under ‘Pūruvaṃśa’. (Śloka 10, Chapter
94, Ādi Parva).
