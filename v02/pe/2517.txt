GṚTSAMADA I¦. A celebrated sage. He was the son of
a sage called Vītahavya. Gṛtsamada was equal to Bṛhas-
pati and a great friend of Indra. Once this sage lec-
tured to Yudhiṣṭhira on the glory of Śiva. Many have
mistaken Grtsamada for Indra. Once the asuras bound
him by ropes mistaking him for Indra. He had a son
named Kucetā. (For more details see under Variṣṭhā)
(Chapters 18 and 30, Anuśāsana Parva).
