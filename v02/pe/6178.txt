ŚAṄKHA I¦. Considered to be a great, sublime treasure,
found in Kubera's court. Brahmadatta, King of
Pāñcāla attained heaven as he used to give Śaṅkha to
good brahmins. (Śānti Parva, Chapter 234 and
Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 137). Śaṅkha appeared on
earth, according to one belief, from the bones of
Śaṅkhacūḍa. (For details see under Tulasī, Para 2).
