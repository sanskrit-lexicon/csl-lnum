ŚUCI I¦. A deva (god) who was born in Agnivaṃśa.
This Śuci was the son of Agnideva who was the eldest
son of Brahmā, and his wife Svāhā. Śuci had two
brothers by name Pāvaka and Pavamāna. These
brothers had fortyfive sons. They are also known as
“Agnis”. Thus there are on the whole fortynine Agnis,
including the father, three sons and their fortyfive child-
ren. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Part 1, Chapter 10).
