VIBHRĀJARĀJA¦. Father-in-law of the daughter of the
hermit Śuka. Śuka, the son of Vyāsa married Pīvarī,
who gave birth to four sons named Kṛṣṇa, Gaura-
prabha, Bhūri and Devaśruta and a daughter named
Kīrti. This daughter Kīrti was married by Aṇuha the
son of King Vibhrāja. Brahmadatta was the son born
to Aṇuha by Kīrti. (Devī Bhāgavata, Skandha 1).
