KṚTĀŚVA¦. A great sage of ancient days. He was the
son-in-law of Dakṣaprajāpati. Dakṣa had sixty daugh-
ters by his wife Vīraṇī, thirteen of whom were wedded
to Kaśyapa, ten to Dharma, twenty-seven to Candra,
two to Bhṛgu, four to Ariṣṭanemi, two to Kṛtāśva and
two to Aṅgiras. (Devī Bhāgavata, 7th Skandha).
