DEVARĀTA II¦. (Śunaśśepha).
<div n="NI"/>{%General.%} A king of Mithilā. The kings of Mithilā were
commonly called Janaka. Devarāta was called Devarāta
Janaka. (See Janaka).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Genealogy.%} From Viṣṇu descended thus:—Brahmā,—
Bhṛgu—Cyavana—Ūrva—Ṛcīka—Devarāta (Śunaśśe-
pha). (For details see Śunaśśepha).
