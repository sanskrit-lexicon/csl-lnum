SUDHANVĀ I¦. A guard of the ends of the quarters.
In the beginning of creation Brahmā made four guards
of the four ends. They were Sudhanvā in the East,
Śaṅkhapāda in the South, Ketumān in the West and
Hiraṇyaromā in the North. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 19).
