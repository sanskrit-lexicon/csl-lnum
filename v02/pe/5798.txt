ṚTAMBHARA¦. An ancient King. He worshipped
Kāmadhenu and obtained a son who was a devotee of
Viṣṇu. The son's name was Satyavān. In connection
with Śrī Rāma's Aśvamedhayāga, Śatrughna who was
leading the horse, arrived in Satyavān's city during
his tour of the eastern lands. (Padma Purāṇa, Pātāla
Khaṇḍa, Chapter 30).
