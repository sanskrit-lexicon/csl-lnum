MAHIṢAKA¦. (MĀHIṢAKA): A special tribe. They
were once Kṣatriyas but they became Śūdras by their
evil mode of living. (Ślokas 22, 23, Chapter 33, Anuśā-
sana Parva). Arjuna during his victory march con-
quered this tribe in the south. (Chapter 83, Aśvamedha
Parva).
