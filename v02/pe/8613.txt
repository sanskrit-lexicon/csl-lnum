VṚKALA¦. A grandson of Dhruva who was the son of
Uttānapāda. Two sons named Śiṣṭi and Bhavya were
born to Dhruva by his wife Śambhū. Succhāyā, the wife
[Page881-a+ 64]
of Śiṣṭi gave birth to five sinless sons named Ripu,
Ripuñjaya, Vipra, Vṛkala and Vṛkatejas. (Viṣṇu
Purāṇa, Aṃśa 1, Chapter 13).
