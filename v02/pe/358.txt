ANUDRUHYU¦. Son of Yayāti.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Descended from Viṣṇu thus: Brahmā,
Atri, Candra, Budha, Purūravas, Āyus, Nahuṣa,
Yayāti, Anudruhyu.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Birth.%} Yayāti had two sons, Yadu and Turvasu, by
Devayānī, and three sons, Druhyu, Anudruhyu and
Pūru by Śarmiṣṭhā. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 12).
