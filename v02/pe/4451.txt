MATINĀRA¦. A King of Puruvaṃśa. The line from Pūru
is as follows: Pūru-Janamejaya-Prācinvān-Manasyu-
Vītabhaya-Śuṇḍu-Bahuvidha-Saṃyāti-Rahovādī-
Bhadrāśva-Ṛkṣeyu-Kṛṣeyu-Sannateyu-Ghṛteyu-Sthaṇ-
ḍileyu-Dharmeyu-Saṃvidheyu-Kṛteyu-Matināra. Mati-
nāra got two sons, Santurodha and Pratiratha. Duṣ-
yanta, husband of Śakuntalā, was the son of Śantu-
rodha. (Chapter 278, Ādi Parva).
