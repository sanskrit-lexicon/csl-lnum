SUDAKṢIṆA¦. II. A king of Kāmboja (Kabul). The
following information is taken from Mahābhārata
about this king.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Sudakṣiṇa had been present at the Svayaṃvara
marriage of Draupadī. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 185,
Verse 15).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He came with an akṣauhiṇī of army to help
Duryodhana against the Pāṇḍavas in the battle of
Bhārata. (M.B. Udyoga Parva, Chapter 19, Verse
21).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Sudakṣiṇa was a valiant fighter in the army of
[Page750-b+ 63]
the Kauravas. (M.B. Udyoga Parva, Chapter 160,
Verse 1).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) On the first day of the battle of Bhārata. there
was a combat between Śrutakarmā and Sudakṣiṇa.
(M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 45, Verse 66).
<div n="NI"/>(v) There was another combat between Abhimanyu
and Sudakṣiṇa. (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 45,
Verse 66).
<div n="NI"/>(vi) Arjuna killed Sudakṣiṇa in the battle of Bhārata.
(M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter 92, Verse 61).
<div n="NI"/>(vii) The younger brother of Sudakṣiṇa confronted
Arjuna to avenge the death of his brother and was
killed by Arjuna. (M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter 56,
Verse 110).
