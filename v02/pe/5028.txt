PĀRĀŚARYA II¦. This sage is different from Vyāsa. This
Pārāśarya was a member of the court of Yudhiṣṭhira.
(Śloka 13, Chapter 7, Sabhā Parva). He was a member
of the court of Indra also. Śrī Kṛṣṇa once met him while
he was going to Hastināpura from Dvārakā. (Śloka 64,
Chapter 83, Udyoga Parva).
