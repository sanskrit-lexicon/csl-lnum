SŪRYATĪRTHA¦. An ancient holy place situated in
Kurukṣetra. It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Vana
Parva, Chapter 88, Stanza 48, that by bathing here and
worshipping the manes one may attain the fruits of
performing the sacrifice of Agniṣṭoma and go to the
region of the Sun.
