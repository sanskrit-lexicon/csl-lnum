BHAYAṄKARA I¦. A prince of the country of Sauvīra.
He was a dependant of Jayadratha. It was this Bhayaṅ-
kara who followed Jayadratha with his flag when he
was trying to kidnap Pāñcālī. Arjuna killed him.
(Chapter 265 and 271, Vana Parva, M.B.).
