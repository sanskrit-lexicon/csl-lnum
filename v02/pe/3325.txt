KARAVĪRA III¦. There was once a country named
Karavīra on the base of the mountain Gomanta.
That country was being ruled by a King called
Sṛgālavāsudeva. He was killed by Śrī Kṛṣṇa and
Balarāma together as per instructions from Paraśurāma.
(10th Skandha, Bhāgavata).
