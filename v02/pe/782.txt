ĀŚĀVAHA I¦. A son of Kaśyapa and Aditi. From Viṣṇu
Brahmā, from Brahmā Marīci and from Marīci Kaśyapa
were born. Kaśyapa married Aditi, daughter of Dakṣa.
Aditi gave birth to ten sons: Bṛhadbhānu, Cakṣur-
ātmā, Vibhāvasu, Ṛcīka, Savitā, Arka, Bhānu, Āśāvaha,
Ravi and Vivasvān. Vaivasvata Manu was the son of
Vivasvān. (M.B., Ādi Parva, Chapter 1, Verse 42).
