VIDYUJJIHVA II¦. Husband of Śūrpaṇakhā. A son
named Śambhukumāra was born to the couple. As
this son Śambhukumāra was standing like a young tree
in the forest Daṇḍakāraṇya, Lakṣmaṇa cut it down by
his sword. Thus Śambhukumāra died. (Kamba
Rāmāyaṇa, Araṇya Kāṇḍa).
