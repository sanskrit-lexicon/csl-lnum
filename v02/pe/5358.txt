PRATARDANA¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} A King of the line of Pūru.
Pratardana who was the son of the daughter of King
Yayāti ruled the country after making Kāśī his capital.
Pratardana's mother was Mādhavī, Yayāti's daughter,
and father, Divodāsa. Pratardana once found
on his way his grandfather, King Yayāti, who had
fallen from Svarga. (See under Yayāti).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Emperor Śibi gave Pratardana a sword. (Śloka 80,
Chapter 166, Śānti Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He once gave a gift of a netra (eye) to the
brahmins. (Śloka 20, Chapter 224, Śānti Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) He slew the son of Vītahavya. (See under Vīta-
havya).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) Pratardana courted death after appointing his
sons for the service of brahmins. (Śloka 5, Chapter
137, Anuśāsana Parva).
