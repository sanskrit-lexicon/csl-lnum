SURĀDEVĪ¦ (VĀRUṆĪ). A daughter of Varuṇa born
of Devī, his brother's wife. She was the apple of the eye
to the Devas. She is the presiding Devatā over liquor
She lives in the court of Brahmā worshipping him.
(Sabhā Parva, Chapter 11, Verse 42; Ādi Parva, Chap-
ter 18, Verse 35 and Chapter 66, Verse 52).
