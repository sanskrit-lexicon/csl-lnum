VEGAVĀN II¦. An asura. It is mentioned in Mahā-
bhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 65, Stanza 24, that this
asura was the son born to Prajāpati Kaśyapa by his
wife Danu. It was this Vegavān who took rebirth as
the prince of Kekaya later. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter
67, Stanza 10).
