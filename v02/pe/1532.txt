CIDAMBARA¦ (SITĀMBARA). A Sanskrit poet of 16th
century A.D. He was a poet in the court of emperor
[Page182-b+ 65]
Veṅkaṭa who ruled over Vijayanagar during the period
1586 A.D. to 1614 A.D. His one great work is ‘Rāghava-
yādavapāṇḍavīya’. There are three Kāṇḍas in it and
one Kāṇḍa each is devoted to the story of Śrī Rāma,
Śrī Kṛṣṇa and the Pāṇḍavas.
