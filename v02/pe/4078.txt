MĀDRĪ¦. Mādrī who was the second wife of Pāṇḍu was
a daughter of the King of Madra. She was the sister
of Śalya. Nakula and Sahadeva took birth from Mādrī.
Pāṇḍu expired when he embraced his wife Mādrī. It
was because of a curse of the hermit Kindama. Mādrī
ended her life in the pyre with her husband. (For fur-
ther details see under the word PĀṆḌU).
