VASĀTI III¦. A King who fought on the side of Duryo-
dhana. When Abhimanyu entered Cakra-vyūha of the
army formed by Droṇa this King took a vow that he
would commit suicide if he did not kill Abhimanyu and
then ran to the scene of the battle. In the fight bet-
ween Vasāti and Abhimanyu, Vasāti was killed. (M.B.
Droṇa Parva, Chapter 43, Verses 8-10).
