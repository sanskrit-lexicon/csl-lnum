DHṚTARĀṢṬRĪ¦. A daughter born from Tāmrā the
wife of Kaśyapa Prajāpati. Tāmrā had five daughters:
Krauñcī, Bhāsī, Śyenī, Dhṛtarāṣṭrī and Śukī. Of these,
from Krauñcī, the owls, the Bhāsas from Bhāsī, the
eagle and vulture from Śyenī, the swans and the ruddy
goose from Dhṛtarāṣṭrī were born in the world. (This
occurs in Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa, Araṇya Kāṇḍa, Sarga 14).
