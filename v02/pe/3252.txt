KĀNTIVRATA¦. A Vrata observed in the month of
Kārttika. It consists in offering worship to Balarāma and
Śrī Kṛṣṇa and taking food only at night. By observing
this Vrata for one year, beauty, longevity and health
can be obtained. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 177).
