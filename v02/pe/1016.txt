BHALANDANA¦. A king. Though by birth he was a
brahmin he became a Vaiśya because of doing things
belonging to the Vaiśya community like trade and
commerce. (Mārkaṇḍeya Purāṇa). According to the
Bhāgavata, Viṣṇu Purāṇa and Vāyu Purāṇa Bhalan-
dana was the son of Nābhāga.
