NIVĪTA¦. Brahmin's wearing upper cloth or the sacred
thread on the left shoulder and under the right hand
is called Upavīta; the same on the right shoulder and
under the left hand is called prācīnāvīta and the same
worn as a garland around the neck is called Nivīta.
