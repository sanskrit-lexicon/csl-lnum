GANDHAMĀDANA I¦. A monkey, who had been help-
ful to Śrī Rāma, was the son of Kubera. (Vālmīki
Rāmāyaṇa, Bāla Kāṇḍa, Canto 17, Verse 11). This
monkey usually stayed on Mount Gandhamādana.
During the Rāma-Rāvaṇa war he led a contingent of
monkeys. (Vana Parva, Chapter 283, Verse 5).
