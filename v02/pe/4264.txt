MĀLYAVĀN V¦. A mountain situated in the country of
Kiṣkindhā. The fight between Bāli and Sugrīva took
place near this mountain. This is on the banks of the
river Tuṅgabhadrā. Uttara Rāmāyaṇa states that the
palace of Sugrīva was on the top of this mountain. Śrī
Rāma stayed on the beautiful peak of this mountain for
four months. (Śloka 40, Chapter 280, Vana Parva).
