HĀRĪTA I¦. A great sage who visited Bhīṣma in his bed
of arrows (Śaraśayyā). (Śanti Parva, Chapter 47,
Verse 7). Once he attended Yudhiṣṭhira's assembly
and spoke on eternal truths conducive to mental peace.
That talk of his became famous as Hārītagītā. (Śānti
Parva, Chapter 278).
