SUMITRA¦ IX. Son of King Suratha. Considered to
be the last King of the Ikṣvāku dynasty, Sumitra was
a contemporary of Kṣemaka of the Pūru dynasty and
Nanda of Magadha dynasty. Alexander conquered
India during his period. Sumitra is called Sumālya
also. (Bhāgavata, Skandha 9).
