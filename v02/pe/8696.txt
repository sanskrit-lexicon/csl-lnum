YAJÑABĀHU¦. An ancient sage who was the son of Priya-
vrata and grandson of Svāyambhuva Manu. Svāyam-
bhuva Manu had two famous sons named Priyavrata
and Uttānapāda. Of these, Priyavrata married Surūpā
and Barhiṣmatī, the two daughters of Viśvakarmā.
Yajñabāhu was the son of Priyavrata by Surūpā. The ten
sons born to Priyavrata and Surūpā were:—Agnīdhra,
Iddhmajihva, Yajñabāhu, Mahāvīra, Rukmaśukra,
Ghṛtapṛṣṭha, Savana, Medhātithi, Vītihotra and Kavi.
They had a daughter, Ūrjjasvatī. Bhāgavata, 8th
Skandha).
