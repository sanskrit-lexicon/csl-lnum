KEDĀRA¦. A holy place in the interior of Kurukṣetra.
It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter
83, that those who bathe in this holy Bath would
obtain the fruits of good actions. One of the twelve
Liṅgas consecrated in important places, is in
Kedāra.
