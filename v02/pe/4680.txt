NAIDHRUVA¦. A gotra-originator of the Kaśyapa dynasty.
He was the grandson of Maharṣi Kaśyapa and son of
Avatsara maharṣi. He was one of the six Brahmavādins
of the family of Kaśyapa the other five being Kaśyapa,
Avatsara, Raibhya, Asita and Devala. (Vāyu Purāṇa,
Chapter 52; Matsyapurāṇa, Chapter 145).
