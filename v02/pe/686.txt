ĀDIŚIŚIRA¦. One of the disciples of Śākalya. Vyāsa
made the Vedas and taught his son Śākalya a portion
of it. Śākalya divided his portion into five different
and equal divisions and taught each of his five disciples,
Vātsyāyana, Maudgalya, Śāli, Ādiśiśira and Gokhali a
division. (Skandha 12, Bhāgavata).
