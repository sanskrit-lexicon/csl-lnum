SUBHŪMIKA¦. A holy place of ancient days in the plains
of river Sarasvatī where Apsarās used to come daily and
bathe. Deva-Gandharvas visit this place every month.
Śrī Kṛṣṇa bathed there once and distributed money to
brahmins. (Śalya Parva, Chapter 37).
