APPAYYADĪKṢITA¦. A reputed Rhetorician in Sans-
krit, who thrived in the 16th Century A.D. He wrote
two treatises on rhetoric, the Kuvalayānanda and
Citramīmāṃsā. The former is an interpretation of
chapter 5 of Jayadeva's Candrāloka and is very popu-
larin South India.
