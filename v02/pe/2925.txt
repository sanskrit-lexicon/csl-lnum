JAYA I¦. A son of Dhṛtarāṣṭra. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chap-
ter 63, Stanza 119). He fought with Arjuna at the time
of the stealing of cows. (M.B. Virāṭa Parva, Chapter
54). This Jaya fought with Nīla and Bhīma in the
battle of Bhārata. Bhīma killed him. (Droṇa Parva,
Chapters 25 and 135).
