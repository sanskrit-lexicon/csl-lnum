ARUPATTIMŪVAR¦. ({%The sixty-three's%}). In ancient
times there were sixty-three devotees of Siva in the south.
They were called Nāyanāras and they have composed
many famous sacred songs. Prominent among them were
Tirujñānasambandha, Tirunāvukkaraśa and Sundara-
mūrti
