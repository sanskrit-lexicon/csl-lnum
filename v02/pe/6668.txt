ŚLIṢṬI¦. Son of Dhruva. Dhruva had two children
Śliṣṭi and Succhāyā by his wife Śambhu. Śliṣṭi married
Succhāyā. Five sons, Ripu, Ripuñjaya, Puṇya, Vṛkala
and Vṛkatejas, were born to them. Ripu, the eldest
of them, was the father of Cākṣuṣa Manu, according to
Harivaṃ a, Chapter 2.
