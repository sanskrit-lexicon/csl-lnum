NANDANA I¦. Son of Hiraṇyakaśipu. Nandana who
had been ruling over the Śveta island had obtained
boons from Śiva and had become invincible. He ruled
over the kingdom for ten thousand years and then
attained Kailāsa and became a gaṇa of Śiva. (Śiva
Purāṇa, Uttara Khaṇḍa, Chapter 2).
