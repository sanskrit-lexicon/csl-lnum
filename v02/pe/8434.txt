VĪRASIṂHA II¦. A prince who was the son of King
Vīramaṇi and the relative of Rukmāṅgada. It is stated
in Padma Purāṇa, Pātāla khaṇḍa that this prince Vīra-
siṃha tied the sacrificial horse of Śrī Rāma and fought
with Śatrughna who led the horse.
