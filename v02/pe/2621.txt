HARṢA II¦. A great poet in Sanskrit, who flourished in
the 12th century A.D., his most reputed work being the
Mahākāvya called Naiṣadha, one of the five Mahākāv-
yas (Epic Poems) in Sanskrit language. Another well-
known work of his is Khaṇḍanakhaṇḍakhādya. He was
a member of the literary assembly of King Jayacanda
of Kanauj. Hīra was his father and Māmalladevī, his
mother.
