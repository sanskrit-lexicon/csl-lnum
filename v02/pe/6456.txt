SATYAKARMĀ II¦. The brother of Suśarmā the king of
Trigarta. Though he took a vow in the Bhārata battle
that he would kill Arjuna, what happened was that he
was killed by Arjuna. (M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter 17,
Verse 17; Śalya Parva, Chapter 27, Verse 39).
