RAMYAKA I¦. A son of Agnīdhra, who had nine sons by
his wife Pūrvacittī. (Bhāgavata, 5th Skandha).
Ramyaka ruled the Kingdom called Ramyaka Varṣa
near Nīlagiri. Arjuna, during his triumphal tour con-
quered Ramyaka and levied taxes from the people there
(Sabhā Parva, Chapter 28).
