MUKULA¦. A King of the dynasty of Pūru. Mukula,
Sṛñjaya, Bṛhadiṣu, Yavīnara and Kṛmila were the sons
of King Bāhyāśva. All these five are known as Pāñcālas.
Of these the family of Mukula comprised of Maukulyas.
Mukula got a son named Pañcāśva. (Chapter 201,
Agni Purāṇa.)
