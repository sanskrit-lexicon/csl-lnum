DEVASTHĀNA¦.
<div n="NI"/>{%General.%} A maharṣi, who was a friend of the Pāṇḍavas.
{%Other information.%} (1) After the great war was over this muni
visited Dharmaputra and induced him to perform yajñas.
<div n="NI"/>(2) Along with some other munis he gave spiritual
advice to Dharmaputra and comforted him. (Śānti
Parva, Chapter 37, Verse 27).
<div n="NI"/>(3) He was one of the munis, who visited Bhīṣma on
his bed of arrows. (Śānti Parva, Chapter 47, Verse 5).
