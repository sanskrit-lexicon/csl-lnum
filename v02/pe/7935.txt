VAJRANĀBHA II¦. A King of the line of Śrī Rāma.
The genealogy is the following. Śrī Rāma-Kuśa-Aditi-
Niṣadha-Nabhas-Puṇḍarīka-Kṣemadhanvā-Devānīka-
Ṛkṣa-Pāriyātra-Bala-Vinda-Vajranābha. Khagaṇa was
the son of Vajranābha. (Bhāgavata, Skandha 9).
