MADHUVAṬĪ¦. A sacred place on the border of Kuru-
kṣetra. If one bathes in a sacred pond there and wor-
ships the Manes one will have to one's credit the benefit
of making a thousand Godānas (giving away cows in
charity). (Śloka 94, Chapter 83, Vana Parva).
