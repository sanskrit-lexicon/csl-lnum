BHĀRADVĀJA III¦. A grammarian. According to the
Ṛktantra, prātiśākhya of Sāmaveda, it was Brahmā,
who first composed the science of grammar. This
science was taught by Brahmā to others in the following
order: Brahmā to Bṛhaspati, he to Indra, Indra to
Bhāradvāja and he to his disciples.
<div n="NI"/>Pāṇini has discussed the grammatical concepts of Bhāra-
dvāja. Ṛkprātiśākhya and Taittirīya have quoted the
opinions of this grammarian.
