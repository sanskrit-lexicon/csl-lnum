SAṂGAVA¦ (SAṄGAVA). The supervisor of the
cattle-shed of Duryodhana. It is stated in Mahābhārata,
Vana Parva, Chapter 228 that this Saṅgava helped
Duryodhana in the fight which ensued at the time of
the great procession conducted by the Kauravas, to
the Pāṇḍavas in the forest.
