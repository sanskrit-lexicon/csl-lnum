KUKARDAMA¦. A wicked monarch of Piṇḍārakadeśa.
He had to be born in Pretayoni (womb of a ghostess)
due to his many sins, and once he came to the āśrama
of Kahoḍa, and the sage recognised him as his former
disciple and absolved him from his ghosthood. (Padma
Purāṇa, Uttara Khaṇḍa, Chapter 139).
