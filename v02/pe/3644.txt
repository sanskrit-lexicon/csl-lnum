KRODHAVAŚA¦. A follower of Indrajit. In the battle
between Rāvaṇa and Śrī Rāma this Asura made himself
invisible and attacked the monkeys. By the help of
Vibhīṣaṇa, who was an expert in the art of vanishing,
the monkeys killed Krodhavaśa in the battle. (M.B.
Vana Parva, Chapter 269).
