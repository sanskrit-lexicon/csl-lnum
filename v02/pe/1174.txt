BHOJAKAṬA¦. The capital of Vidarbha. Once Saha-
deva, one of the Pāṇḍavas conquered this city. It was
at this place that Śrī Kṛṣṇa defeated Rukmī, the brother
of Rukmiṇī at the time of Rukmiṇī's Svayaṃvara. The
original name of Bhojakaṭa was “Kuṇḍinapura”.
(M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 31 and Udyoga Parva,
Chapter 158).
