SATYASANDHA¦. I. (SATYAVRATA, SATYASENA,
SANDHA). One of the hundreds ons of Dhṛtarāṣṭra. The
following information about this Satyasandha is from
Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(1) This Satyasandha was one of the eleven great
chariot-fighters in the army of the Kauravas. (M.B. Ādi
Parva, Chapter 63, Verse 119).
<div n="NI"/>(2) This Satyasandha stood as the bodyguard of Śalya
and fought in the Bhārata-battle. (M.B. Bhīṣma
Parva, Chapter 62, Verse 17).
<div n="NI"/>(3) Abhimanyu wounded him in the Bhārata-battle.
(M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 73, Verse 24).
<div n="NI"/>(4) Sātyaki showered arrows on Satyasandha. (M.B.
Droṇa Parva, Chapter 116, Verse 7).
<div n="NI"/>(5) Bhīmasena killed Satyasandha in the battle of
Bhārata. (M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter 84, Verse 2).
