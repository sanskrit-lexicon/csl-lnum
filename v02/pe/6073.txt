SAṂHLĀDA¦ (SAṂHRĀDA) I. A son of Hiraṇyakaśipu.
Two sons called Hiraṇyākṣa and Hiraṇyakaśipu and a
daughter called Siṃhikā were born to Kaśyapaprajāpati
by his wife called Diti, and Hiraṇyakaśipu had four sons
called Anuhrāda, Hrāda, Prahlāda and Saṃhrāda.
Saṃhrāda had three sons named Āyuṣmān. Śibi, and
Bāṣkala. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 19). According to
verse 17, Chapter 65 of Ādi Parva, Prahlāda, Saṃhlāda,
Anuhlāda, Śibi and Bāṣkala were the sons of Hiraṇya-
kaśipu. This Saṃhlāda, in his next life, was born as
Śalya, the Bālhīka king.
