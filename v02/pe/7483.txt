TAMASĀ¦. A sacred river. People of Bhārata were using
the water of this river for drinking. (Śloka 3, Chapter
9, Bhīṣma Parva). The famous āśrama of Vālmīki was
on the banks of this river. This place was the scene of
the well-known episode of the Krauñca birds and the
famous curse of Vālmīki beginning with ‘Mā niṣāda’.
(Bāla Kāṇḍa, Sarga 2, Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa).
