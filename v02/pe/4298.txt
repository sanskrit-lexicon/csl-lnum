MANDARĀ¦. One of the wives of Viśvakarmā. The
monkey named Nala was the son of Mandarā. This
monkey was the chief of those who helped Śrī Rāma
to build a bridge to Laṅkā. Viśvakarmā had once
blessed Mandarā saying that her son would one day
become great. (Sarga 22, Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa).
