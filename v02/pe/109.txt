AIRĀVATA II¦. Name of a serpent born to Kaśyapa and
his wife Kadru. This is mentioned in Śloka 5, Chapter
35, Ādi Parva of the Mahābhārata. Arjuna's father-in-
law and Ulūpi's father, Kauravya belonged to the family
of this serpent. (See Śloka 18, Chapter 213, Ādi
Parva).
