VARAṆĀ¦. A river famous in the Purāṇas. It is mention-
ed in Vāmana Purāṇa that the river Varaṇā which
flowed from the right leg and the river Asī, which
started from the left leg of Bhagavān, who lies in con-
templation at Prayāga, are two holy rivers. (See under
Vārāṇasī).
