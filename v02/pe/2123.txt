DṚḌHASYU¦. A son born to Agastya maharṣi by Lopā-
mudrā. He was born only after remaining in the
mother's womb for seven years. He was a very great
scholar and tapasvī. He began studying the Vedas
from the very time of his birth. He is called Idhma-
vāha also. (See Idhmavāha).
