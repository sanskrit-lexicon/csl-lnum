VASUDĀNA I¦. A Kṣatriya King. This King who was
a member of the council of Yudhiṣṭhira. was the ruler
of Pāṃśu. It is stated in Mahābhārata that this King
presented Yudhiṣṭhira with twentysix elephants and
two thousand horses. (Sabhā Parva, Chapter 52, Stanza
27). In the Bhārata battle this King was the support
of Yudhiṣṭhira and Dhṛṣṭadyumna. But he was killed
by the arrow of Droṇa. (M.B. Udyoga Parva, Chapters
151 and 171; Droṇa Parva, Chapters 23 and 190; Karṇa
Parva, Chapter 6).
