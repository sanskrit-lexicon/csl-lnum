PĀVAKA¦. A son of Agni. Agni got of his wife Svāhā
three sons, Pāvaka, Pavamāna and Śuci. These three
brilliant sons got together 45 sons and they were also
called Agnis. Thus there were 49 Agnis made up of the
father, his three sons and their 45 sons. Pāvaka had
another name also—Mahān (Chapter 219, Vana Parva).
