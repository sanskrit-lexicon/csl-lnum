ŚRUTĀYUS III¦. A Kṣatriya king. He was born from a
portion of the Daitya, Krodhavaśa. We get the follow-
ing details about him from Mahābhārata:
<div n="NI"/>(i) Śrutyāyus was the re-birth of Krodhavaśa, the
Daitya. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 67, Verse 64).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Śrutāyus, who was a Mahāratha (a great warrior)
was present at the Svayaṃvara of Draupadī. (M.B.
Ādi Parva, Chapter 185, Verse 21).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) This Śrutāyus was a member of Yudhiṣṭhira's
assembly. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 4, Verse 28).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) On the first day of Bhārata battle, there was a
clash between Śrutāyus and Irāvān. (M.B. Bhīṣma
Parva, Chapter 45, Verse 69).
<div n="NI"/>(v) He was the King of Ambaṣṭha country. In the
Bhārata battle he encountered Arjuna. (M.B. Bhīṣma
Parva, Chapter 59, Verse 75).
<div n="NI"/>(vi) It was this Śrutāyus who stood in the middle
portion of the Krauñca Vyūha formed by Bhīṣma in
the above battle. (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 75,
Verse 22).
<div n="NI"/>(vii) Yudhiṣṭhira defeated Śrutāyus. (M.B. Bhīṣma
Parva, Chapter 84, Verse 1).
<div n="NI"/>(viii) At Kurukṣetra, Arjuna killed Śrutāyus in the
battle. Arjuna had to fight a fierce battle in order to
kill Śrutāyus. (M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter 98).
