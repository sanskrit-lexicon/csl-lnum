GUHYAKA¦. A Yakṣa. (A division of Yakṣas who were
prominent members of the court of Kubera). They were
present at the marriage of Draupadī. (Śloka 7, Chapter
186, Ādi Parva).

<div n="NI"/>(i) The palace of Kubera in the sky is borne by Guh-
yakas. (Śloka 3, Chapter 10, Sabhā Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Bhīmasena slew many Guhyakas on the mountain
of Gandhamādana. (Śloka 55, Chapter 11, Śalya
Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Some of the soldiers who died in the Mahābhā-
rata battle went to the world of the Guhyakas. (Śloka
