ŚATĀNĪKA III¦. A prince born as the son of Janame-
jaya (Parīkṣit's son) of his wife called Vapuṣṭamā. He
married the princess of Videha and to the couple was
born a son called Aśvameghadatta. (Ādi Parva, Chap-
ter 96, Verse 88). He had also another son named
Sahasrānīka. (Kathāsaritsāgara). (See under Udayana).
