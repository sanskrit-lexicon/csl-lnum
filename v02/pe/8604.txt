VṚJA¦. A hermit who was born in the family of emperor
Pṛthu. Antardhāna and Vādī were the two sons of
Pṛthu. A son named Havirdhāna was born to Antar-
dhāna by his wife Śikhaṇḍinī. Dhiṣaṇā who was born
in the family of Agni, became the wife of Havir-
dhāna. Prācīnabarhis, Śukra, Gaya, Kṛṣṇa, Vṛja and
Ajina were the six sons of Havirdhāna by Dhiṣaṇā.
Of these, Prācīnabarhis became a great Prajāpati.
(Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśa 1, Chapter 14).
