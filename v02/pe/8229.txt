VIBHĪṢAṆA II¦. Mention is made in Mahābhārata, as
given below, about another Vibhīṣaṇa who had ruled

<div n="NI"/>Once Ghaṭotkaca went to the palace of Vibhīṣaṇa as the
messenger of Sugrīva. Vibhīṣaṇa who heard from
Ghṭotkaca about Yudhiṣṭhira honoured the messenger
greatly and gave him a large quantity of valuable
presents.
