MAKARĀKṢA¦. Son of the demon Khara. In the epic
battle between Rāma and Rāvaṇa after the death of
Kumbha and Nikumbha the most prominent fighter
who entered the battle-field on the side of Rāvaṇa was
Makarākṣa. He gave a great fight to Rāma but was in
the end killed by him. (Sarga 79, Yuddha Kāṇḍa, Vāl-
mīki Rāmāyaṇa).
