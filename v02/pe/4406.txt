MĀRKAṆḌEYAPURĀṆA¦. One of the eighteen
Purāṇas. The number of granthas in it is nine thousand.
It contains a critical study of Dharma and Adharma.
It is considered to be very good to give this Purāṇa as
[Page489-b+ 65]
gift on the full-moon day in the month of Kārttika
(November). (Chapter 272, Agni Purāṇa).
