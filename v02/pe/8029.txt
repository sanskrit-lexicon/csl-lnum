VARATRI¦. One of the four sons of Śuka. The other
three were, Pṛthuraśmi, Bṛhadaṅgiras and Rajata.
Devendra killed them because they were against sacrifice.
It is stated in Brahmāṇḍa Purāṇa that after a while
from the severed heads of these sons date palm trees
grew up.
