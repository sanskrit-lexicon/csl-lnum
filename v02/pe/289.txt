ANDHRAKA II¦. The warriors of Andhra Deśa were
called Andhrakas also. (M.B., Karṇa Parva, Chapter
20, Verses 10 and 11). In the Bhārata war the King of
Pāṇḍya defeated those warriors. Kṛṣṇa incited Arjuna
to kill the Andhras and the Pulindas. (M.B., Karṇa
Parva, Chapter 73, Verses 19 to 21).
