SŪRYADATTA¦. A brother of King Virāṭa. It is stated
in Mahābhārata, Virāṭa Parva, Chapter 31, Stanza 11,
that this Sūryadatta was known by the name Śatānīka
also. Sūryadatta also took part in the fight following
the theft of the cattle of Virāṭa by Duryodhana and
others. After killing the hundred warriors in the army
of Trigarta, this Sūryadatta entered into the midst of
the enemies and was killed by Droṇa in the battle of
Bhārata. (M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter 6, Verse 34).
