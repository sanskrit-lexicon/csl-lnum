PARIHARA¦. A King of the country of Kālañjara
situated near Citrakūṭa. Parihara who was an
Atharvaparāyaṇa (devoted to the Atharvaveda) ruled
the country for twelve years and during his reign he
gave a strong opposition to the spread of Buddhism.
(Pratisarga Parva, Bhaviṣya Purāṇa).
