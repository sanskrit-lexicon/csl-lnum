AVANTI¦. A country called Mālava in ancient
India. The famous Ujjayinī was the capital of Avanti.
This city is situated on the bank of Śiprā. The renown-
ed poets such as Kālidāsa and others, lived in this
city. This is one of the seven cities considered to be
capable of giving Mokṣa (heavenly bliss). (M.B.,
Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 9, Stanza 43).
