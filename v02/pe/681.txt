ĀDIGADĀDHARA¦. It was with the bones of an asura
(demon) named Gada that Mahāviṣṇu made the first
gadā (mace). By that mace Viṣṇu killed Heti and other
asuras of that lot and got the name Ādigadādhara (He
who first handled the mace). (See under Gadā).
