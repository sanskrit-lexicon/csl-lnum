PALĀŚAVANA¦. A sacred forest. Once the sage Jama-
dagni performed a sacrifice in this forest. On that occa-
sion all the rivers in the world were present there
carrying their water. The sage at this Yāga gave wine to
all the other sages to their heart's content. (Śloka 16,
Chapter 94, Vana Parva).
