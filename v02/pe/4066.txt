MADIRĀ¦. Wife of Vasudeva, father of Śrī Kṛṣṇa. Vasu-
deva had seven wives named Rohiṇī, Bhadramāninī,
Madirā, Ilā, Rocanā, Pauravī and Devakī (9th Skandha,
Bhāgavata). Of these Devakī, Rohiṇī, and Bhadrā
abandoned their lives by jumping into the funeral pyre
of Vasudeva. (Śloka 18, Chapter 7, Mausala Parva).
