MACAKRUTA¦. A sacred place on the border of
[Page458-b+ 65]
Kurukṣetra. A Yakṣa of name Macakruta keeps guard
over this place. If one bows down before this Yakṣa

Godānas (giving away cows in charity). (Chapter
83, Vana Parva, M.B.).
