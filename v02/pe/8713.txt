YAKṢĪ¦ (YAKṢIṆĪ). Women folk of Yakṣas. Agni
Purāṇa, Chapter 50 mentions that the images of Yakṣī
to be installed in temples should have fixed and
long eyes. Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 54,
Verse 105, states that Yakṣiṇī is a Devī and by eating
the prasāda (naivedya) of the Devī one would be absolv-
ed of the sin of Brahmahatyā (killing a Brāhmaṇa).
