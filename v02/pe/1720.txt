DAṆḌADHARA I¦. A Kṣatriya King of Magadha.
The following information about this King is available
from the Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>1) Daṇḍadhara was born as the rebirth of a giant
known as Krodhavardhana. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter
67, Stanza 46).
<div n="NI"/>2) Bhīmasena, during his conquest of the countries,
overcame King Daṇḍadhara and his brother Daṇḍa.
[Page201-a+ 64]
(M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 30, Stanza 17).
<div n="NI"/>3) In the battle between the Pāṇḍavas and the Kaura-
vas, Daṇḍadhara fought from the back of an elephant
against the Pāṇḍavas. When Daṇḍadhara began to
exterminate the army of the Pāṇḍavas, Śrī Kṛṣṇa per-
suaded Arjuna to fight against Daṇḍadhara, who was
killed in the fight. (M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter 8,
Stanzas 1 to 13).
