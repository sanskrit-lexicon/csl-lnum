BHŪTĀLAYA¦(M). A famous village in the purāṇas.
It was a resort of thieves and robbers. Dead bodies
were often thrown into the river which was flowing
through this village. Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chap-
ter 129, verse 9 says that it was against Śāstras to bathe
in this river.
