ŚALA V¦. Son of King Parīkṣit of the Ikṣvāku dynasty.
Suśobhanā, daughter of the Maṇḍūka King was his
mother. He had two brothers called Dala and Bala.
Śala did not return some horses, which he had taken
as loan from Vāmadeva maharṣi. Angered at this the
maharṣi created some Rākṣasas, who killed Śala. (Vana
Parva, Chapter 192).
