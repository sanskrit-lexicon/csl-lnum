PARṆĀDA II¦. A brahmin of the country of Vidarbha.
It was Parṇāda who was sent out to know whether
Nala was alive and he found out Nala living in
disguise under the name of Bāhuka and after collecting
all information about him returned to Vidarbha. (See
under Damayantī).
