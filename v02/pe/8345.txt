VIKUÑJA¦. An ancient country in India. Mention is
made in Mahābhārata, Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 56,
Stanza 9, that it was the warriors of this country who
stood with the king Bṛhadbala on the left wing of the
Garuḍa Vyūha of the army formed by Bhīṣma, in the
battle of Bhārata.
