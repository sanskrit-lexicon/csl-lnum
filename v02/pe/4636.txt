MŪṢAKĀDA¦ (MŪṢIKĀDA). A serpent born to
Kaśyapaprajāpati of his wife Kadrū. This serpent sits
in the court of Varuṇa and worships him. Once Nārada
introduced this nāga to Mātali. (Śloka 10, Chapter 9,
Sabhā Parva and Śloka 14, Chapter 103, Udyoga
Parva).
