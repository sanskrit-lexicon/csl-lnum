SĀRAṆA I¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} A Kṣatriya of the Yadu clan.
It is stated in Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 218,
Verse 17, that he was the son of Vasudeva by Devakī
and the brother of Śrī Kṛṣṇa and Subhadrā.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) This Sāraṇa was one of those who took the dowry
to Hastināpura at the marriage of Arjuna and
Subhadrā. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 220, Verse 32).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Sāraṇa shone in the court of Yudhiṣṭhira: (M.B.
Sabhā Parva, Chapter 4, Verse 30).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Sāraṇa participated in the Rājasūya sacrifice of
Yudhiṣṭhira. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 34, Verse 15).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) When Śrī Krṣṇa went to Hastināpura from
Dvārakā to take part in the horse-sacrifice of Yudhi-
[Page694-b+ 65]
ṣṭhira, Sāraṇa also accompanied him. (M.B. Aśva-
medha Parva, Chapter 66, Verse 4).
<div n="NI"/>(v) Sāraṇa was the foremost of those who disguised
Samba the son of Jāmbavatī as a woman, and abused
the hermits. (M.B. Mausala Parva, Chapter 1, Verse 15).
