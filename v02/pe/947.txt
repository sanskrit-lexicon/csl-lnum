BĀRHASPATYA¦. The Nītiśāstra of Brahmā. Bṛhaspati
condensed and codified the laws of Ethics by Brahmā.
This was compiled by Bṛhaspati. This book contains
three thousand chapters. Mention is made about this
Book in Mahābhārata, Śānti Parva, Chapter 59, Stanza
84.
