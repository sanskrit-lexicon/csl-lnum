ŪRJJĀ I¦. A wife of Vasiṣṭha. Seven sons named Rajas,
Gotra, Ūrdhvabāhu, Savana, Anagha, Sutapas and
Śukra, were born to Vasiṣṭha by his wife Ūrjjā. These
seven holy men were the seven hermits (Saptarṣis) in
the third Manvantara. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśa 1,
Chapter 10).
