REṆUKĀ II¦. A holy place frequented by Sages. It is
mentioned in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 82.
Stanza 82 that those who bathe in this holy bath would
become as pure as Candra (Moon). It is stated in
Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 82, that this holy
place lies within the boundary of Kurukṣetra.
