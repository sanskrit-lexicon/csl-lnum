CANDRASENA IV¦. Another Candrasena, a partisan of
the Kauravas, is referred to in the Bhārata as having
fought against the Pāṇḍavas. His duty was to guard the
chariot wheels of Śalya; he was killed by Yudhiṣṭhira.
(Śalya Parva, Chapter 12, Verse 52).
