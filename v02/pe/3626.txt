KRAUÑCĪ¦. A daughter born to Kaśyapaprajāpati by
his wife Tāmrā the daughter of Dakṣa. Five daughters,
Krauñcī, Bhāsī, Śyenī, Dhṛtarāṣṭrī and Śukī were born
of Tāmrā. From Krauñcī, were born the owls, the Bhāsas
from Bhāsī, the hawks and vultures from Śyenī, the
swans and ruddy geese from Dhṛtarāṣṭrī and Narā, the
mother of Vinatā from Śukī. (Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa,
Sarga 14).
