ANU¦. The Prince named Aṇu was the son-in-law of
Śuka Brahmarṣi, son of Vyāsa. Śuka Brahmarṣi married
the beautiful Pīvarī, the daughter of the Pitṛs. Śuka-
deva and Pīvarī had four sons: Kṛṣṇa, Gauraprabha,
Bhūri and Devaśruta and a daughter named Kīrti.
Kīrti was married to Prince Aṇu the son of Digbhrāja.
(Devī Bhāgavata, Prathama Skandha).
