BĀLADHI¦. An ancient hermit. He was powerful. He
performed a severe penance for getting sons. The
Devatās appeared and granted him a boon. Accordingly
a son called Medhāvin was born to him. Once Medhāvin
teased the great hermit Dhanuṣākṣa, who cursed him to
death. (M.B., Vana Parva, Chapter 135).
