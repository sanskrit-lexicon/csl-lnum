EKACAKRA¦. A famous demon born to Kaśyapaprajā-
pati of his wife Danu. Demons Śaṃbara, Vipracitti,
Namuci, Pulomā, Viśruta, Durjaya, Ayaśśiras, Aśvaśi-
ras, Ketu, Vṛṣaparvā, Aśvagrīva, Virūpākṣa, Nikuṃbha,
Kapaṭa and Ekapāt are brothers of Ekacakra and are
equally famous. (Chapter 65, Ādi Parva, M.B.).
