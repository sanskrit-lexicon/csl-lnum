BṚHADRATHA II¦. A king of the Aṅga family. It is
stated in Agnipurāṇa, chapter 277 that he was the son
of Jayadratha and that Viśvajit was the son of Bṛha-
dratha. The Lamsuras, a forest tribe of the mountain
of Gṛddhrakūṭa, saved this King from the attempt of
extermination of the Kṣatriyas by Paraśurāma.
(Mahābhārata, Śānti Parva, Chapter 49; Droṇa Parva,
Chapters 57 and 62).
