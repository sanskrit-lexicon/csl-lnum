DĀSA¦. A term used as a suffix to the name of a Śūdra. In
ancient India the rule was that the proper suffix for a
Brahmin's name should be ‘Śarmā’, for a Kṣatriya's name,
‘Varmā’, for a Vaiśya's name, ‘Gupta’ and for a Śūdra's
name, ‘Dāsa’. (See under Cāturvarṇya).
