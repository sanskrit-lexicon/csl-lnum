KOHALA¦. A Brahmin scholar. It is stated in Mahā-
bhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 53, Stanza 4, that this
Brahmin was present at the ‘Sarpa Sattra’ (sacrifice to
kill serpents) of Janamejaya. Once Bhagīratha gave
this hermit as alms one lakh of cows with calves. (M.B.
Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 138, Stanza 27).
