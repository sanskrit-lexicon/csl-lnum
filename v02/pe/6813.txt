STHŪLAŚIRAS I¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} A hermit. It is observed that
he was alive in the ages of both Rāmāyaṇa and Mahā-
bhārata.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) He shone in the court of Yudhiṣṭhira. (M.B.
Sabhā Parva, Chapter 4, Verse 11).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva,
Chapter 135, Verse 8, that Yudhiṣṭhira once visited his
beautiful hermitage.
<div n="NI"/>(iii) In olden days this hermit had performed penance
on the North East side of Meru. (M.B. Śānti Parva,
Chapter 243, Verse 59).
[Page744-a+ 64]
<div n="NI"/>(iv) It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Anuśāsana
Parva, Chapter 26, Verse 5, that this hermit Sthūla-
śiras was one of the hermits who had visited Bhīṣma on
his bed of arrows.
<div n="NI"/>(v) Kabandha became a Rākṣasa (giant) because of
the curse of Sthūlaśiras. (For detailed story see under
Kabandha).
