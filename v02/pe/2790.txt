IRĀVATĪ I¦. Grand daughter of Kaśyapa. Ten daughters
were born to Kaśyapa by his wife Krodhavaśā, viz.
Mṛgī, Mṛgamandā, Harī, Bhadramatā, Mātaṃgī,
Śārdūlī, Śvetā, Surabhi and Kadrū, and Irāvatī was
Kadrū's daughter. (Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa, Araṇya
Kāṇḍa).
