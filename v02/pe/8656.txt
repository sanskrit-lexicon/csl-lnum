VṚṢAPARVĀ I¦. A noble Asura. The following inform-
ation about him is taken from Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Vṛṣaparvā was born to Prajāpati Kaśyapa by his
wife Danu. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 65, Stanza 24).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He took rebirth in the earth as King Dīrghaprajña.
(M.B. Ādi Parva Chapter 67, Stanza 15).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) He had a daughter named Śarmiṣṭhā. (See under
Devayānī).
