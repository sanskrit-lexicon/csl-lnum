AKARKKARA¦. A serpent, son of Kadru.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Descended from Viṣṇu in this order:
Brahmā-Marīci-Kaśyapa-Akarkkara.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Birth.%} Kaśyapa married the eight daughters of
Dakṣa named Aditi, Diti, Danu, Kālikā, Tāmrā,
Krodhavaśā, Manu and Analā. Krodhavaśā got ten
sons. One of them is Kadru. Ananta and Akarkkara
were born of Kadru. (Śloka 16, Chapter 35, Ādi Parva,
M.B.).
