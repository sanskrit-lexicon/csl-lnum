ŚATRUÑJAYA II¦. A son of Dhṛtarāṣṭra. The following
facts about him are gathered from the Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Duryodhana deputed him for the protection of
Bhīṣma in the great war. (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 51,
Verse 8).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He attacked the five Kekaya kings. (Bhīṣma Parva,
Chapter 79, Verse 56).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Bhīma killed him. (Droṇa Parva, Chapter 227,
Verse 29).
