JAṬHARA II¦. A mountain within the ranges of Mahā-
meru. On the eastern side of Mahāmeru there exist two
mountains called Jaṭhara and Devakūṭa, 18,000
yojanas in extent and 2000 yojanas high. On the
western side of Meru are the mountains called Pava-
māna and Pāriyātra, on the southern side Kailāsa and
Karavīra, and on the northern side Triśṛṅga and
Makaragiri. (Devī Bhāgavata, 8th Skandha).
