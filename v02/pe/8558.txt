VĪṬĀ¦. A ball made of wood. The Kaurava boys played
with this ball and by accident the Vīṭā fell in a well. It
is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 130,
Stanza 17, that the teacher Droṇa recovered it from the
well by shooting a number of arrows, one upon the tail
of another.
