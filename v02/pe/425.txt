ARDHASĀDHAKA¦. A minister of King Daśaratha. He
had eight ministers.
<div n="NI"/>“The great king had eight ministers who were pure
and famous and experts in dealing with matters of their
concern: Jayanta, Dhṛṣṭi, Vijaya, Siddha, Ardha-
sādhaka, Aśoka, Mantrapāla and Sumantra were
they”. (Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa, Bālakāṇḍa, Sarga 7,
Stanzas 2 and 3: Vallathol Translation).
