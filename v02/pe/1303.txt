BṚHADDHALA¦ I A great King of Kosala. He gave
fourteen thousand best horses for the Rājasūya (impe-
rial inauguration) sacrifice performed by Yudhiṣṭhira
as present. In the battle of Mahābhārata, Bṛhaddhala
sided with the Kauravas and fought against the Pāṇḍa-
vas and was killed in the battle by Abhimanyu. (M.B.
Droṇa Parva, 46; 24; Bhāgavata, Skandha 9).
