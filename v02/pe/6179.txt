ŚAṄKHA II¦. A nāga born to Kaśyapaprajāpati of
Kadrū. The following information about it is from the
Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Nārada once introduced Śaṅkha to Mātali,
charioteer of Indra (Udyoga Parva, Chapter 103,
Verse 12).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Śaṅkha was one of the chief nāgas which came to
lead the soul of Balabhadra to Pātāla at the time of his
death. (Mausala Parva, Chapter 4, Verse 7).
