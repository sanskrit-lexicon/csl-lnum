INDRASENA III¦. Mahābhārata, Sabhā Parva, Chapter
33. Verse 30 refers to one Indrasena, charioteer of
the Pāṇḍavas. He accompanied the Pāṇḍavas in their
forest life. (M.B. Vana Parva, Chapter 1, Verse 11).
<div n="NI"/>When the Pāṇḍavas arrived at Mount Gandhamādana
they left Indrasena with Subāhu, the Pulinda King.
(M.B. Vana Parva, Chapter 140, Verse 27). After
some time the Pāṇḍavas sent him to Dvārakā. (M.B.
Virāṭa Parva, Chapter 4, Verse 58). Indrasena was
present at the wedding of Abhimanyu which was
celebrated in the city of Upaplavya. (M.B. Virāṭa
Parva, Chapter 72, Verse 23).
