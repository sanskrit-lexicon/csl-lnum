ŚALA IV¦. Son of Somadatta, a King born in the Kuru
dynasty. He had a brother called Bhūriśravas.
<div n="NI"/>(1) Śala was present at the wedding of Draupadī.
(Ādi Parva, Chapter 185, Verse 15).
<div n="NI"/>(2) He was present at Yudhiṣṭhira's Rājasūya yajña.
(Sabhā Parva, Chapter 34, Verse 8).
<div n="NI"/>(3) He was a fierce warrior in Duryodhana's army.
(Udyoga Parva, Chapter 55, Verse 68).
<div n="NI"/>(4) He fought from the southern wing of the great
vyūha set up by Bhīṣma. (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 51,
Verse 57).
<div n="NI"/>(5) He fought with Abhimanyu in the great war.
(Droṇa Parva, Chapter 37).
<div n="NI"/>(6) He was killed by Śrutavarman in the battlefield
of Kurukṣetra. (Droṇa Parva, Chapter 108, Verse 10).
<div n="NI"/>(7) He was one among the valiant souls brought to
[Page672-b+ 64]
the surface of the Gaṅgā by Vyāsa (Āśramavāsika
Parva, Chapter 32, Verse 10).
<div n="NI"/>(8) After death he became one with the Viśvadevas.
(Svargārohaṇa Parva, Chapter 5, Verse 16).
