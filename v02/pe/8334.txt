VIKAṬA III¦. (VIKAṬĀNANA). One of the hundred sons
of Dhṛtarāṣṭra. In the Bhārata-battle, fourteen sons of
Dhṛtarāṣṭra joined together and wounded Bhīmasena.
Vikaṭa was one of them. This Vikaṭa was killed by
Bhīmasena. (Mahābhārata, Karṇa Parva, Chapter 51).
