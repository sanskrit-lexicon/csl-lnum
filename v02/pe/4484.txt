MAYŪRA¦. An asura who fought against Subrahmaṇya.
Skanda Purāṇa, Vīramahendra Kāṇḍa describes the
terrible battle between Mayūra and Subrahmaṇya.
Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 65 says that after
death, Mayūra was reborn in the world as a King named
Viśva.
