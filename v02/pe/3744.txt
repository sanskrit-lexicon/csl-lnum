KṢĪRASĀGARA¦. Sea of Milk. It is mentioned in
Mahābhārata, Udyoga Parva, Chapter 102, that
Brahmā drank too much of Amṛta and vomited. From
the vomit the cow Surabhi came into being. The milk
of Surabhi flowed and collected into a sea. This sea is
called the milk-sea or Kṣīrasāgara.
