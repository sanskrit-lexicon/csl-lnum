VIBHĀVARĪ¦. A mental daughter of Brahmā. She is
considered to be the personation of Night. It is mention-
ed in Matsya Purāṇa, Chapter 154, that according to
the instruction of Brahmā, Vibhāvarī entered the body
of Umā and from that day onwards the body of Umā
became dark.
