VIKṢARA¦. A noble asura born to Kaśyapa Prajāpati
by his wife Danu. The most powerful of the Dānavas
(asuras) given birth to by Danū, were Vikṣara, Bala,
Vīra and Vṛtra. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 65, Stanza
33). Of these Vikṣara took rebirth later as King
Vasumitra. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 67, Stanza 41).
