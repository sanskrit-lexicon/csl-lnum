PURUJIT¦. A king who was the son of Kuntibhoja and
brother of Kuntī, mother of the Pāṇḍavas. He had a
brother named Kuntibhoja. In the great battle he
fought against Durmukha of the Kaurava army. When
he died Purujit went to Yamaloka. (Chapter 14, Sabhā
Parva; Chapter 6, Karṇa Parva, Chapter 23, Droṇa
Parva).
