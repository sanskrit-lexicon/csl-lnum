PINĀKA¦. The bow of Śiva. Pīnāka was formerly the
three-forked spike of Śiva. Once it fell down from the
hands of Śiva and on falling down took the shape of a
bow. From then onwards it was used as a bow.
See under Pāśupata. (Chapter 38. Sabhā Parva:
Chapter 141, Anuśāsana Parva; Chapter 289. Śānti
Parva).
