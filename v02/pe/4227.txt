MAKARAGIRI¦. A mountain near Mahāmeru. Mahā-
meru is two thousand yojanas long and six hundred
yojanas high and stands in the midst of eight mountains
two on each of the four sides. Makaragiri and Triśṛṅga
are on the north side. On the east are Jaṭhara and
Devakūṭa, on the south are Kailāsa and Karavīra and on
the west are Pavamāna and Pāriyātra. (8th Skandha,
Devī Bhāgavata).
