VIVASVĀN IV¦. The first human being who performed
sacrifice. This Vivasvān is considered to be the father
of Manu and Yama. (Ṛgveda 8. 52; 10; 14, 16). In
Taittirīyasaṃhitā, mention is made that people of the
earth are the children of this Vivasvān. (Taittirīya
Saṃhitā, 6. 5. 6).
