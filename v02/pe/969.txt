BHADRA¦(M). A kingdom in ancient India. The Kṣa-
triya princes of Bhadram gave costly presents to
Dharmaputra at the Rājasūya Yāga (Sabhā Parva,
Chapter 52, Verse 14). Karṇa, in the course of his
digvijaya (conquest of countries) subjugated this king-
dom. (Vana Parva, Chapter 254.)
