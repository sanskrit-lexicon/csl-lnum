TEJAŚCAṆḌA¦. A deva who adorns the god Sūrya with a
flower garland daily. The idol of Teja caṇḍa is also used
to be installed in temples. The idol is figured as one
with a huge face and holding a sword and a lotus in his
hands. (Chapter 51, Agni Purāṇa).
