MAHĀVĪRA I¦. A son of Priyavrata. Svāyambhuva
Manu had two famous sons named Priyavrata and
Uttānapāda. Of them, Priyavrata married Surūpā and
Barhiṣmatī, the two beautiful and virtuous daughters of
Viśvakarma Prajāpati. By his first wife Surūpā, Priya-
vrata had ten sons, namely, Agnīdhra, Idhmajihva,
Yajñabāhu, Mahāvīra, Rukmaśukra, Ghṛtapṛṣṭha,
Savana, Medhātithi, Vītihotra and Kavi. The youngest
of his children was a daughter named Ūrjjasvatī. (Devī
Bhāgavata, 8th Skandha).
