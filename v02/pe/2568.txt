HAṂSAPATHA¦ (M). A region famous in the Purāṇas.
The armies from this place fought in the great war.
They fought taking their position at the grīvāsthāna
(neck position) of the Garuḍa vyūha (A particular
array of soldiers) set up by Droṇa. (Droṇa Parva,
Chapter 20, Verse 7).
