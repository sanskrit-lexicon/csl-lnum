VEDANĀ¦. A goddess who caused pain to living things.
Adharma married Hiṃsā. Two daughters named Nṛtā
and Nirṛti were born to them. From them Bhaya,
Naraka, Māyā and Vedanā were born. Mṛtyu was the
daughter of Māyā. Duḥkha was the son of Vedanā.
(Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 20).
