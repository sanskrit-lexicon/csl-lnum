SATYAKA¦. A king of the Yādava clan. He was the
father of Sātyaki. Satyaka also took part in the festivals
conducted on the Raivata-mountain by Śrī Kṛṣṇa and
the others. It is stated in the aśvamedha Parva, Chapter
62, Verse 6, that Satyaka conducted offerings to the
manes in respect of Abhimanyu.
