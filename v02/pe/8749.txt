YATINĀTHA¦. An incarnation of Siva. Āhuka, a forest-
dweller and his wife who lived in Arbuda (Abu) moun-
tain were blessed by Yatinātha and as a result of the
blessing they were born as Nala and Damayantī in their
next birth. (Śiva Purāṇa, Śatarudra Saṃhitā).
