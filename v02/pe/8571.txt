VĪTIHOTRA I¦. A King in ancient India. Vītihotra
was one of the ten sons born to Priyavrata by his wife
Barhiṣmatī, the daughter of Viśvakarmā. Agnīdhra,
Idhmajihva, Yajñabāhu, Mahāvīra, Hiraṇyaretas,
Ghṛtapṛṣṭha, Savana, Medhātithi and Kavi were the
brothers of Vītihotra. (Bhāgavata, Skandha 5).
