BRAHMAPURĀṆA¦, (BRĀHMAPURĀṆA). This is a
great book of twenty-five thousand verses taught to
the great hermit-sage Marīci by Brahmā. It is mention-
ed in Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 271, that if a copy of this
book is taken and offered to a Brahmin on the full
moon day of the month of Vaiśākha with cow and
water, the giver will attain heaven.
