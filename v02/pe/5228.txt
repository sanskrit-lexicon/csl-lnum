PRABHADRAKA¦. A special division of the army of
Pāñcālarāja. This division comprises of Kṣatriyas only.
Dhṛṣṭadyumna and Śikhaṇḍī were its leaders. During the
Kurukṣetra battle Śalya slew many of the Prabhadrakas.
The rest of the Prabhadrakas were killed by Āśvatthāmā
while they were sleeping. (Chapter 8, Sauptika Parva;
Chapter 57. Udyoga Parva; Chapter 19, Bhīṣma Parva,
Chapter 11, Śalya Parva).
