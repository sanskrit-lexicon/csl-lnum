SAIṂHIKEYA¦(S). The Asuras (Demons) who were
the sons of Siṃhikā. Two sons named Hiraṇyākṣa and
Hiraṇyakaśipu and a daughter named Siṃhikā were
born to Prajāpati Kaśyapa by his wife Diti. Siṃhikā
was given in marriage to Vipracitti. The sons of this
couple were known by the name Saiṃhikeyas. Rāhu
was the first of the Saiṃhikeyas. (Agni Purāṇa. Chap-
ter 19). For further details see under Rāhu.
