ŚIBI III¦. A King born in the dynasty of Uśīnara. He
was present at the wedding of Draupadī. He fought on
the side of the Pāṇḍavas in the great war and was
killed by Droṇa. (Droṇa Parva, Chapter 155, Verse 19).
King Śibi had four sons Bhadra, Suvīra, Kekaya and
Vṛṣadarbha. (Bhāgavata, 9th Skandha).
