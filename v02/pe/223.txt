AṂŚUMĀN¦. Name of a King belonging to the Solar
dynasty (Sūrya Vaṃśa).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Aṃśumān was descended from Viṣṇu
through Brahmā-Marīci-Kaśyapa-Vivasvān-Vaivasvata-
manu-Ikṣvāku-Vikukṣi-Śaśāda-Purañjaya-Kakutstha-
Anenas-Pṛthulāśva-Prasenajit-Yuvanāśva-Māndhātā-
Purukutsa-Trasadasyu-Anaraṇya-Aryaśvan-Vasuma-
nas-Sudhanvā-Traiyyāruṇa-Satyavrata-Triśaṅku-Hariś-
candra-Rohitāśva-Harita-Cuñcu-Sudeva-Bharuka-
Bāhuka-Sagara-Asamañjas-Aṃśumān.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Birth.%} Sagara, a king of the Sūrya vaṃśa, (mention-
ed above) had two wives, Sumati (Vaidarbhī) and
Keśinī (Saibā) by name. Keśinī gave birth to a son
named Asamañjas. Aṃśumān was the son of Asamañjas
and the father of the famous Bhagīratha. (See under
the title, BHAGĪRATHA).
<div n="NI"/>3) {%Some other details.%} Aṃśumān was one of the Kings
who attended the Svayaṃvara of Pāñcālī. (Vide
Śloka 11, Chapter 185 of Ādi Parva of the Mahā-
bhārata).
