SAṂYĀTI II¦. A king, who was the great-grandson of
emperor Pūru and son of Prācinvān. Aśmakī, born of
the Yadu dynasty was his mother. He married Varāṅgī
daughter of Dṛṣadvān and a son called Ahaṃyāti was
born to the couple. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 95, Verse 14).
