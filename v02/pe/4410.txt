MARU I¦. A king of the Ikṣvāku dynasty, the father of
Prasuśruta and son of Śīghra. He had become
“Cirañjīvī” (immortal) by his Yogic power. According
to Bhāgavata Purāṇa, all Kṣatriya families would
perish in Kaliyuga. At that time, Maru would come
back to the world to revive the Kṣatriya race.
(Bhāgavata, 9th Skandha).
