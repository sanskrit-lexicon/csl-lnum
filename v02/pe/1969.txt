DHĀTĀ II¦. It is seen in Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśa 1, Chapter
10, that two sons named Dhātā and Vidhātā and a
daughter Lakṣmī were born to Bhṛgu, the son of Brahmā,
by his wife Khyāti. Of them Dhātā and Vidhātā
married Āyati and Niyati, the daughters of Meru.
Lakṣmī became the wife of Mahāviṣṇu.
