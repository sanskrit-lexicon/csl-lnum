PRĀJĀPATYA¦. A kind of marriage.
<Poem>Sahobhau caratāṃ dharma-
miti vācānubhāṣya ca /
Kanyāpradānamabhyarcya
Prājāpatyo vidhiḥ smṛtaḥ //</Poem>
(Sloka 30, Chapter 3, Manusmṛti).
<div n="NI"/>Giving in marriage to a brahmacārī one's daughter
after inviting him to his house and worshipping him is
called Prājāpatya.
