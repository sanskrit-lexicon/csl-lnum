PUṢPAVATĪ¦. A sacred place. If one stays here for
three nights fasting and bathes in the holy pond there
one would get the benefit of making a thousand
godānas (cow-gifts) and his family will be absolved of
all sins. (Śloka 12, Chapter 85, Vana Parva).
