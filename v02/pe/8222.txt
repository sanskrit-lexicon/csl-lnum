VIBHAṆḌAKA¦ (VIBHĀṆḌAKA).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} A hermit, born in the family of
Kaśyapa. Once he happened to see Urvaśī. When he
pondered over her, seminal flow occurred to him. An
[Page846-a+ 65]
antelope swallowed it and gave birth to a son. That son
was known as the hermit Ṛṣyaśṛṅga, when he grew up.
(For details see under Ṛṣyaśṛṅga).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Vibhaṇḍaka stays in the palace of Indra and glori-
fies him. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 7, Stanza 18).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Vibhaṇḍaka is as radiant as Prajāpati. (M.B. Vana
Parva, Chapter 110, Stanza 32).
