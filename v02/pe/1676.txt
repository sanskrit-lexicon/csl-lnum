DAKṢIṆASINDHU¦. A holy place. This place is on the
shore of the southern sea. If one visits this place and
takes bath there he will get the fruits of performing the
sacrifice of Agniṣṭoma, and the opportunity of travelling
by the aeroplane of the gods. (M.B. Vana Parva,
Chapter 82, Stanza 53).
