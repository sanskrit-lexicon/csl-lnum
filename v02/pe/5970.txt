ŚAKTI I¦.
<div n="NI"/>(1) {%General.%} Son of Vasiṣṭha by Arundhatī. He was
the first-born among the hundred sons of Vasiṣṭha.
Kalmāṣapāda, the Rākṣasa captured and ate Śakti
and his pregnant wife Adṛśyantī spent her days in
great grief with Vasiṣṭha. Parāśaramuni, father of
Vyāsa, was Adṛśyantī's son. (For details see under
(Adṛśyantī).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other information%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) It was Śiva, who incarnated himself as the son of
Vasiṣṭha.
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Gopāyana, Bharadvāja, Āpastamba and Arṇodara
[Page668-b+ 64]
belong to the line of disciples of Śakti. (Vāmana
Purāṇa, Chapter 6).
