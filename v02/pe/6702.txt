ŚOṆITAPURA¦. The capital city of Bāṇāsura. This
city was protected by Śiva, Kārttikeya, Bhadrakālī,
Agni and other divinities. In the battle between Śrī
Kṛṣṇa and Bāṇa, the former defeated all the sentries
and entered the city through the northern gate. Within
the fort, Bāṇa was defeated. Mahābhārata, Sabhā
Parva, Dākṣinātya Pāṭha, Chapter 38 mentions that
Śrī Kṛṣṇa released Aniruddha and Uṣā from their
prison.
