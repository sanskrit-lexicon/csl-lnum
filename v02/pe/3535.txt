KĪKAṬA¦. A King born in the dynasty of Priyavrata,
son of Manu. He was one of the nineteen sons of King
Bharata, the other eighteen being Kuśavarman, llā-
varta, Brahmāvarta, Āryāvarta, Malaya, Bhadraketu,
Sena, Indraspṛk, Vidarbha, Kapi, Hari, Antarīkṣa,
Prabuddha, Pippalāyana, Āvirhotra, Dramiḍa, Camasa
and Karabhājana. (Bhāgavata, 5th Skandha).
