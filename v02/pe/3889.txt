KURUJIT¦. A king born in King Janaka's dynasty. Aja
was his father and Ariṣṭanemi his son. The genealogy
from Janaka to Kurujit is as follows: Janaka—Vīrada—
Nandivardhana—Suketu—Devarāta—Bṛhaddhṛta—
Mahāvīra—Dhṛtaketu—Haryaśva—Maru—Pratisvaka-
Kraturatha—Devamīḍha—Vidhṛta—Mahādhṛta—
Kṛtirāta—Mahāromā—Svarṇaromā—Prastharomā—
Sīradhvaja (Janaka, the father of Sītā)—Kurudhvaja
—Dharmadhvaja—Kṛtadhvaja—Keśiketu—Bhānumān
—Śakradyumna—Śuci—Vanadhvaja—Ūrjaketu—Aja
—Kurujit. (Bhāgavata, 9th Skandha).
