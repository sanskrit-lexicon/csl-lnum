REVATA¦. The father of Revatī, the wife of Balabhadra-
rāma. Revata was the son of Ānartta and the grandson
of king Śaryāti. It is mentioned in Devī Bhāgavata,
Skandha 7, that Revata was the first king who erected
his capital in the Island Kuśasthalī and began to rule
over it.
