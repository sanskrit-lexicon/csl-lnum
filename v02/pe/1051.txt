BHARADVĀJA IV¦. A renowned sage. Bharata, a King
of the Pūru line of kings, had no sons and as he was
spending his days in sorrow Marutta gave Bharata this
Bharadvāja as a son. Bharadvāja who was by birth a
brahmin from then onwards became a Kṣatriya.
(Matsya Purāṇa 49. 27-39 and Vāyu Purāṇa 99. 152-
158).
