KṚTAVĪRYA I¦. Father of the famous Kārtavīryārjuna
(For genealogy see under Kārtavīrya). He got a Soma
Yajña conducted by the sages of the family of Bhṛgu and
gave away a lot of wealth to people of Bhṛgu's family
(Ādi Parva, Chapter 177, Verse 18). Kṛtavīrya was a
member of the court of Yamarāja. (Sabhā Parva,
Chapter 8, Verse 9).
