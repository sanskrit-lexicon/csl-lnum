SANNATEYU¦. A son born to Raudrāśva, the third son
of emperor Puru, by Miśrakeśī, the apsarā woman. He,
a great archer, had nine sons called Ṛceyu, Vaneyu,
Jaleyu, Pakṣeyu, Kṛpaṇeyu, Sthaṇḍileyu, Tejeyu,
Satyeyu and Dharmeyu. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 94, Verse
8).
