KṚTI I¦. A Sage who belonged to the order of the disciple
of Vedavyāsa. Jaimini was the disciple of Vyāsa, and
Sumantu the son of Jaimini. Sumantu had a son called
Sutvā, and Sukarmā was the son of the latter. He
had two disciples called Hiraṇyanābha alias Kausalya
and Pauṣpiñji. Kṛti was a disciple of Hiraṇyanābha,
and he composed twentyfour Saṃhitās for the Sāma-
veda and taught them to his disciples. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa,
Part 3, Chapter 6).
