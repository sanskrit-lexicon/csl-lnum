AṂŚĀVATĀRA¦. The incarnation of God on earth is
called avatāra. When the incarnation is only partial,
that is, when only some of the divine elements incarnate,
it is called aṃśāvatāra (Aṃśa=part). Chapters 54 to
64 of Ādi Parva of the Mahābhārata give us a list of
the gods who have incarnated partially. The following
list of devas and their aṃśāvatāras is based upon the
account given in the fourth Skandha of Śrī Mahādevī
Bhāgavata.
