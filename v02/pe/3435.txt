KAUŚIKĀ¦. (GOMATĪ). A river. The hermitage of
Viśvāmitra stood on the bank of this river. The modern
name of river Kauśikā is Kosī. The river Kosī flows
through Bihar. Those who bathe in this river will
obtain remission of sins. See under Gomatī. (M.B. Vana
Parva, Chapter 84).
