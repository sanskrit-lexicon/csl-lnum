SUDHARMĀ II¦. The great assembly hall of the
Yādavas. It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva,
Chapter 219, Verse 10, that at the time of the carrying
away of Subhadrā, the soldiers ran to Sudharmā and
reported the matter there. This hall had the name
‘Dāśārhī’ also. This great hall was one yojana long
and one yojana broad. It was in this hall that Śrī
Kṛṣṇa received Indra. (M.B. Dākṣiṇātya Pāṭha, Sabhā
Parva, Chapter 38).
