{%Citrakarma%}¦ — a Gaṇeśa; a sculptor created a beautiful
form out of the ashes left by Madana's body burnt down by
Śiva. Thus having been restored to life he was asked to
meditate Śatarudriya in honour of Rudra. This was Bhaṇḍa
who was blessed by Śiva to rule for 6000 years.
