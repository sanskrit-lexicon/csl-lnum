{%Bhadrāśva(m)%} (II)¦ — a continent of Mandara hill east
of Meru; bounded on one side by Gandhamādana; traversed
by the stream Sītā; the capital of Bhadrāśvas, son of Dharma,
engaged in the worship of Hayagrīva with due praises;<sup>1</sup> coun-
try conquered by Parīkṣit;<sup>2</sup> people residing here are white
in colour and women excel in beauty living for thousands
(10,000-{%Vā. P.%}) of years; non-injury and truth prevalent
here; people here worship Śaṅkara and Gaurī;<sup>3</sup> Viṣṇu in the
form of Hayaśiras.<sup>4</sup>
