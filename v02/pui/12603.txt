{%Vāruṇakratu%}¦ — Brahmā took the Vāruṇi body and offer-
ed his śukra (semon) to Agni to beget sons; hence eight sons
who are ṛṣis; Bhṛgu, Angiras, Marīci, Pulastya, Pulaha,
Kratu, Atri, and Vasiṣṭha; all devas and yajñaṅgas in the
shape of human forms were present; also Vedas as well as
lakṣaṇa, svara, sthopa, nirukta, devakanyas, deva-patnis,
devamātaras, all in human form.
