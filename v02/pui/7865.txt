{%Piṅgalā%} (I)¦ — an aged prostitute of the Videha city; her
story as narrated by Avadhūta to Yadu is as follows; she
spent her life as a public woman living by earning money
every day from one paramour or other; one night she did
not get a paramour though she waited to the middle of the
night; she then grew disgusted with her life and turned her
thought on Hari which resulted in her restful sleep;<sup>1</sup> her
view of life cited by the Gopis to Uddhava.<sup>2</sup>
<F>1) Bhā. XI. 8. 22-44.</F>
<F>2) Ib. X. 47. 47.</F>
