{%Vajra%} (I)¦ — Indra's thunderbolt; a weapon shaped out
of Dadhīci's limbs by Viśvakarman for the use of Indra; with
this Indra was able to break the back of the mountains. By
this Vṛtra was slain. But it was of no use against Namuci;
the embodiment of Brahmanical energy;<sup>1</sup> from the {%tejas%} of
the sun;<sup>2</sup> a weapon of war;<sup>3</sup> in the battle with Kṛṣṇa.<sup>4</sup>
