{%Sāraṇa%} (I)¦ — a son of Vasudeva (Ānakadundubhi) and
Rohiṇī and father of Sārṣṭhi and others.<sup>1</sup> Followed Vṛṣṇis
to Bāṇa's city. Was consulted by Kṛṣṇa on the eve of attack
on Jarāsandha; being on the left detachment of Kṛṣṇa's
army, defended Dvārakā against Śālva.<sup>2</sup> Went to see the Pāṇ-
ḍavas at Upaplāvya, and to Syamantapañcaka for the solar
eclipse.<sup>3</sup>
