{%Trayambaka%} (I)¦ — Śiva;<sup>1</sup> his riding animal {%Vṛṣabha,%}<sup>2</sup>
followed by the Naiṛṛta clan of Rākṣasas;<sup>3</sup> one of the eleven
Rudras; the eighth of twelve incarnations who slew Asuras in
the battle of Andhaka;<sup>4</sup> sacrifice to, by Puroḍāśa made in
Kapālas; Gāyatrī, Triṣṭub and Jagati are present as three
means of the Puroḍāśa;<sup>5</sup> with ten arms in Śivapura; his
weapons are Paṭṭiśa, bow, trident, and sword; Gandharva
leaders entertain him with music; the bull is there, and
Skanda and others form the sabhā; sculptures of Dig Devas
in the columns of the palace.<sup>6</sup> Ambakas are the yonis of
Vanaspati, king of trees;<sup>7</sup> curse of Dakṣa to be born as
Prācetas in the Cākṣuṣa Manu epoch.<sup>8</sup>
