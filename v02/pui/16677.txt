and Chāyā; father of Manu, Śrāddhadeva and Yama and
Yami; see Vivasvan.<sup>1</sup> Presented Pṛthu with arrows from his
rays; worship of: in Plakṣadvīpa,<sup>2</sup> begot a son on Pṛthā, still

wife, and Tapati daughter;<sup>7</sup> is Vibhāvasu ten Kalas of;<sup>8</sup> gives
life to Agni.<sup>9</sup> (Āditya): came to Kārtavīrya Arjuna in Brah-
