{%Kuśa%} (VIII)¦ — {%(Kuśadvīpa, Kumudadvīpam?)%} — thrice
the Suroda in size surrounded by Ghṛtoḍa (sea of Ghee)
(milk ocean-{%M.P.%}). Its name comes from a shining divine
cluster of grass in it. Its king was a son of Priyavrata,
Hīraṇyaretas, who divided it among his seven sons. Here
Agni is worshipped.<sup>1</sup>

Jambūdvīpa;<sup>4</sup> of different villages and the residence of
Kumuda the wily sister of Mahādeva.<sup>5</sup>
