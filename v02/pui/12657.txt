{%Vāsuki%} (I)¦ — a son of Kadru: a chieftain of the Nāga-
loka. He was used as the rope in the {%amṛtamathana.%} Identi-
fied with Hari.<sup>1</sup> A friend of Indra: the milkman of the Nāgas
for milking the earth: moving with the sun for two months;<sup>2</sup>
in the Vaiḍūrya śālā of Lalitā;<sup>3</sup> his aid to Tripurāri;<sup>4</sup> an ear
ornament of Śiva; shaken by Hiraṇyakaśipu: sports in
Amarakaṇṭaka;<sup>5</sup> heard the {%Viṣṇu Purāṇa%} from Dhṛtarāṣṭra
the Nāga and narrated it to Vatsa.<sup>6</sup>
