{%Naikavaktra%}¦ — the hump-backed Kubja in the employ
of Kamsa for preparing perfumes. Kṛṣṇa got some for the
mere asking. Pleased, Kṛṣṇa cured her deformity and made

residence, which he promised to do sometime later.
