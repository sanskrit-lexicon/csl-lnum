torial division of Śālmalidvīpa:<sup>1</sup> The island where Kālīya
originally lived. Here the serpents agreed to give {%bali%} every
fortnight to Garuḍa, except Kālīya.<sup>2</sup> South of Śveta and

pleasant to look at, devoid of disease and difficulties.<sup>3</sup>
