{%Subhadrā%} (I)¦ — a daughter of Vasudeva and Devakī:
queen of Arjuna, mother of Abhimaṇyu, and grandmother
of Parīkṣit, younger sister of Kṛṣṇa of dark complexion;<sup>1</sup>
taken by Kṛṣṇa to Dvārakā;<sup>2</sup> heard from the wives of Kṛṣṇa
of their marriage and was in wonder;<sup>3</sup> grieved at
Kṛṣṇa's departure from Hastināpura: welcomed Vidura;<sup>4</sup> the
pride of the Vṛṣṇis.<sup>5</sup>
