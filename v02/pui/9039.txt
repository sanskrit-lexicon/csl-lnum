{%Bāhlīka%} (I)¦ — a son of Pratipa and father of Soma-
datta;<sup>1</sup> father of seven sons, kings of Bāhli Kingdom;<sup>2</sup> the
[Page2-482+ 37]
foremost among the Kurus;<sup>3</sup> was stationed by Jarāsandha
at the southern gate of Mathurā;<sup>4</sup> placed on the south dur-
ing the siege of Gomanta;<sup>5</sup> informed by Uddhava of Rāma's
visit to Hastināpura;<sup>6</sup> his sons took active part in
Yudhiṣṭhira's Rājasūya and in Duryodana's army;<sup>7</sup> could
not vanquish Arjuna, supported by Kṛṣṇa;<sup>8</sup> went with his
sons to Syamantapañcaka for the solar eclipse where he
met the Vṛṣṇis; left Syamantapañcaka;<sup>9</sup> sisters, Rohiṇī and
Pauravī, married to Vasudeva.<sup>10</sup>
