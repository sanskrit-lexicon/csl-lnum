{%Rāvaṇa%}¦ — a son of Viśravas and Keśinī;<sup>1</sup> pleased Śiva
by his praises;<sup>2</sup> afraid of Māndhāta;<sup>3</sup> heard of Śītā's beauty
and set up Mārīca to display himself as a golden deer and
entice Rāma away;<sup>4</sup> resented Arjuna-Haihaya's action in
ruining his camp by blocking a river and was overpowered
by him in the presence of women and imprisoned in his
capital Māhiṣmatī;<sup>5</sup> pursued by the king of kites, compared to
Indra being pursued by Vīra;<sup>6</sup> killed by Rāma;<sup>7</sup> lost his king-
dom through pride of power; desire for more territory.<sup>8</sup>
Vanquished by Vāli at Puṣkara, agreed to be his ally.<sup>9</sup>
A description of:<sup>10</sup> Killed Anaraṇya;<sup>11</sup> also Daśagrīva;
defeated by Kārtavīrya and released at the request of
Pulastya.<sup>12</sup>
