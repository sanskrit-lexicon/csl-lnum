{%Kaliṅga%} (III)¦ — a southern kingdom of madhyadeśa unfit
for {%śrāddha;%} a Janapada of the Dakṣiṇāpatha. Its king was
stationed by Jarāsandha on the east gate of Mathurā, and on
the same direction during the siege of Gomanta;<sup>1</sup> present at
[Page1-333+ 46]
Pradyumna's marriage. Advised Rukmin to vanquish
Balarāma in dice, and laughed at the latter when he was
defeated. His teeth were broken by Rāma.<sup>2</sup> Its 32 kings.<sup>3</sup>
On its south flows the Narmadā where the hill Amarakaṇ-
ṭaka is.<sup>4</sup> In the {%Kṛtayuga,%} the first man appeared in this
country<sup>5</sup> under Guhas.<sup>6</sup>
