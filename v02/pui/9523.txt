{%Bhaviṣya-Purāṇa (Bhaviṣyam)%}¦ — one among the {%Mahā-%}
{%purāṇas;%} comprises 14,500 ślokas; narrated by Brahmā to
Manu containing the māhātmyam of the sun, and the account
of Aghora kalpa with the characteristics of Bhūtagrāma; he
who makes a gift of it with a {%kumbha%} on the Full Moon
day of the Puṣya month gets the fruits of the Agniṣṭoma;
Lomaharṣaṇa revealed it to the sages.
