{%Yādavas%} (II)¦ — the family of Yadu;<sup>1</sup> unlimited in num-
bers; had 38 millions of teachers of army;<sup>2</sup> consisted of 101
families over whom Viṣṇu was ruler and teacher;<sup>3</sup> consisted of
Kukuras, Vṛṣṇis and Andhakas;<sup>4</sup> to deprive them of all their
possessions was the plan of Kamsa;<sup>5</sup> on Kṛṣṇa's advice and
in fulfilment of the curse of sages to die of the iron club
born to Sāmba, left Dvārakā for Prabhāsa; having drunk
they attacked one another and finally took hold of rushes
grown out of the iron pieces of musala and killed them-
selves.<sup>6</sup>
