<L>9820<pc>2-586<k1>Boja<k2>Boja<h>II
{%Bhojas%} (II)¦ — a Yādava tribe to which Kamsa belonged:
were related to the Pāṇḍavas;<sup>1</sup> defended Dvārakā against
the enemies and praised the heroic deeds of Kṛṣṇa;<sup>2</sup> Kamsa
planned in vain to vanquish them. Fought with their kith
and kin and ended their lives;<sup>3</sup> line of the, traced from Mahā-
bhoja; kings of Mṛttikāvara Pura; hence Mārttikāvaras:
killed Sātvata Śatrughna and left Dvārakā with Akrūra.<sup>4</sup>
