<L>4011<pc>1-508<k1>gada<k2>gada<h>I
{%Gada%} (I)¦ — a son of Kṛṣṇa<sup>1</sup> who was stationed at the
western gate of Mathurā for defence.<sup>2</sup> Had a place on the
left detachment of Kṛṣṇa's army. When Jarāsandha
attacked Mathurā for a third time, Gada played a heroic
part.<sup>3</sup> Attacked Caidya party which pursued Kṛṣṇa taking
away Rukmiṇī.<sup>4</sup> Went with Vṛṣṇis to the city of Bāṇa.<sup>5</sup>
Accompanied Sāmba and others to play and came across a
huge lizard in a well and reported it to Kṛṣṇa.<sup>6</sup> Took part
in defending Dvārakā against Śālva and in expelling his
forces.<sup>7</sup> Went to Syamantapañcaka for solar eclipse.<sup>8</sup>
At Prabhāsa.<sup>9</sup>
