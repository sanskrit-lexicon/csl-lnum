{%Tāmasa%} (I)¦ — a son of Priyavrata, and a Manvantara
adhipati.<sup>1</sup> The fourth Manu and brother of Uttama. He
had Pṛthu and nine other sons. During this epoch the
gods went by the name of Satyakas, Haris, etc. Triśikha
was Indra. Jyotirdhāmā and others were the seven sages.<sup>2</sup>
Brother of Raivata.<sup>3</sup> For different names of Gods, sages and
Indra in this epoch;<sup>4</sup> represents ukāra;<sup>5</sup> twenty-seven gaṇas
of Gods mentioned in this epoch — Supāra, Haraya, etc. Śibi
was Indra; Nara and Khyāti, his sons.<sup>6</sup>
