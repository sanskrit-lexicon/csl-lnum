{%Mitravindā%} (II)¦ — daughter of Kṛṣṇa's aunt in Avantī.
Discomfiting Vinda and Anuvinda, Kṛṣṇa married her; her
sons;<sup>1</sup> welcomed by Draupadī to Hāstinapura, she explained
how she was married to Kṛṣṇa.<sup>2</sup>
