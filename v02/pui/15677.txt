{%Sahadeva%} (V)¦ — a son of Mādrī (Mādravatī-{%Br. P.%}), born
through the grace of the Aśvins. Father of Śrutakarman.
His other son was Suhotra by Vijayā;<sup>1</sup> joy at Kṛṣṇa's visit
to Indraprastha; paid his respects to him; was consoled by
[Page3-571+ 24]
Kṛṣṇa when banished to the forest;<sup>2</sup> was sent to southern
countries with Sṛñjayas.<sup>3</sup> His suggestion that Kṛṣṇa should
be accorded the first honour among the Sadasyas in the
Rājasūya of Yudhiṣṭhira was accepted. Entertained guests
in that sacrifice. Approved of Draupadī's desire to release
Aśvatthāman.<sup>4</sup>
