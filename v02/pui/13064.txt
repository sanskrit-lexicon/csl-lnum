{%Virāj (Virāṭ-Puruṣa)%}¦ — the form of the Lord during the
period of creation, just emerged from Aṇḍa.<sup>1</sup> To rouse him
to activity gods were created; but he was not roused until
the Kṣetrajña along with citta entered the heart and roused
the Puruṣa from waters;<sup>2</sup> is Hari who is described as Mahā-
Puruṣa;<sup>3</sup> son of Nara;<sup>4</sup> Antarikṣa so called.<sup>5</sup>

X. 3. 15; XI. 3. 11-12; 17. 13.</F>
<F>3) Ib. XII. 11. 5-26.</F>
<F>4) Br. II. 14. 68.</F>
