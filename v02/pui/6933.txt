{%Narakāsura%}¦ — (Naraka, s.v. Bhauma); Born of Hari in
his Boar manifestation when he touched and raised the
Earth,<sup>1</sup> hence son of Earth,<sup>2</sup> an asura friend of Kamsa;<sup>3</sup> a
friend of the Vānara Dvivida;<sup>4</sup> ran away with the umbrella
of Varuṇa, Kuṇḍala of Aditi and contested the position of
Indra.<sup>5</sup> Fought with Sanaiścara in the Devāsura war;<sup>6</sup> find-
[Page2-208+ 33]
ing Mura and his sons dead, came out with elephants, which
were all killed by Garuḍa; fought with Kṛṣṇa till his head
was cut off and the ladies of his harem married by Him
(Kṛṣṇa);<sup>7</sup> lost his kingdom through pride of power.<sup>8</sup>
