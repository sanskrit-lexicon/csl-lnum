{%Kālanemī%}¦ — a son of Virocana and father of four sons.
Kaṃsā's previous birth as Kālanemī sent his {%śūla%} towards
Hari who vanquished him with the same implement;<sup>2</sup> resid-
ing in Atalam (IV tala or gabhastalam).<sup>3</sup> Killed by Hari in
Tārakāmaya, where he was a commander; rode on a chariot
of elephants, used illusory powers and {%brahmāstram%} in the
war; devas as prisoners of war but released by Tāraka;
attacked Hari and fell dead slain by his cakra.<sup>4</sup>
