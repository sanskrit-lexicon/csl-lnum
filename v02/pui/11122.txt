{%Yakṣas%}¦ — a semi-celestial group, usually of the class of
demons; born of Viśvā and Kaśyapa;<sup>1</sup> followers of Rudra
(Śiva), their overlord; their lord, Kubera;<sup>2</sup> milked the cow-
earth, with Vaiśravaṇa as the calf and {%āma%} as the vessel;<sup>3</sup>
worship the Barhiṣad manes;<sup>4</sup> helped Vṛtra against Indra
and went with Satī to Dakṣa's sacrifice;<sup>5</sup> sport of;<sup>6</sup> got {%mokṣa%}
meditating on Hari;<sup>7</sup> usurped the Ānarta kingdom of
Kakudmi when he was absent at Brahmā's court;<sup>8</sup> came
with the gods to see Kṛṣṇa and saw Kṛṣṇa retiring to his
own region;<sup>9</sup> (see Puṇyajanas); vanquished by Rāvaṇa;<sup>10</sup>
worship the Pitṛs and ruin the śrāddha;<sup>11</sup> their kingdom;<sup>12</sup>
their loka;<sup>13</sup> the nails of Vāmana when he grew.<sup>14</sup>
