{%Girivraja%}¦ — the capital of Jarāsandha, entered by
Kṛṣṇa, Arjuna and Bhīma disguised as Brahmaṇas.<sup>1</sup> Here
Gautama retired and Kakṣivat attained Brahmanhood.
Here too the Bṛhadrathas ruled.<sup>2</sup> Capital of Somādhi, son
of Sahadeva killed in Bhārata war; capital of Śiśunāka after
Nandivardhana while his son ruled from Benares.<sup>3</sup> Capital
of the Māgadheyas.
<F>1) Bhā. X. 70. 24; 72. 16.</F>
<F>2) Br. III. 74. 95, 110 and 128.</F>
<F>3) M. 271. 19; 272. 6.</F>
<F>4) Vā. 99. 296, 315.</F>
