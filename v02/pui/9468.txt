{%Bharata%} (II)¦ — a son of Daśaratha;<sup>1</sup> had two sons Takṣa
and Puṣkala who had Gāndhāra for their kingdom;<sup>2</sup> wore
bark of wood and ate food dressed with gomūtra, took bed
on the bare earth during Rāma's exile, when he was at Nandi-
grāma meditating on the {%Pādukā%} of Rāma he took his place
on the śilā; his {%āśrama,%} where holy men congregated; return-
ed to Ayodhyā with all royal paraphernalia on hearing of
Rāma's arrival; embraced by Rāma;<sup>3</sup> was present at Rāma's
coronation;<sup>4</sup> killed a number of Gandharvas.<sup>5</sup>
