{%Viśvāmitra%} (I)¦ — A sage of the Vaivasvata epoch;<sup>1</sup> was
invited for the Rājasūya of Yudhiṣṭhira;<sup>2</sup> came to see Kṛṣṇa
at Syamantapañcaka<sup>3</sup> and Parikṣit practising {%prāyopaveśa.%}<sup>4</sup>
Acted as Purohita at Kṛṣṇa's sacrifice in Kuruk-
ṣetra, left Dvārakā for Piṇḍāraka;<sup>5</sup> Purohita of Jamadagni;
the 6th incarnation of Viṣṇu to do away with the Kṣatriyas;
became a Brāhmaṇa by tapas.<sup>6</sup>
