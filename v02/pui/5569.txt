{%Taittirīya%}¦ — Yajus-śākha; when Yājñavalkya vomitted
the Yajus due to a misunderstanding with his Guru, the other
sages who were pupils of Vaiśampāyana became transformed
into Tittirā birds and received those portions of the Yajur-
veda. Hence the name Taittirīya.
<div n="P"/>Bhā. XII. 6. 64-5; Br. II. 35. 75; Vi. III. 5. 13; Vā. 61. 66.
