{%Ketumāla%} (II)¦ — a continent bounded on one side by
Mālyavat, and traversed by the stream Cakṣus. Viṣṇu
reveals himself here as Kāmadeva when Lakṣmī praises his
glory.<sup>1</sup> Conquered by Parīkṣit.<sup>2</sup> Adjacent to Meru and
noted for {%panasa%} tree;<sup>3</sup> of Mlecchas;<sup>4</sup> māla, thrown by Indra
during the churning of the ocean, struck on a tree there
and hence the name; description of;<sup>5</sup> Viṣṇu in the form of
Varāha.<sup>6</sup>
