{%Nāgas%} (IV)¦ — elephants; created for use in the
Devāsura wars; other names are Dvirada, Hasti, Kari,
Vāraṇa, Danti, Gaja, Kuñjara, Mātanga, Dvīpa, Sāmaja:
turning of the tongue due to the curse of Agni and two
tusks and enormous strength by curse of the Gods; see also
dignāgas.
