{%Svarbhānu%} (V)¦ — Rāhu, the planet;<sup>1</sup> has a dark chariot
drawn by eight steeds;<sup>2</sup> in the army at Tārakāmaya;<sup>3</sup> mahā-
graha left unskilled with Virocana;<sup>4</sup> comes out of the sun
and enters the moon in Parvas; then from the moon to the
sun; comes out from behind and crossing the shadow of the
earth forms a circle itself; its place is Bṛhadsthānam full of
darkness;<sup>5</sup> has only one ray.<sup>6</sup>
