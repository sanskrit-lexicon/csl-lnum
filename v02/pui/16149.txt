{%Sudharmā%}¦ — the heavenly sabhā sent by Indra and
brought to Dvārakā by Kṛṣṇa, taken by Vāyu under orders
of Kṛṣṇa; in this Kṛṣṇa seated himself.<sup>1</sup> It formed the council
hall of Dvārakā. Here Kṛṣṇa met the elders. Entered by
Kṛṣṇa after the Kurukṣetra war;<sup>2</sup> went back to heaven after
Kṛṣṇa's departure to it.<sup>3</sup>
