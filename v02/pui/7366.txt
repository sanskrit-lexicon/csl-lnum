{%Naimiṣā%}¦ — (also Naimiṣālaya s.v.) sacred to Viṣṇu; here
Śaunaka and other sages performed a {%satra%} extending over a
thousand years;<sup>1</sup> visited by Balarāma who was honoured by
all except Śūta Romaharṣaṇa; Balarāma killed the Śūta in
anger thus committing brahmicide; then at the instance of
the sages and as an expiation for the offence he killed Asura
Balvala here;<sup>2</sup> revisited by Balarāma who took part in the
sacrifices;<sup>3</sup> sages of, visited Dvārakā.<sup>4</sup>
