{%Druhyu%}¦ — a son of Yayāti and Sarmiṣṭhā and father of
Babhru and Satu;<sup>1</sup> after being refused by Yadu and Turvasu
approached by Yayāti; he also declined to part with his
youth to his father and was therefore cursed to have no
pleasures in life and to be wandering about the countries
and oceans with no settled kingdom;<sup>2</sup> became over-lord of
the south-eastern (west {%Br. P., Vā. P.%} and {%Vi. P.%}) part of the
kingdom;<sup>3</sup> from him begins the Bhoja line.<sup>4</sup>
