{%Mūlaka%}¦ — a son of Aśmaka; when the Kṣatriyas were
rooted out of the earth, he was protected by naked women;
hence he was known as Nārikavaca. The originator of the
new Kṣatriya race after its ruin by Paraśurāma; father of
Daśaratha.
