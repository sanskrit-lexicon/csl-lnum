{%Sahadeva%} (II)¦ — a son of Jarāsandha of the Māgadha
line; father of Somāpi(a) and Mārjāspi;<sup>1</sup> enthroned by
Kṛṣṇa; commanded by Kṛṣṇa, he arranged for the bath, dress
and meals of the released kings; honoured Kṛṣṇa when he
left the capital.<sup>2</sup> Killed in Bhārata battle; his son Somāpi
(Somādhi-{%M. P.%}) ruled for 58 years, at Girivraja. The latter's
son was Śrutaśravas.<sup>3</sup>
