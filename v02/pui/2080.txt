{%Ṛcīka%} (I)¦ — the son of Nāhuṣi (Aurva) and father of
Jamadagni by Satyavatī; the sage who married Gādhi's
daughter by paying a price of a thousand white horses
with one black ear. (See {%Satyavatī%}). A {%mantrakṛt%}<sup>1</sup> com-
pared to Dhiṣṇi fire. Blessed his wife with a {%caru%}
and his mother-in-law with another for the birth res-
pectively of a Brāhmaṇa and a Kṣatriya son. His wife
wrongly took that intended for her mother. She gave birth
to Jamadagni and became converted as Kauśikī river;<sup>2</sup> father
of two other sons Śunahśepa and Śunahpuccha;<sup>3</sup> had
100 sons who in turn had 1000 sons — all Bhārgavas.<sup>4</sup>
