{%Sītā%} (I)¦ — a branch of the Gangā issuing from the city
of Brahmā: descends Kesara and other hills, falls on the
summits of Gandhamādana, and traversing the continent of
Bhadrāśva, falls into the Eastern Ocean;<sup>1</sup> rises out of the
Sitānta slopes of the Mukuñja hill, flows through Sumañjasa,
Mālyavata, Vaikanka, Maṇiparvatam, Ṛṣabham, Jaṭhara,
Devakūṭa, and reaches through the Gandhamādana and takes
the name of Alakanandā and enters the Mānasa lake.
<F>1) Bhā V. 17. 5-6; Br. II. 12. 16; 18. 22, 41-5; 26. 44; III. 56. 52;
Vā. 42. 17-35; 47. 39, 43; 55. 42; Vi. II. 2. 34-5; 8. 113.</F>
