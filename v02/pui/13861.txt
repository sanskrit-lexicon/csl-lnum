{%Vraja%} (II)¦ — (Gokulam) the residence of cowherds.<sup>1</sup>
Here lived Rohiṇī, Nanda and others. Keśin was killed in
this place. Visit of Akrūra to. Nanda's return to Vraja
after Kamsa's death. Visited by Uddhava at Kṛṣṇa's
request. Visited by Balarāma.<sup>2</sup> Deserted by the cowherds
after their supposed ill omens, the boy sports of Kṛṣṇa.<sup>3</sup>
