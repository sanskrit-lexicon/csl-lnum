{%Krodhavaśā%}¦ — a daughter of Dakṣa and one of Kaśya-
pa's wives, and mother of Dandaśūka and other serpents.<sup>1</sup>
Her twelve daughters were married to Pulaha. Bhūtas,
Piśācas, Kinnaras, Vānaras and others belonged to her line.<sup>2</sup>
Some of her sons fell a prey to Bhīmasena's sword.<sup>3</sup>
<F>1) Bhā. VI. 6. 26 and 28; Vā. 66. 54.</F>
<F>2) Br. III. 3. 56; 7. 171,
444 and 467; 8. 72; Vi. I. 15. 125.</F>
<F>3) M. 6. 2 and 43; 146. 18.</F>
