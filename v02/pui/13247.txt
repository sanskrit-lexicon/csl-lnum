{%Viśvasphāni%}¦ — a Māgadha king equal to Hari in battle:
cut at the root of the Kṣatriya order and established his own
castes: viz. Kaivartas, Madrakas, Pulindas, Brāhmaṇas, and
Pañcakas; said to be an eunuch; offerings of, to devas, pitṛs,
and Brahmans; died on the banks of the Ganges as an ascetic
and went to Indraloka.
