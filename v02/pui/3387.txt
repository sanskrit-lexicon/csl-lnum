{%Kūpakarṇa%}¦ — a minister of Bāṇa. Went with Jarāsan-
dha to assault Mathurā. Fought with māyā as weapon
which Kṛṣṇa removed by use of Vijñānāstra. Defeated by
Kṛtavarman. Seeing Kumbhāṇḍa unconscious, fled towards
the city. Was attacked by Balarāma in besieging Śonita-
pura, and was defeated.
