{%Nagnādaya%}¦ — devoid of the three outer coverings of the
body — unfit for śrāddha occasions; Pāṣaṇḍas also termed
Nagnas as also others who pretend to be {%tapasvins%} and
ascetics, proceed on wrong lines, murder Brahmanas, prove
ungrateful friends, defile the teacher's bed and also robbers;
they are to be abandoned.
