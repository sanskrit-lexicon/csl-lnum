{%Niṣāda%} (III)¦ — 20th kalpa; Prajāpati Niṣāda performed
austerities controlling the senses for a thousand divine years;
seeing the son getting wearied by penance Brahmā said:
“refrain from it,” hence the name, Niṣāda. Out of him
came {%Niṣādavānsvara.%}
