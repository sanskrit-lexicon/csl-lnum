{%Pratardana%} (II)¦ — a son of Divodāsa; father of Vatsa
and Garga;<sup>1</sup> earned the title, Śatrujit by destroying the line
of Bhadraśreṇīya; known as Vatsa, so called by his father
out of love, and as Ṛtadhvaja by being truthful. For posses-
sing a horse by name Kuvalāyaśva, he came to be known
by that name. Alarka was the son of Vatsa.<sup>2</sup>
