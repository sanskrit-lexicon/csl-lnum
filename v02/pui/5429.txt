{%Tārakāmaya%} (I)¦ — A Devāsura battle in Kṛta yuga;
Tārā's (Tārakā, Bṛhaspati's wife) abduction by Soma,
was the ground for war between the Asuras and the Suras.
Śukra joined Soma while Śiva and Indra took up Bṛhas-
pati's cause.<sup>1</sup> Here Viṣṇu killed Kālanemi, and Indra, Viro-
cana, son of Prahlāda; (Virocana was killed by Viṣṇu in the
[Page2-020+ 29]
guise of Indra-{%Vā. P.%}). After the war there was a famine
when Vasiṣṭha helped the people with fruits and roots;<sup>2</sup> the
fifth battle in which Virocana was blessed with eternal life by
Śiva;<sup>3</sup> ended with the intervention of Brahmā who gave Tārā
back to Bṛhaspati.<sup>4</sup>

178.</F>
<F>3) Vā. 97. 21, 74 and 80.</F>
<F>4) Vi. IV. 6. 16-19.</F>
