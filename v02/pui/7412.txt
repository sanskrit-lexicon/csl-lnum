{%Pañcajana%} (I)¦ — an asura in the form of a conch in the
Prabhāsa. Son of Samhrāda and Kṛtī, and father of Asikni.
He seized the son of Sāndipana (or Sāndīpanī) and devour-
ed him. Kṛṣṇa plunged into the sea and killing him, remov-
ed the conch which covered his body.<sup>1</sup> Killed in Prāg-
jyotiṣa.<sup>2</sup>
<F>1) Bhā. III. 3. 2; VI. 4. 51; 18. 14; X. 45. 40-42. Vi. V. 21. 27-8.</F>
