{%Maitreya%} (I)¦ — (also Kauṣārava); a son of Mitrā; a
siddha; himself a Purāṇa of information;<sup>1</sup> went with Kṛṣṇa
to Mithilā; met Kṛṣṇa on the eve of his departure to Heaven,
and was ordered to be the preceptor to Vidura; the latter
met him on the Ganges and after describing the creation of
the world, answered Vidura's questions.<sup>2</sup> Taught him {%Ātma%}
[Page2-740+ 28]
{%vidyā%} and told him that the goal was Hari;<sup>3</sup> was invited for
the Rājasūya of Yudhiṣṭhira.<sup>4</sup>
