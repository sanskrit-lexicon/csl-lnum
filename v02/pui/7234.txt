{%Nilāyanakrīḍā%}¦ — a game of the Gopas in which the Gopas
put on the guise of thieves, watchmen, and goats; Vyoma
an Asura entered it in disguise and carried away almost all
the Gopas to a cave near by; seeing only few left, Kṛṣṇa
discovered the Asura and killed him at once.
