{%Yadus%}¦ — (also Yadu kula and Yadu vamśa); members
and descendants innumerable; one hundred and one families
recognised.<sup>1</sup> Their king was Ugrasena. Ill-treated by
Kamsa, they migrated to different countries like Kuru and
Pāñcāla, and became delighted at Kamsa's death. Their
Purohita was Garga.<sup>2</sup> Krsna born among them;<sup>3</sup> cursed by
[Page3-011+ 31]
Yayāti, his successors could not be kings; unacceptable to
good men according to Śiśupāla;<sup>4</sup> took part in the marriage
festivities of Kṛṣṇa and Rukminī, and attended the Rājasūya
of Yudhiṣṭhira; turned into a caste by Purañjaya;<sup>5</sup> destroyed
by Kṛṣṇa before he left the earth, under the pretext of the
Brahmanas' curse; the rest lost their reason after Kṛṣṇa's
departure to Heaven, drank wine and killed one another.
Four or five alone left alive. Attained {%yoga%} through the
grace of Datta; became defunct after the Mahābhārata war.<sup>6</sup>
