{%Rājasūya%} (I)¦ — the conqueror of cardinal points was
fit to perform this: Yudhiṣṭhīra on this, to Kṛṣṇa: Done by
Soma who conquered the three worlds;<sup>1</sup> Yudhiṣṭhira's desire
to be a Pārameṣṭhī. So Ṛtviks were sent for, including
Bhīṣma, Vidura. Even Śūdras were invited. After the
sacrifice, on the suggestion of Sahadeva, the first honour was
given to Kṛṣṇa. Śiśupāla's protest and vilification of Kṛṣṇa
who had his head cut off. In the sacrifice each brother was
assigned specific functions — Duryodhana in charge of
treasury, Bhīma cooking, Nakula the supply of provisions,
etc. The {%avabhṛta%} bath at Gangā accompanied by divine
music. Every visitor duly honoured, returned back.
Jealousy of Duryodhana at the success of the sacrifice.<sup>2</sup> The
consecration ceremony of a king done by Pṛthu, Vāli and
others.<sup>3</sup>
