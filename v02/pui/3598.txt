{%Keśin%} (II)¦ — an asura friend of Kamsā. Set up by him,
Keśin appeared in Vraja as a huge horse and attacked Kṛṣṇa
with his feet. Being thrown off by Kṛṣṇa, he fell at a dis-
tance. Recovering his consciousness, he once again attack-
ed Kṛṣṇa when the latter thrust his arm into his mouth until
he was suffocated to death.
