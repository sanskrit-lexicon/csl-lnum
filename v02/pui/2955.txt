{%Kālindī%} (I)¦ — the daughter of the sun god; wandering
in the forest in quest of Kṛṣṇa, she expressed herself to
Arjuna who was there with Kṛṣṇa; the latter married her;
welcomed by Draupadī to Hāstinapura, she narrated to her
how she married Kṛṣṇa.<sup>1</sup> Devī and wife of Kṛṣṇa; mother
of Śruta and other sons.<sup>2</sup>
