{%Tārkṣya%} (I)¦ — (also Tārkṣa) — see Garuḍa.<sup>1</sup> Married
four daughters of Dakṣa. These were Vinatā, Kadrū,
Patangī, and Yāminī, who were mothers of birds, snakes, etc.<sup>2</sup>
Tārkṣyasuta inimical to serpents.<sup>3</sup> Invoked for protection
on the ground.<sup>4</sup>
