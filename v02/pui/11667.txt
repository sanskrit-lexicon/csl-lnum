{%Rukmapārijātavana%}¦ — in Gayā where Śankara sported
with Pārvatī and to which Marīci went to gather fruits and
flowers; interrupted in his enjoyment Śiva cursed him, but
the latter praised him in prayer; Śiva promised him release
from the curse if he would visit Gayā; the blackman became
changed into white colour.
<div n="P"/>Vā. 112. 35-39.
