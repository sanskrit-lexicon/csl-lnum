ful), a measure of grain, the 4th of a droṇa and equal

4096 māṣas; or containing nearly 7 lbs. 11 ozs. avoir-
dupois. In Bengal an {%āḍhaka%} is equal to two mans or

N. of a country. {%—Āḍhakajambuka, as, ī, am,%}
