{#vESvadeva#}¦ {%vaiśvadeva, as, ī, am%} (fr. {%viśva-%}
{%deva%}), belonging or dedicated to the Viśva-devas or
Viśve-devāḥ, ({%vaiśvadevo lokaḥ,%} the world of the
Viśva-devas, Manu IV. 183); offered to the Viśva-
devas; relating to all the divinities; ({%am%}), n. an
offering or sacrifice to the Viśva-devas; an offering
to all deities (said to be performed by casting a little
food into fire before a meal); ({%ī%}), f. the eighth
day of the second half of the month Māgha; a
species of the Paṅkti metre. {%—Vaiśvadeva-karman,%}
{%a,%} n. an oblation to the Viśva-devas; an offering to
the deities collectively. {%—Vaiśvadeva-homa, am,%}
n. oblation to the Viśva-devas.
