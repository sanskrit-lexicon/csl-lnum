{#alam#}¦ {%alam,%} ind. (said to be fr. rt. {%al%}),
enough, sufficient, adequate, equal to, competent, able.
({%Alam%} may govern a dat., e. g. {%alaṃ jīvanāya,%}
sufficient for living; also a loc. or inf., e. g. {%alaṃ%}
{%vijñāne%} or {%vijñātum,%} able to conceive; also inst.,
e. g. {%alaṃ śaṅkayā,%} enough, i. e. away with fear!
It may be used with the future tense, e. g. {%alaṃ%}
{%haniṣyati,%} he will be able to kill; or with an indecl.
part., e. g. {%alam bhuktvā,%} enough of eating, i. e. do
not eat more; {%alaṃ vicārya,%} enough of consider-
ation.)
