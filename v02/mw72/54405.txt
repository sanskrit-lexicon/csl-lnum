{#hEhaya#}¦ {%haihaya, as,%} m. (thought by some
to be connected with {%haya,%} a horse), N. of a tribe
of people (said to have been descendants of Yadu;
they are described in the Purāṇas as separated into
five divisions, viz. the Tāla-jaṅghas, Vīti-hotras,
Āvantyas, Tuṇḍikeras, and Jātas; they are said to
have overrun parts of India along with the Śakas or
Scythian tribes); N. of the great grandson of Yadu;
of Arjuna Kārtavīrya (as ninth in descent from
Haihaya or as king of the Haihayas; he is said to
have had a thousand arms; see {%kārtavīrya%}).
