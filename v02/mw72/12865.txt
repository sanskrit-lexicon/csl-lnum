{#Etareya#}¦ {%aitareya, as,%} m. a descendant of Itarā,
to whom the Aitareya-Brāhmaṇa and Āraṇyaka were
revealed; ({%as, ī, am%}), originating from Aitareya.
{%—Aitareya-brāhmaṇa%} or {%aitareyaka, am,%} n. (scil.
{%brāhmaṇa%}) title of a Brāhmaṇa attached to the
Ṛg-veda and prescribing the duties of the Hotṛ
priest. It consists of forty Adhyāyas, divided into
eight Pañcikās. {%—Aitareyopaniṣad (°ya-up°), t,%}
f., N. of an Upaniṣad forming part of the Aitareya-
Āraṇyaka. {%—Aitareyopaniṣad-bhāṣya, am,%} n. a
commentary on the last by Saṅkarācārya. {%—Aitare-%}
{%yopaniṣadbhāṣya-ṭippaṇī,%} f., N. of a commen-
tary on the last work by Anantānandagiri.
