{#guggula#}¦ {%guggula, as,%} m. or {%guggulu, u, us,%}
n. m. (but always m. in the later language), a fragrant
gum resin, bdellium or the exudation of the Amyris
Agallochum, a perfume and medicament; sometimes
with the epithet {%saindhava%} or {%samudriya,%} i. e.
‘obtained near rivers or the sea,’ (perhaps some
other substance coming from rivers or the sea?);
a species of Morunga with red flowers, Morunga
Hyperanthera; ({%ū, ūs%}), f., N. of an Apsaras.
