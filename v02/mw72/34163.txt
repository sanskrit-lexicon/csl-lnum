{%tum,%} to float, swim; to go in a boat, cross in a

vibrate, hover, soar, fly; to fly away, haste away;

leap, jump, spring, (in these senses originally {%pru%});
to hop, skip, dance; to spring over, jump over,
leap across, spring upon, spring down; to be length-
ened or prolated (as a vowel): Caus. P. {%plāvayati,%}
{%yitum,%} Aor. {%apuplavat, apiplavat,%} to cause to
float or swim, flood, deluge, inundate, submerge; to

pour water upon, sprinkle, bathe, wash; to wash
away, remove; to cause to leap; to cause to stagger;
to lengthen, prolate (a vowel): Pass. of Caus. {%plāv-%}
{%yate,%} to be deluged, to be overflowed, to be inun-
dated: Desid. of Caus. {%piplāvayiṣati, puplāvayi-%}
{%ṣati:%} Desid. {%puplūṣate:%} Intens. {%poplūyate,%}
{%poploti,%} to float about, swim about; to swim rapidly;
