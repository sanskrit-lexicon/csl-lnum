<div n="P">Balarāma went to the {%tīrtha%} of the Gan-
dharvas ({%gandharvāṇāṃ … tīrtham%}) from
Subhūmikā 9. 36. 10, (8); there the Gan-
dharvas, Viśvāvasu and others, practised aus-
terites and performed delightful dances, played
on musical instruments and sang ({%nṛttavādi-%}
{%tragītaṃ ca kurvanti sumanoramam%}) 9. 36.
11; Haladhara (Balarāma) gave there wealth
of different kinds as also goats and sheep,
cows, donkeys, and camels, gold and silver
to the Brāhmaṇas; he also fed them and
satisfied them by giving large amounts of
wealth as desired; he went from the Gan-
dharvatīrtha ({%tasmād gandharvatīrthāc ca%}),
accompanied and praised by the Brāhmaṇas,
to the Gargasrotas 9. 36. 12-14.
