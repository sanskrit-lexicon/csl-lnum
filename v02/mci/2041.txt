{@B.@} Epic events: (1) When Dhṛtarāṣṭra
decided to leave Hāstinapura and live in
an {%āśrama,%} he went to Kurukṣetra; there
he reached the hermitage of Śatayūpa,
the great king of the Kekayas ({%tatrāśrama-%}
{%padaṃ … abhigamya … āsasāda … śatayū-%}
{%pam%}); with him Dhṛtarāṣṭra went to Vyāsā-
śrama 15. 25. 9-11; after he was initiated
by Vyāsa ({%sa dīkṣāṃ tatra saṃprāpya%}),
Dhṛtarāṣṭra returned to the {%āśrama%} of
Śatayūpa and lived there; as suggested by
Vyāsa, Śatayūpa gave complete instructions
to Dhṛtarāṣṭra regarding the mode of life
of a forest-dweller ({%śatayūpāśrame tasmin%}
