<L>599<pc>149-a<k1>SibikA<k2>SibikA
{@Śibikā@}¦ f.: Name of a weapon ({%śastra%})
of Kubera.
<div n="P">When Indra started for the fight with
Kṛṣṇa and Arjuna at the time of the burning
of the Khāṇḍava forest, Dhaneśvara (Kubera)
picked up his Śibikā to help him 1. 218. 31.
(According to a view cited by Nīlakaṇṭha,
{%śibikā%} means {%gadā.%} Nīlakaṇṭha's reading is
{%gadāṃ caiva,%} but he adds {%śibikām iti pāṭhe%}
{%śibikā gadeti prāñcaḥ%} on Bom. Ed. 2. 227.
32). [See {@Antardhāna<sup>2</sup>; Kaubera astra@} in
the Addenda].
