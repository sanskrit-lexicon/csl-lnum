{@Vaidad-aśvi,@}¦ ‘descendant of Vidadaśva,’ is the patronymic of
Taranta in the Rigveda.<sup>1</sup> In the Pañcaviṃśa Brāhmaṇa<sup>2</sup> and
the Jaiminīya Brāhmaṇa<sup>3</sup> the Vaidadaśvis are Taranta and
{@Purumīlha.@} The latter is not a Vaidadaśvi in the Rigveda, a
clear sign of the worthlessness of the legends relative to these
two men in the Brāhmaṇas.
