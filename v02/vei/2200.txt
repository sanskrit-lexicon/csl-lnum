{@Bainda@}¦ is the name of one of the victims at the Puruṣamedha
(‘human sacrifice’) in the Yajurveda.<sup>1</sup> According to the
commentator Mahīdhara, the word denotes a Niṣāda, but
according to Sāyaṇa a catcher of fish. See {@Mṛgayu.@}
