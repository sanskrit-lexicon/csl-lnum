{@Pitva@}¦<sup>1</sup> or {@Pidva@}<sup>2</sup> is the name of an animal included in
the list of victims at the Aśvamedha (‘horse sacrifice’) in the
Yajurveda Saṃhitās. According to the commentator on the
Taittirīya Saṃhitā<sup>1</sup> it means ‘lion.’ But it may be identical
with {@Petva.@}

Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā, xxiv. 32. {%Cf.%}

field, {%Journal of the American Oriental%}
{%Society,%} 29, 290.</F>
