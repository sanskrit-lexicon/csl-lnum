{@Kausalya,@}¦ ‘prince of Kosala,’ is the designation of {@Para Āṭṇāra@}
in the Satapatha Brāhmaṇa,<sup>1</sup> and of {@Hiraṇyanābha@} in the
Sāṅkhāvana Śrauta Sūtra.<sup>2</sup> Āśvalāyana is styled Kausalya, as
‘belonging to the Kosala country,’ in the Praśna Upaniṣad,<sup>3</sup>
and the Kaśī-Kausalyāḥ, or the ‘Kāśis and people of Kosala,’
are mentioned in the Gopatha Brāhmaṇa.<sup>4</sup>
