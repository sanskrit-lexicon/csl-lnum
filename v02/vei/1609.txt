{@Nṛ-medha,@}¦<sup>1</sup> {@Nṛ-medhas,@}<sup>2</sup> is the name of a protégé of Agni
in the Rigveda,<sup>3</sup> where he also appears with {@Sumedhas@} in a
hymn that Griffith<sup>4</sup> with justice declares to be unintelligible.

{@Parucchepa,@} and in the Pañcaviṃśa Brāhmaṇa<sup>2</sup> he is an
Āṅgirasa and a seer of Sāmans (Chants).
