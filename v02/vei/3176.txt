<L>3176<pc>2-356<k1>SayARqaka<k2>SayARqaka
{@Śayāṇḍaka@}¦ is the form in the Taittirīya Saṃhitā<sup>1</sup> of the
name of an animal which in the Maitrāyaṇī<sup>2</sup> and Vājasaneyi
Saṃhitās<sup>3</sup> is written as Śayaṇḍaka. Some sort of bird is meant
according to Roth,<sup>4</sup> but the commentator on the Taittirīya
Saṃhitā equates the word with {@Kṛkalāsa,@} ‘chameleon.’
