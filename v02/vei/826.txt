<L>826<pc>1-215<k1>Kalva<k2>Kalva
{@Khalva@}¦ is some sort of grain or leguminous plant, perhaps, as
Weber<sup>1</sup> thinks, the {%Phaselus radiatus.%} It is mentioned with
other grains of all sorts in the Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā,<sup>2</sup> and as
being crushed with the {@Dṛṣad@} in the Atharvaveda.<sup>3</sup> It occurs
also in the Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad,<sup>4</sup> where Śaṅkara glosses
it with {%niṣpāva.%}

it by {%caṇaka,%} ‘chick-pea.’</F>
