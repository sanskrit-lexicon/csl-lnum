{@Naimiśīya,@}¦<sup>1</sup> {@Naimiṣīya@}<sup>2</sup> denotes the dwellers in the Naimiśa
forest. They are mentioned in the Kāṭhaka Saṃhitā<sup>2</sup> and the
Brāhmaṇas,<sup>2</sup> being clearly of special sanctity. Hence in the
Epic the Mahābhārata is said to have been recited to the Ṛṣis
dwelling in the Naimiṣa forest.<sup>3</sup>
