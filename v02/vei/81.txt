meaning of ‘supplementary narrative’ seems to follow. In two<sup>1</sup>

is hardly felt, the expression being used to indicate a subse-
quent portion of the book itself. But in the third<sup>2</sup> passage it
is distinguished from the {@Itihāsa@} (‘story’) proper, and there
must mean ‘supplementary narrative.’ {%Cf.%} {@Anuvyākhyāna.@}

is to vi. 6, 4, 8).</F>
<F>2) xi. 1, 6, 9.
