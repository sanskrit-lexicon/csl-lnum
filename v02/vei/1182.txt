{@Tṛkṣi@}¦ is, in the Rigveda,<sup>1</sup> the name of a prince who was
a Trāsadasyava, ‘descendant of {@Trasadasyu.@}’ He also appears
with the Druhyu and the Pūru peoples in another hymn.<sup>2</sup> It
has been conjectured, but it is not probable, that the steed
{@Tārkṣya@} (as ‘belonging to Tṛkṣi’) was his.<sup>3</sup>
<F>1) viii. 22, 7.</F>
<F>2) vi. 46, 8. It is not certain that the
two persons are identical ({%cf.%} Hille-
brandt, {%Vedische Mythologic,%} 1, 113,
notes 3, 4), but it is probable.</F>
<F>3) Macdonell, {%Vedic Mythology,%} p. 149.</F>
