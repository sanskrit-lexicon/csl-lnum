{@Āruṇeya.@}¦ — An epithet of {@Śvetaketu,@} indicating his descent
from {@Uddālaka Āruṇi@} and {@Aruṇa Aupaveśi.@} It is apparently

<F>1) x. 3, 4, 1; xi. 2, 7, 12; 5, 4, 18; 6, 2, 1; xii. 2, 1, 9; Bṛhadāraṇyaka

<F>2) v. 3, 1; vi. 1, 1.</F>
