{@Dadhyañc Ātharvaṇa@}¦ is a purely mythical sage. In the
Rigveda<sup>1</sup> he is clearly a divinity of some kind, but in the later
[Page1-339+ 55]
Saṃhitās<sup>2</sup> and the Brāhmaṇas<sup>3</sup> he is metamorphosed into a
teacher. In the Pañcaviṃśa Brāhmana<sup>4</sup> he is by oversight
called an Āṅgirasa.
