These birds are described as dark in colour on the back ({%nīla-%}
{%pṛṣṭha%});<sup>3</sup> they fly in troops,<sup>4</sup> swim in the water ({%uda-prut%}),<sup>5</sup>

credited with the power of separating Soma from water (as

as one of the victims at the Aśvamedha (‘horse sacrifice’).<sup>9</sup>
