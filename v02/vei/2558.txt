<L>2558<pc>2-185<k1>yati<k2>yati
{@Yati@}¦ is the name of an ancient clan which is connected with
the {@Bhṛgus@} in two passages of the Rigveda,<sup>1</sup> where the Yatis
certainly seem to be real persons. In another hymn,<sup>2</sup> however,
they already appear as almost mythical. In the Yajurveda
Saṃhitās,<sup>3</sup> and elsewhere,<sup>4</sup> the Yatis are a race whom Indra, in
an evil moment, gave over to the hyænas ({@Sālāvṛka@}): exactly
what is referred to is uncertain. Yati is mentioned with Bhṛgu
in a verse of the Sāmaveda.<sup>4</sup>
