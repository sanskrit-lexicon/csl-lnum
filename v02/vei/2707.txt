<L>2707<pc>2-225<k1>ruru<k2>ruru
{@Ruru@}¦ is one of the victims at the Aśvamedha (‘horse
sacrifice’) in the Yajurveda.<sup>1</sup> A kind of deer is meant. The
Rigveda<sup>2</sup> mentions ‘deer-headed’ ({%ruru-śīrṣan%}) arrows, meaning
such as have points made of deer's horn.
<F>1) Taittirīya Saṃhitā, v. 5, 19, 1;
Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā, xxiv. 27. 39;
Maitrāyaṇī Saṃhitā, iii. 14. 9.</F>
<F>2) vi. 75, 15.
