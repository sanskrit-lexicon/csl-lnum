{@Nirukta,@}¦ ‘explanation’ of a word or passage, is found in the
Chāndogya Upaniṣad (viii. 3, 3), but does not appear as the
name of a work before the later Upaniṣads. It is, however,
probable that Yāska's Nirukta is not later than the rise of
Buddhism. {%Cf.%} {@Nirvacana.@}
