{@Hari-ścandra Vaidhasa@}¦ (‘descendant of Vedhas’) {@Aikṣvāka@}
(‘descendant of {@Ikṣvāku@}’) is the name of a probably mythical
king whose rash vow to offer up his son {@Rohita@} to Varuṇa is
the source of the tale of {@Śunaḥśepa@} in the Aitareya Brāhmana
(vii. 14, 2) and the Śāṅkhāyana Śrauta Sūtra (xv. 17).
