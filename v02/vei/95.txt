<L>95<pc>1-027<k1>apvA<k2>apvA
{@Apvā.@}¦ — A disease affecting the stomach,<sup>1</sup> possibly dysentery,
as suggested by Zimmer,<sup>2</sup> on the ground that the disease is
invoked to confound the enemy.<sup>3</sup> Weber<sup>4</sup> considers that it is
diarrhœa induced by fear, as often in the Epic.<sup>5</sup> This view is
supported by Bloomfield,<sup>6</sup> and was apparently that of Yāska.<sup>7</sup>
