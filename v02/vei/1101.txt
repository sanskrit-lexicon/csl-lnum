{@Taṇḍula,@}¦ ‘grain,’ especially ‘rice grain,’ is mentioned very
often in the Atharvaveda<sup>1</sup> and later,<sup>2</sup> but not in the Rigveda.
This accords with the fact that rice cultivation seems hardly
known in the Rigveda.<sup>3</sup> Husked ({%karṇa%}) and unhusked ({%akarṇa%})
rice is referred to in the Taittirīya Saṃhitā.<sup>4</sup>
