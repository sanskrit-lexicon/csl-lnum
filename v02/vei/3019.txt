1. {@Vṛka,@}¦ ‘wolf,’ is mentioned frequently in the Rigveda,<sup>1</sup>
and also later.<sup>2</sup> It was an enemy of sheep<sup>3</sup> and of calves,<sup>4</sup>
being dangerous even to men.<sup>5</sup> Its colour is stated to be
reddish ({%aruṇa%}).<sup>6</sup> The ‘she-wolf,’ Vṛkī, is also mentioned several
times in the Rigveda.<sup>7</sup>
