<L>2125<pc>2-058<k1>bakura<k2>bakura
{@Bakura@}¦ is mentioned in one passage of the Rigveda,<sup>1</sup> where
it is said that the Aśvins made light for the Āryan by blowing
their Bakura against the {@Dasyus.@} According to the Nirukta,<sup>2</sup>
the thunderbolt<sup>1</sup> is meant; but much more probable is Roth's<sup>3</sup>
view, that the object blown was a musical instrument. See
also {@Bākura.@}
