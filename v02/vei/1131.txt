{@Tāṇḍya@}¦ is the name of a teacher in the Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa,<sup>1</sup>
quoted on a point bearing on the Agniciti, or piling of
the sacred fire. He is also mentioned in the Vaṃśa Brāhmaṇa.<sup>2</sup>
The Tāṇḍya Mahābrāhmaṇa or Pañcaviṃśa Brāhmaṇa<sup>3</sup> of the
Sāmaveda represents the school of the Tāṇḍins.
