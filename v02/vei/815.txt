<L>815<pc>1-213<k1>Kaqga<k2>Kaqga
{@Khaḍga@}¦ is the reading in the Maitrāyaṇī Saṃhitā<sup>1</sup> of the
name of an animal which, in the text of the Vājasaneyi
Saṃhitā,<sup>2</sup> variously appears as Khaṅga and Khaḍga. The
rhinoceros seems clearly to be meant.<sup>3</sup> In the Śāṅkhāyana
Śrauta Sūtra<sup>4</sup> a rhinoceros hide is mentioned as the covering of
a chariot.
