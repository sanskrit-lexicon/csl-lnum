<L>2783<pc>2-238<k1>vatsa<k2>vatsa<h>2
2. {@Vatsa@}¦ occurs several times in the Rigveda<sup>1</sup> as the name
of a singer, a son or descendant of {@Kaṇva.@} In the Pañcaviṃśa
Brāhmaṇa<sup>2</sup> he is said to have passed successfully through a fire
ordeal to which he resorted for the purpose of proving to his
rival, {@Medhātithi,@} the purity of his descent. He is also mentioned
in the Śāṅkhāyana Śrauta Sūtra<sup>3</sup> as the recipient of bounty
from {@Tirindara Pāraśavya.@}
<F>1) viii. 6, 1; 8, 8; 9, 1; 11, 7.</F>
<F>2) xiv. 6, 6.</F>
<F>3) xvi. 11, 20. He also occurs in
Āpastamba Śrauta Sūtra, xxiv. 5, 11.
