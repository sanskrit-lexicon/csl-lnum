{@Ākhu.@}¦ — The exact sense of this word is uncertain. Zimmer<sup>1</sup>
renders it ‘mole,’ while Roth<sup>2</sup> prefers ‘mouse’ or ‘rat.’ It is
frequently mentioned in the later Saṃhitās,<sup>3</sup> and is known to
the Rigveda,<sup>4</sup> where, however, the word is regarded by Pischel<sup>5</sup>
as having acquired the secondary sense of ‘thief.’ This is
denied by Hillebrandt.<sup>6</sup>
