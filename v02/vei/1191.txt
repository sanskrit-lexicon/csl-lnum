{@Tejanī@}¦ denotes in the later Saṃhitās and Brāhmaṇas a bundle
of reeds,<sup>1</sup> and in some cases such a bundle twisted into a rope,<sup>2</sup>
for the two ends of the Tejanī are mentioned.
<F>1) Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa, xiii. 8, 3, 12;
perhaps Kāṭhaka Saṃhitā, xxiii. 9.</F>
<F>2) Kāṭhaka Saṃhitā, xxii. 13; Aitareya
Brāhmaṇa, i. 11, as rendered by Sāyaṇa.</F>
