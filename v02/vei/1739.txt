{@Pavana@}¦ (‘purifier’) in the Atharvaveda<sup>1</sup> denotes an instrument
for purifying grain from husks, etc.; either a ‘sieve’ or a
‘winnowing basket’ may be meant. In the Sūtras<sup>2</sup> it is
mentioned as used for cleaning the bones of the dead after
cremation.
<F>1) iv. 34, 2; xviii. 3, 11. {%Cf.%} Nirukta,
vi. 9.</F>
<F>2) Āśvalāyana Gṛhya Sūtra, iv. 5, 7.</F>
