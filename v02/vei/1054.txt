{@Jāmbila,@}¦ ‘hollow of the knee,’<sup>1</sup> occurs once in the Maitrāyaṇī
Saṃhitā<sup>2</sup> The word also occurs in the form of Jāmbīla in the
Kāṭhaka Saṃhitā<sup>3</sup> and the Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā.<sup>4</sup> Mahīdhara,
in his commentary on the latter text, interprets the word as
‘knee-pan,’ which he says is so named because of its resemblance
to the citron, {%jāmbīra.%}
