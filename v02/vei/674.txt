{@Kusīdin@}¦ is a designation of the ‘usurer’ found in the Śata-
patha Brāhmaṇa<sup>1</sup> and the Nirukta,<sup>2</sup> and often in the Sūtras.
Jolly,<sup>3</sup> referring doubtless to the expression {%kusīda apratītta,%}<sup>4</sup> ‘a
loan not yet repaid,’ occurring in connexion with {%an-ṛṇa,%} ‘free
from debt,’ appears to be right in taking Kusīda to have the
sense of ‘loan’ in the Taittirīya Saṃhitā. The rate of interest
on loans is not specified before the Sūtra period.<sup>5</sup> {%Cf.%} {@Ṛṇa.@}
