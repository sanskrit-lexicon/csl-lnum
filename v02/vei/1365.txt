{@Deva-malimluc,@}¦ ‘robber of the gods,’ is the epithet of
Rahasya,<sup>1</sup> who is said in the Pañcaviṃśa Brāhmaṇa<sup>2</sup> to have
slain the pious {@Vaikhānasas@} at {@Munimaraṇa@} (‘saint's death’).
He was apparently an Asura, but may have been a real person.
<F>1) Or Rahasyu.</F>
<F>2) xiv. 4, 7. {%Cf.%} Hopkins, {%Transactions%}
{%of the Connecticut Academy of Arts and%}
{%Sciences,%} 15, 51, 52.</F>
