{@Tiraścī@}¦ is, according to the Anukramaṇī, the author of a
Rigvedic hymn<sup>1</sup> in which he appeals to Indra to hear his call.
The Pañcaviṃśa Brāhmaṇa<sup>2</sup> adopts this view of the name,
and mentions a Tiraścī Āṅgirasa. But Roth<sup>3</sup> thinks that the
word is not a proper name at all.
