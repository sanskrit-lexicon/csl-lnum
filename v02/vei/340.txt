{@Ut-tāna Āṅgirasa@}¦ is mentioned in the Taittirīya Brāhmaṇa<sup>1</sup>
as a quasi-mythical person who received all good things, and
yet was not harmed, as he was really a form of the earth,
according to Sāyaṇa's<sup>2</sup> explanation. His name occurs also in
the Kāṭhaka Saṃhitā,<sup>3</sup> the Pancaviṃśa Brāhmaṇa,<sup>4</sup> and the
Taittirīya Āraṇyaka.<sup>5</sup>
