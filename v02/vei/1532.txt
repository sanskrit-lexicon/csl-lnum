{@Nāga@}¦ appears once in the Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa<sup>1</sup> in the form
{%mahānāga,%} where ‘great snake’ or ‘great elephant’ may be
meant. In the Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad,<sup>2</sup> and in a citation
found in the Aitareya Brāhmaṇa<sup>3</sup> the sense of ‘elephant’ is
clearly intended. In the Sūtras<sup>4</sup> the mythic Nāga already
occurs.
