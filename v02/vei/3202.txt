<L>3202<pc>2-366<k1>Salmali<k2>Salmali
{@Śalmali@}¦ is the name of the ‘silk cotton tree’ ({%Salmalia Mala-%}
{%barica%}). Its fruit is regarded as poisonous in the Rigveda,<sup>1</sup> but
the car of the bridal procession is made of its wood.<sup>2</sup> It is
described as the tallest of trees.<sup>3</sup>

<F>2) x. 85, 20.</F>
<F>3) Taittirīya Saṃhitā, vii. 4, 12, 1;
Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā, xxiii. 13; Śata-
patha Brāhmaṇa, xiii. 2, 7, 4; Pañca-
viṃśa Brāhmaṇa, ix. 4, 11, etc.
