{@Priya-vrata Somāpi@}¦<sup>1</sup> or {@Saumāpi@} is the name of a teacher
in the Aitareya Brāhmaṇa<sup>1</sup> and the Śāṅkhāyana Āraṇyaka,<sup>2</sup> in
which he is said to be the son of {@Somapa.@} The name Priya-
vrata is also found in the Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa,<sup>3</sup> where a
{@Rauhiṇāyana@} of that name is mentioned as a teacher.
