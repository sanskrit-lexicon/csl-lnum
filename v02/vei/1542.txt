{@Nābhāka,@}¦ ‘descendant of Nabhāka,’ is the name of a Ṛṣi,
or seer, in the Rigveda.<sup>1</sup> The Anukramaṇī (Index) ascribes
three or four hymns of the Rigveda<sup>2</sup> to him. According to
Ludwig,<sup>3</sup> the man was an Āṅgirasa,<sup>4</sup> not a Kaṇva.
<F>1) viii. 41, 2; Nirukta, x. 5.</F>
<F>2) viii. 39-41, and doubtfully 42.</F>
<F>3) Translation of the Rigveda, 3, 107.</F>
<F>4) viii. 40, 12.</F>
