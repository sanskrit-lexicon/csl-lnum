2. {@Maśaka Gārgya@}¦ (‘descendant of {@Garga@}’) is the name of
a teacher, a pupil of {@Sthiraka Gārgya,@} in the Vaṃśa Brāhmaṇa.<sup>1</sup>
He is also mentioned in the Sūtras<sup>2</sup> of the Sāmaveda, and is
the reputed author of an extant Kalpa Sūtra.
