{@Soma-śuṣma Sātya-yajñi@}¦ (‘descendant of {@Satyayajña@}’) is the
name in the Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa (xi. 6, 2, 1. 3) of a travelling
Brahmin who met {@Janaka@} of {@Videha.@} He may be identical
with the man of the same name with the additional patronymic
{@Prācīnayogya@} (‘descendant of Prācīnayoga’), who is mentioned
as a pupil of Satyayajña in the Jaiminīya Upaniṣad Brāhmaṇa
(iii. 40, 2).
