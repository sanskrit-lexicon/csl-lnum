{@Piśita@}¦ in the Atharvaveda<sup>1</sup> and later<sup>2</sup> denotes raw flesh
({%of.%} {@Piśāca@}). In one passage of the Atharvaveda<sup>3</sup> the sense

Dictionary suggests that Piśita here stands for {%piṣita,%} equivalent
to {%piṣṭa%} (what is ‘pounded,’ then ‘particle’).
