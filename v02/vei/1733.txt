{@Palāla@}¦ is found with Anu-palāla in the Atharvaveda (viii. 6, 2)
as the name of a demon. The meaning of the word is ‘straw,’
in which sense it occurs in the Kauśika Sūtra (lxxx. 27), while the
feminine form, Palālī, is found in the Atharvaveda itself (ii. 8, 3)
as the straw of barley ({@Yava@}).
