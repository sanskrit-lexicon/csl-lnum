{@Nākra@}¦ is the name of an aquatic animal included among the
victims at the Aśvamedha, or ‘horse sacrifice,’ in the Yajurveda
Saṃhitās.<sup>1</sup> Perhaps the animal meant is the crocodile, which
later is called Nakra.<sup>2</sup>
<F>1) Taittirīya Saṃhitā, v. 5, 13, 1;
Maitrāyaṇī Saṃhitā, iii. 14, 2; Vāja-
saneyi Saṃhitā, xxiv. 35.</F>
<F>2) Zimmer, {%Altindisches Leben,%} 96, fol-
lowing one version given by Mahī-
dhara on Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā, {%loc. cit.;%}
Hillebrandt, {%Vedische Mythologie,%} 3, 21,
n. 4.</F>
