{@Pṛṣātaka@}¦ is the name of a mixture like {@Pṛṣadājya,@} and
consisting, according to the late Gṛhyasaṃgraha,<sup>1</sup> of curds
({@Dadhi@}), honey ({@Madhu@}), and {@Ajya.@} It is mentioned in a late
passage of the Atharvaveda<sup>2</sup> and in the Sūtras.<sup>3</sup>
<F>1) ii. 59.</F>
<F>2) xx. 134, 2.</F>
<F>3) Mānava Gṛhya Sūtra, ii. 3, etc.
