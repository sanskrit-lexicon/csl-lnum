<L>1138<pc>1-307<k1>tAbuva<k2>tAbuva
{@Tābuva@}¦ is the name in the Atharvaveda<sup>1</sup> of a remedy against
snake poison. The Paippalāda recension has Tāvuca instead.
Weber<sup>2</sup> thinks that the original form was Tāthuva, from the
root {%sthā,%} ‘stand,’ and that it meant ‘stopping’; but this is
hardly probable.<sup>3</sup>
