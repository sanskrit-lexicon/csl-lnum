<L>230<pc>1-057<k1>Atreya<k2>Atreya
{@Ātreya@}¦ is the patronymic of a pupil of Māṇṭi in the
Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad.<sup>1</sup> An Ātreya appears as a Purohita
of {@Aṅga@} in the Aitareya Brāhmaṇa.<sup>2</sup> An Ātreya was regularly
the priest in certain rites,<sup>3</sup> and an Ātreyī occurs in an obscure
passage in the Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa.<sup>4</sup>
<F>1) ii. 6, 3; iv. 6, 3 (in both ver-
sions).</F>
<F>2) viii. 22.</F>
<F>3) {%Ibid.,%} vii. 7; Satapatha Brāhmaṇa,
iv. 3, 4, 21; Kātyāyana Srauta Sūtra,
x. 2, 21 ({%sadasaḥ purastāt%}).</F>
<F>4) i. 4, 5, 13. {%Cf.%} Roth, St. Peters-
burg Dictionary, {%s.v.%}</F>
