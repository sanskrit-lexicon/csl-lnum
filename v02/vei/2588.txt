{@Yāska@}¦ (‘descendant of Yaska’) is mentioned in the first two
Vaṃśas (lists of teachers) of the Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad<sup>1</sup> as
a contemporary of {@Āsurāyaṇa@} and a teacher of {@Bhāradvāja.@}
Whether Yāska, author of the Nirukta,<sup>2</sup> was the same person,
it is, of course, impossible to say.
