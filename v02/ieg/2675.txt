{%Kāyastha%}¦ (EI 24; ASLV; HD), a clerk; explained by some
as ‘a registrar’ (EI 31); a scribe or writer in the king's revenue
department according to some. See {%Yājñavalkyasmṛti,%} I. 322;
{%Viṣṇu Dharma Sūtra,%} VII. 3; etc. Cf. {%Aśvaghāsa-kāyastha%} and
{%Grāma-kāyastha%} ({%Rājataraṅgiṇī,%} V. 175; {%IHQ,%} Vol. IX,
p. 12). See also {%Hist. Dharm.,%} Vol. II, pp. 75-77. For
derivation, cf. {%Bhār. Vid.,%} Vol. X, pp. 280 ff.
