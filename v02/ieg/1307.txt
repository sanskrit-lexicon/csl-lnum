{%cora-daṇḍa-varjya%}¦ (CII 3), a fiscal term which means either
that no fines for theft should be collected from the gift village
or, more probably, that the donee was not entitled to fines
realisable on thieves in the gift villages. But see also {%caura-varjam.%}
