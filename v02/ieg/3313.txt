{%Mahattara%}¦ (IE 8-3; EI 23, 29, 30; CII 4; BL; HD),
literally, ‘an elder [of a town or village]’; according to Stein
({%Rājataraṅgiṇī,%} VII. 659); a chamberlain, a village headman

Vol. VI, p. 114 {%(rāṣṭra-grāma-mahattara); Daśakumāracarita,%} III,
p. 77 ({%janapada-mahattara%}).
