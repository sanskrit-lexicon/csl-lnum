<L>211<pc>013<k1>AjYapti<k2>AjYapti, AjYApti
{%Ājñapti, Ājñāpti%}¦ (IE 8-3; EI 24, 27, 30; SII 1; HD), ‘the
agency that obtains the command’; the executor of a grant; one
who communicates, at the order of the king, the details about a
land grant to local officers; probably the same as {%Dūtaka.%} Cf.
{%Ājñā;%} also Tamil {%Vāy-kkeḻvi, Vāy-kkeḻviyār.%} See {%Ind. Ep.,%} pp. 143-44;
{%Ep. Ind.,%} Vol. VII, p. 184; Vol. VIII, p. 146. Cf. {%Ājñā-sañcārin.%}
