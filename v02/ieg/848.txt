{%Bhagavat%}¦ (ML; CII 3, 4), ‘the lord’ or ‘the divine’; an
epithet of divinities such as Viṣṇu, Buddha, Jinendra, Nārāyaṇa,
Śiva, the Sun-god, Kārttikeya, etc; also applied to sages, etc.
in the sense of ‘venerable’, e. g. to Vyāsa, the arranger of the
Vedas; rarely applied to kings apparently on account of their
saintliness (cf. {%Ep. Ind.,%} Vol. XXX, p. 19).
