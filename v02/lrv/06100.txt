{#uta#}¦ {%(II) ind.%} A particle expressing- (1) doubt, indecision (or), {#tatkimayatamAtapadozaH syAduta yaTA me manasi vartate#} <ls>Sak.</ls>iii., {#pibAmaH SAstrODAnuta viviDakAvyAmftarasAn#} <ls>Bhartr.</ls>iii.40; (2) alternative, option (or), <ls>K.S.</ls>vi.23 (in these two senses it is usually a correlative of {#kim#} meaning ‘whether’); (3) connection, association (and, also), {#aDarmo'BiBavatyuta#} <ls>Bg.</ls>ii.29; (4) interrogation. (Sometimes it is used as a mere expletive).<P>With {#Aho#} or {#Ahosvit#} or {#svit#}, it is used as a particle of doubt or deliberation, e.g. {#yakzI vA rAkzasI vA tvamutAho'si surAMganA#}.<P>With {#kim#}, it expresses- (1) ‘or,’ {#kimuta sakale jAte vAhniprEyatvAmihezyasi#} <ls>Am.S.</ls>9; (2) ‘how much more,’ ‘how much less’ {#ceto munerapi haretkimutAsmadIyam#} <ls>Ch.P.</ls>39, or {#mayi nAMtako'pi praBuH prahartuM kimutAnyAhiMsrAH#} <ls>R.</ls>ii.62.<P>With {#prati#}, it means ‘on the contrary,’ {#sAmavAdAH sakopasya tasya pratyuta dIpakAH#} <ls>Sis.</ls>ii.55.<P>When repeated, it has the sense of ‘either-or.’
