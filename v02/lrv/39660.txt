{#sam#}¦ {%ind.%} When prefixed to verbal themes, it means ‘with, together with.’<P>Sometimes it intensifies the idea contained in the simple root aud may then be translated by ‘much, greatly, thoroughly, quite, very,’ (e.g. {#saMtApa#}, {#saMtoza#}).<P>It may also express ‘completeness, perfection, or beauty.’<P>It is sometimes prefixed to nouns in the sense of ‘same, like, similar.’<P>As the first member of a compound, it means ‘with, near, before,’ (e.g. {#samakza#}).
