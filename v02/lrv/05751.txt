When used to indicate the meaning of a sentence it is merely added on to that sentence, e.g. {#prAptasya bADAdityevaM parisaMKyA trodizikA#} /M. Kārikā./ <P>As a particle, it implies- (1) cause or purpose (because, since, that), {#purARamityeva na sADu sarvam#} <ls>Mal.</ls>i.
