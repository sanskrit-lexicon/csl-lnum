{#eva#}¦ {%ind.%} 1. Just, exactly, e.g. {#evameva#} ‘exactly so’; 2. same, identical, e.g. {#sa evAgataH#}; 3. only (implying exclusion), e.g. {#pArTa eva DanurDaraH#} (‘not others’ is the implication); 4. like, as (implying similarity), e.g. {#SrIsta eva me'stu#}; 5. it sometimes implies emphasis, e.g. {#ahameva gacCAmi#}.
