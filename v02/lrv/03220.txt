{#araRya-candrikA#}¦ {%f.%} moonlight in a forest (lit.), any decoration which does not serve its purpose (fig.). Moonlight in a forest is not appreciated or enjoyed by people and is thus purposeless. Similarly decoration when not appreciated or enjoyed by those for whom it is intended is useless. Thus on ‘{#strIRAM priyAlokaPalo hi vezaH#}’ <ls>K.S.</ls>vii.22, <ls>Mall.</ls> observes {#anyaTA aranyacandrikA syAt#}.
