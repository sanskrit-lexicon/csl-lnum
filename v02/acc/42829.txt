{#keSavamiSra#}¦ the author of the Alaṃkāraśekhara, lived
under Māṇikyacandra, son of Dharmacandra, grandson
of Rāmacandra. A notice in Cunningham's Arch.
Survey V, 160 states that Māṇikyacandra, son of
Dharmacandra, came to the throne in Kangra in
1563.--Keśavamiśra wrote also a Vākyaratna
which in the Alaṃkāraśekhara is quoted twice.
