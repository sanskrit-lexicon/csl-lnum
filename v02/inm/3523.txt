[{@Dhṛtarāṣṭra-putranāma-kathana@}]¦ (“enumeration
of the names of Dhṛtarāṣṭra's sons”). § 182 (Sambhavap.):
Enumeration of the children of {%Dhṛtarāṣṭra%} (α) according
to the succession of their birth (cf. § 130). Worthy wives
were in time selected for all of them, and {%Duḥśalā%} was given
to {%Jayadratha%} (I, {@117@}).
