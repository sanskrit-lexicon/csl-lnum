[{@Mahendrācalagamana(ṃ)@}]¦ (“the going to the
Mahendra mountain”). § 399 (cf. Tīrthayātrāp.): On the
fourteenth day of the moon {%Rāma%} showed himself and was
worshipped by the Pāṇḍavas. {%Rāma%} praised them, and at
his direction {%Yudhiṣṭhira%} spent the night on {%Mahendra%}, and
then started for the south (III, {@117@}).
