{@Āditya-tīrtha@}¦ (“the holy place of the Sun”), upon the
Sarasvatī. § 615{%ee%} (Baladevat.): IX, {@49@}, 2846. There
Sūrya, having performed a sacrifice, obtained the sovereignty

on the bank of the river, D. with I., V.-D., M., G., Aps.,
{%Vyāsa, Śuka, Kṛṣṇa%}, Y., Rā., Pś., etc., always reside.

Kaiṭabha, had performed his ablutions. Vyāsa, having
bathed in that {%tīrtha%}, obtained great {%Yoga%}-powers and
success ({%siddhiṃ%}). And R. Asita Devala (ff.), having bathed
in that {%tīrtha%} with soul rapt in {%Yoga%}, obtained great {%Yoga-%}
{%powers%}.
